'''<dfn>[http://apple.com/ Apple]</dfn>''' is a for-profit publicly traded ($AAPL) corporation that produces various products ([[iPhone]], [[iPod]], [[Macintosh]]) & software ([[iOS]], [[macOS]]), and provides services ([[iCloud]], [[iMessage]], [[iTunes]], [[Apple Music]]), all of which focus on good [[UX]], also an IndieWeb [[principle]], and thus are used by many in the IndieWeb community.
