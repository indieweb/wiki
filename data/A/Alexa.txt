=== training using real conversations ===
* 2023-09-26 NBC News: [https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/security/amazon-alexa-ai-listening-to-you-me-voice-save-record-rcna117205 Amazon will use real user conversations to train Alexa's AI model] / The disclosure that Amazon would train its AI from real user conversations came in a Bloomberg TV interview. <blockquote><p>Amazon announced new AI capabilities for its Alexa products last week, based on a model it’s calling AlexaLLM (LLM refers to the “large language model”). The technology will make Alexa “more personalized to your family” and allow it to remember relevant context throughout conversations like a human, Amazon said.</p><p>But along with those new capabilities, said Amazon’s senior vice president of devices and services, Dave Limp, Amazon would use some user voice interactions with Alexa to train its AI model. </p></blockquote>
