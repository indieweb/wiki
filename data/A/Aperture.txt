'''<dfn>Aperture</dfn>''' is an open source [[Microsub]] server developed by {{aaronpk}}. It is currently in alpha, and is not recommended for use by other people yet. 

It can be used with any [[Microsub]] client such as [[Together]] (web) or [[Indigenous]] (iOS).

If you would like to develop a Microsub client, {{aaronpk}} can give you an account on his hosted instance.
