'''<dfn>[https://www.amazon.com/gp/seller/asin-upc-isbn-info.html Amazon Standard Identification Numbers]</dfn>''' ('''ASIN''') is a unique 10-character identifier [[Amazon]] uses for products. For books, the ASIN is the same as the 10-digit [[ISBN]]. 13-digit ISBNs can be converted to 10-digit using a tool like {{t}}'s http://asin.cc/.
