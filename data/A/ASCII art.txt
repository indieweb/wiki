'''<dfn>ASCII art</dfn>''' is the use of text characters, typically ASCII code points 33-127 (7-bit visible), nearly always in a monospace font to create coarse, abstract, and usually monochromatic art, that members of the IndieWeb community sometimes post on their sites or use as home page decorations.
