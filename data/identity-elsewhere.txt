IndieWeb sites often have sections of their website (typically home page) that link to profile pages on social network [[silos]]. These are often in a list with the heading "Elsewhere".

== Examples ==
* tantek.com - with heading "Elsewhere" and use of rel="me" to link to silo profiles
* ...
