'''<dfn>[https://simpleicons.org Simple Icons]</dfn>''' is a collection of over 200 SVG icons for popular brands. It is being maintained by [http://danleech.com Dan Leech]. As it features a diverse set of community website logos it lends itself particularly well for linking to external profile pages.
