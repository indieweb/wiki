'''<dfn>acquishutdown</dfn>''', sometimes spelled <dfn>acqui-shutdown</dfn> (or called <dfn>acquicide</dfn>), is the act of a company acquiring another company, and then shutting it down, typically taking its site offline as well, or redirecting it to some unrelated page or site of the acquiring company.
