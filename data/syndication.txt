'''<dfn>syndication</dfn>''' may refer to:
* The '''act of syndication''', posting copies of your content on other sites (e.g. to [[silos]]). See also [[POSSE]].
* An '''instance of syndication''', that is a particular copy of one of your original posts. When original posts link to their syndications, such links should be marked up with [[rel-syndication]].
* The '''[[rel-syndication]]''' microformat - a [[rel]] value for use when linking to copies your original posts.
