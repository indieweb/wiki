'''<dfn>[http://readmill.com/ Readmill]</dfn>''' was a [[silo]] for reading ebooks and making annotations that [[silo-deaths#Readmill|shut down]] 2014-07-01.

== Exports ==
Exports were available from Readmill until 2014-07-01 and produced files in various formats.

<blockquote>"All of your books, reading data and account information will be permanently deleted after July 1, 2014. User information will not be archived, shared or saved in any way."</blockquote>

<nowiki>http://readmill.com/export</nowiki> (required login) output .zip archive with .txt .json .xml plus a bunch of assets.
