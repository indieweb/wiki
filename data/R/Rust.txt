'''<dfn>Rust</dfn>''' is a system [[programming language]] by [[Mozilla]].

== Library ==
The objectives of [[https://crates.io/crates/indieweb the Rust library]] is to provide conforming implementations of the following:

* [[IndieAuth]]
* [[Webmention]]
* [[Micropub]] and supported [[Micropub-extensions|extensions]]
* [[Microsub]]
* [[WebSub]]

as well as the following [[algorithms]]:

* [[representative h-card]] parsing
* [[authorship]] parsing
* [[original post discovery]]
