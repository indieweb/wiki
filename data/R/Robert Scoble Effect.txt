'''<dfn>The Scoble Effect</dfn>''' is named for [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Scoble Robert Scoble], a former tech evangelist at Microsoft, and is the potential of unexpected consequences or crashes when famous people begin using or interacting with websites, apps, and especially social networks with their resulting network effects, though almost nobody these days remembers it.
