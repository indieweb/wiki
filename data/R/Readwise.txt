'''<dfn>[https://readwise.io/ Readwise]</dfn>''' is a paid web app/service that allows users to sync highlights from various web and digital sources and review them (using [[spaced repetition]]) via an app or daily email.

== Features ==
[[highlight|Highlight]] sync support is available for Amazon [[Kindle]], [[Instapaper]], [[Pocket]], and [[iBooks]].

Their UI will also allow one to [[tag]], [[annotation|annotate]], [[search]], and organize content.

* https://twitter.com/readwiseio apparently also allows you to save individual tweets by replying to a tweet with "@readwiseio save" which then replies to you with "Done! We've saved this tweet to your library so you can revisit/remember it" and some stats like number of times that tweet was saved, and both unique & all saved tweets from that author

* Readwise also supports unrolling a [[tweetstorm]] and saving a copy of it: https://twitter.com/readwiseio/status/1309209103475056640

== Export ==
The service provides highlight sync/export to note-taking tools [[Evernote]], [[Notion]], and [[Roam Research]].
