'''<dfn>RabbitMQ</dfn>''' is a highly scalable and reliable Open Source [[message broker]] that implements Advanced Message Queuing Prototocol [[AMQP]] written in Erlang. It can be configured to handle most messaging needs, including acting as a [[message queue]].
