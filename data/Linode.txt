'''<dfn>[https://www.linode.com Linode]</dfn>''' is a company that provides [[virtual private server|virtual private servers]] and [[DNS]] hosting.

== Security auto-updates ==
Linode will update servers as needed when it receives notice of security updates, and if necessary reboot servers (sometimes with very little notice, like a day or days).

* 2015-12-13 [http://status.linode.com/incidents/ngql6k6dm420 Linode Critical Xen Maintenance]

Such reboots may affect long running processes, and any applications you were running that require a manual restart will require you to do so.
