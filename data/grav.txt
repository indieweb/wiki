'''<dfn>[http://www.getgrav.org/ Grav]</dfn>''' is a [[flat-file]] [[CMS]] built on [[PHP]], [[Twig]] and [[YAML]] and [[Markdown]]. Articles are stored in a YAML + Markdown format.

At the moment is filesystem based only, Admin area is under development.

Its plugin system would make it possible to add indieweb functionality.
