'''<dfn>DNSSEC</dfn>''' is a security extension to [[DNS]] to allow verification and authentication of DNS records and domain names to prevent tampering with domain records and DNS-based man-in-the-middle attacks.

== Hosted DNS Providers with DNSSEC support ==
* [https://blog.cloudflare.com/introducing-universal-dnssec/ CloudFlare]
* [https://blog.dnsimple.com/2017/03/dnssec-beta-launch/ DNSimple] (public beta as of March 2017)

== Validating Resolvers ==
* [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Public_DNS Google Public DNS] (8.8.8.8)
* [https://cloudns.com.au CloudNS]
* [https://dns.watch/index DNS.WATCH]
