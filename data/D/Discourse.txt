== How To ==
=== How to browse without JavaScript ===
{{Zegnat}} has a simple browser extension, [https://github.com/Zegnat/browserext-discourse-html Discourse HTML], which rewrites Discourse URLs to their non-JS versions

You can manually append <code>?_escaped_fragment_</code> to a Discourse URL to get the HTML only view.
