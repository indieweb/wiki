'''<dfn>DeleteFacebook</dfn>''' is a popular [[hashtag]] on [[Twitter]] ([https://twitter.com/hashtag/DeleteFacebook #DeleteFacebook]) and a statement (<dfn>Delete Facebook</dfn>) used by people to proclaim when they leave [[Facebook]] (to varying degrees), a call to action for others to leave as well, and on a 2021 Time Magazine cover as <dfn>Delete “Facebook”</dfn>.
