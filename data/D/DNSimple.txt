'''<dfn>[https://dnsimple.com DNSimple]</dfn>''' is a [[DNS]] provider and [[domain registrar]].

In early 2017 they [https://blog.dnsimple.com/2017/01/dnsimple-platform-developer-preview/ announced a platform initiative] to make it easy for services to easily offer to set up a user's domain to be used with the service, e.g. a hosted blogging tool being able to easily offer to link a user's domain to their blog.
