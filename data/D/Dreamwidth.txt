'''<dfn>[https://www.dreamwidth.org Dreamwidth]</dfn>''' is an [[open source]]<ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/site/opensource Dreamwidth: Open Source]</ref> social networking and [[blog|blogging]] site based on [[LiveJournal]] code and committed to open access, [[interoperability]], and respecting user [[privacy]].<ref name ="principles">[https://www.dreamwidth.org/legal/principles Dreamwidth Guiding Principles]</ref><ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/support/faqbrowse?faqid=1 Dreamwidth FAQ: What is Dreamwidth Studios?]</ref> It was originally launched in 2009.<ref>[https://dw-news.dreamwidth.org/5035.html Welcome to Dreamwidth Studios!]</ref>

Dreamwidth allows [[OpenID]] to be used for subscribing to Dreamwidth journals, using a Reading Page (subscription feed), and commenting on Dreamwidth posts.<ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/openid/ Dreamwidth: OpenID]</ref> 

Dreamwidth generates [[RSS]] and [[Atom]] feeds for all accounts.<ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/support/faqbrowse?faqid=117&q=rss&lang= Dreamwidth FAQ: Can I get an RSS or Atom feed of my Dreamwidth journal?]</ref> A Dreamwidth account can also be used to subscribe to external RSS or Atom feeds.<ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/feeds/ Dreamwidth: Feeds]</ref><ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/support/faqbrowse?faqid=27&q=rss&lang=  Dreamwidth FAQ: How do I create or subscribe to a feed (RSS/Atom)?]</ref>

Dreamwidth has played a role in actively combatting legal challenges to user privacy on the internet.<ref>[https://dw-advocacy.dreamwidth.org/260.html The Current List of Cases]</ref> In partnership with NetChoice, Dreamwidth staff have participated in cases such as  NetChoice v. Skrmetti<ref>[https://dw-advocacy.dreamwidth.org/514.html Netchoice v Skrmetti (TN)]</ref> and Netchoice, LLC v Yost.<ref>[https://dw-news.dreamwidth.org/43698.html In which we help to sue Ohio]</ref>

Dreamwidth Studios, LLC operates on a user-funded [[business model]]. Users can support the site by purchasing extra features for their accounts.<ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/support/faqbrowse?faqid=233 Dreamwidth FAQ: What is a paid/premium paid/seed account?]</ref><ref>[https://www.dreamwidth.org/support/faqbrowse?faqid=4 Dreamwidth FAQ: What are the extra services available?]</ref> Dreamwidth does not accept or display third-party advertising.<ref name="principles" />
