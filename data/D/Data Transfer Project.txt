[https://datatransferproject.dev/ '''<dfn>Data Transfer Project</dfn>'''] is a collaboration of organizations committed to building a common framework with open-source code that can connect any two online service providers, enabling a seamless, direct, user initiated portability of data between the two platforms.

It was formed in 2017 by some of the leading silos (like [[Google]], [[Facebook]] and [[Twitter]]).
