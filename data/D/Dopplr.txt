'''<dfn>Dopplr</dfn>''' was a [[silo]] (at dopplr.com) for sharing future [[travel]] plans.

== Shutdown ==
2013-11-01 '''Dopplr''' discontinuing ([http://dopplr.com/ home page] and [http://ourincrediblejourney.tumblr.com/post/64397333538/dopplr]).
* Announced 2013-10-18 - just two week notice. 

== Data Export ==
Until 2013-11-01, data export still available after logging in at: 
* http://www.dopplr.com/account/data_export
Follow instructions there.

More export URLs:
* See http://www.dopplr.com/account for individual ICS and Atom feeds, under heading "Getting things out of Dopplr"). 

To export your Dopplr contacts (which are not included in the above data exports)
* Use Firefox to go to <kbd>http://www.dopplr.com/account/connections</kbd>
* From the '''File''' menu, choose '''Save Page As...'''
* Be sure '''Web Page, complete''' is selected in the popup
* Save the file locally and you'll get an HTML file and a directory next to it of its resources made locally accessible.

Other pages to consider archiving when logged in:
* http://www.dopplr.com/account/others - people who Dopplr thinks you should have connected with.
