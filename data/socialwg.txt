The '''<dfn>[https://www.w3.org/wiki/socialwg Social Web Working Group]</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>Social Web WG</dfn> or <dfn>Social WG</dfn>) is a [[W3C]] Working Group that is chartered with developing specifications for the [[social web]], based on the outcome of the [[osfw3c]] workshop in 2013-08.

== Social WG IRC ==
The Social Web WG uses the #social IRC channel on the W3C IRC network (irc.w3.org). It's the best place to catch up on discussions and meeting (telcon, face-to-face) interactions:
* Logs: http://socialwg.indiewebcamp.com/irc/social/today
