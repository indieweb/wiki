== Brainstorming ==
Eli Pariser in [https://www.ted.com/talks/eli_pariser_what_obligation_do_social_media_platforms_have_to_the_greater_good What obligation do social media platforms have to the greater good?] (at about 11:55 into the video) suggests building in a facepalm response to other's social media posts to indicate a soft, negative social feedback. This could be in lieu of more drastic online moderated responses like banning, [[shun|shunning]], [[block|blocking]], [[report abuse]], shadow banning, or [[deplatforming]].
