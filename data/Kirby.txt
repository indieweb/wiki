'''<dfn>[http://getkirby.com/ Kirby]</dfn>''' is a filed-based CMS. Its main developer Bastian Allgeier [http://bastianallgeier.com/notes/indie-web "can't wait to spend more time on integrating indie web components into Kirby"] after making a [https://github.com/bastianallgeier/kirby-webmentions Kirby webmention plugin] at IndieWebCamp ([[2015/Germany/Demos#Bastian_Allgeier]]).

== Features ==
* Public APIs to write and read files, so you can provide your own interface for it. 
* Creates [[Markdown]] files.
* Currently has custom authentication system, would like to combine with indieauth
-- http://indiewebcamp.com/irc/2015-05-09#t1431160990178
