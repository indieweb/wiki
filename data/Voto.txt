=Voto=

Voto is [[User:vasilis.nl|Vasilis van Gemert]]'s personal photo gallery. He's created it in order to publish his own pictures to his own domain from any device he owns. Pictures are syndicated to other services via RSS. 
He's written in detail about [http://nerd.vasilis.nl/voto-self-hosted-indieweb-photo-gallery/ the ideas behind it] on his blog.
