'''<dfn>toot</dfn>''' was the humorous term for [[note]] post in [[Mastodon]] that had its origins in a 2008 Twitter joke by Merlin Mann, suggested for adoption in Mastodon by Hbomberguy and adopted by Mastodon in 2016, and finally removed in 2021.
