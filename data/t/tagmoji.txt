'''<dfn>Tagmoji</dfn>''' is the use of a single [[emoji]] to categorize a piece of content. Similar to a [[hashtag]], but utilizing an emoji. The most common form of this in the IndieWeb is through including an emoji in a [[note]].

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} has supported displaying linked tagmoji on his posts since [https://aaronparecki.com/2017/02/20/7/emoji 2017-02-20]
* example post with tagmoji: https://aaronparecki.com/2017/02/20/7/emoji
* example tagmoji archive page with posts with that emoji: [https://aaronparecki.com/emoji/%F0%9F%A4%93 ü§ì]
