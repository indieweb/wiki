'''<dfn>Tagmoji</dfn>''' is the use of a single [[emoji]] to categorize a piece of content, similar to [[hashtag]]s, and is supported by at least one IndieWeb site and the [[micro.blog]] service. 

== IndieWeb Examples ==
The most common form of tagmoji in the IndieWeb is with an emoji in a [[note]].

=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} has supported displaying linked tagmoji on his posts since [https://aaronparecki.com/2017/02/20/7/emoji 2017-02-20]
* example post with tagmoji: https://aaronparecki.com/2017/02/20/7/emoji
