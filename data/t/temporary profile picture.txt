'''<dfn>temporary profile picture</dfn>''' is a profile picture that expires after a set amount of time and reverts to the previous profile picture.

== Silo Examples ==
=== Facebook ===
[[Facebook]] allows you to set a temporary profile picture, selecting when the photo should expire. It appears to only be supported with their mobile apps. https://www.facebook.com/help/816899741763786
