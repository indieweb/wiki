'''<dfn>travel</dfn>''' is a [[post]] type about plans to change [[locations]] in the future, similar to an [[event]] post about a future event, and related to [[exercise]] posts that involve changing location.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} uses [[p3k]] to post [http://aaronparecki.com/travel travel plans] (including cancellations) on aaronparecki.com [http://aaronparecki.com/travel/2013/11/01/2/ since 2013-11-01]. Examples:
* http://aaronparecki.com/travel/2013/11/01/2/
* http://aaronparecki.com/travel/2014/05/07/1/
* http://aaronparecki.com/travel/2013/11/09/1/ (a cancelled trip)

=== Christina Milcher ===
Christina uses [[Pe_pplog]] to post travel plans on tine.pagekite.me. Examples:
* http://tine.pagekite.me/?viewDetailed=00247, May 2016 travel plans marked up with h-entry, travel plans presented as band-style tour poster
