A '''<dfn>toolchain</dfn>''' is how web development is often made far more complicated than necessary (most toolchains are targeted at enterprises or large dev teams, you can start with valid [[semantic HTML]]+CSS+JS+DOM and a text editor instead), scaring away all but the most privileged from venturing into web development.
