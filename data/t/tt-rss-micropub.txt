'''<dfn>[https://github.com/cweiske/tt-rss-micropub tt-rss-micropub]</dfn>''' is a plugin for the [[Tiny Tiny RSS]] feed reader that adds a [[reply]] form as well as [[bookmark]] and [[like]] buttons to [[tt-rss]] to post those [[reactions]] to your own site using the [[Micropub]] protocol.
