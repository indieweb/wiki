'''<dfn>timeline review</dfn>''' is a feature on some [[silos]] (in particular [[Facebook]]) that is a form of [[moderation]] in particular to approve some (or all) [[posts]] by others both explicitly to your [[wall]] (timeline) and implicitly by [[person-tag]]ging you.
