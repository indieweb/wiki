'''<dfn>top Tweets</dfn>''' is the default [[algorithmic feed]] shown on your Twitter home page (or app 🏠 screen) that in addition to showing a subset of out of time order (re)posts from your followings, includes some posts they’ve liked, which tends to amplify snark, gotchas, and other negative aspects of default Twitter culture.
