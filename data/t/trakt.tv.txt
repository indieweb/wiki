'''<dfn>[https://trakt.tv/ trakt.tv]</dfn>''' is a [[silo]] service to automatically track what you're [[watch|watching]].

They [[scrobble]] what you're watching from a variety of services like Netflix, Plex, Kodi, VLC, Emby, Media Portal, Infuse, and Serviio.
