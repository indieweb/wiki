A '''<dfn>table of contents</dfn>''' is an outline of a HTML document that usually links to specific sections and sub-sections.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{capjamesg}} displays tables of contents on long wiki pages on his site, like https://jamesg.blog/movies.
** Tables of contents are enclosed within a details/summary HTML tags so users can open and close them. Tables of contents are closed by default.
