'''<dfn>[https://dev.twitter.com/web/tweet-button Tweet button]</dfn>''' is a [[Twitter]]-specific "post" [[webaction]].

== Why ==
If you [[POSSE]] to Twitter, you may want to also provide a "Tweet" button as a fallback to a "post" [[webaction]] on those posts syndicated to Twitter.

== How ==
See https://dev.twitter.com/web/tweet-button
