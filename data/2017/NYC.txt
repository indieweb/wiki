'''<dfn class="p-name">IndieWebCamp NYC 2017</dfn>''' was a two-day maker event for creating and/or improving your personal website and the fifth IndieWebCamp in NYC!
{{update|'''See [[NYC|IndieWebCamp NYC]] for the most recent and a list of all past IndieWebCamps in NYC!'''}}
It was held 2017-09-30 and 2017-10-01 at the Dalberg offices in Manhattan, New York City.
