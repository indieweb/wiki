'''<dfn>IndieWebCamp Bellingham 2017</dfn>''' was the first IndieWebCamp held in [[Bellingham]], WA 2017-05-04 through 2017-05-05. 

[[IndieWebCamp]] is a gathering for independent web [[creators]] of all kinds, from graphic artists, to designers, UX engineers, coders, hackers, to share ideas, actively work on [[selfdogfood|creating for their own]] personal websites, and [[build]] upon each others creations.

= IndieWebCamp Bellingham =
One of several [[IndieWebCamp]] [[events]] in [[Planning|2017]].

<div class="p-description description">'''Own your data.'''

We should all own the content we're creating, rather than just posting to third-party content silos. Publish on your own domain, and syndicate out to silos. 

Join us for two days of brainstorming &amp; creating at <span class="p-name summary">IndieWebCamp Bellingham 2017</span> and be a part of advancing &amp; growing the indieweb.</div>

If you can't get to the Bellingham event, consider remote participating or finding/running an IndieWebCamp remote party near you.

=== Travel & Lodging ===

==== Public Transit ====
If you are flying into BLI, unfortunately it is a small airport and public transportation does not go directly to it. The closest stop, route 50, is about 0.9 miles. Otherwise, public transit around town and to the venue is good. See: http://ridewta.com

==== Taxi ====
See: https://www.portofbellingham.com/BusinessDirectoryII.aspx?lngBusinessCategoryID=84

==== Uber ====
You can also take Uber from the airport. The cost from the airport to the venue will be about $17-21 for UberX. More info: https://www.uber.com/cities/bellingham/

==== Hotels ====
Here are some hotels sorted by proximity to the venue:

* Hotel Bellwether - [http://hotelbellwether.com website]
* Days Inn - [https://www.wyndhamhotels.com/days-inn/bellingham-washington/days-inn-bellingham/overview website]
