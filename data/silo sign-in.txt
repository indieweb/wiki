'''<dfn>silo sign-in</dfn>''' refers to all the delegated sign-in/login services offered by popular [[silos]] such as [[Facebook]] Connect, [[Sign in with Google]], and [[Sign in with Twitter]]. Instead of silo sign-in, you should use and support [[IndieAuth]].
