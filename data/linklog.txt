A linklog is a post type found in various popular blogs around the web.  Typically, a linklog post consist of the title of the post echoing the title of the page that the title links to.  Below the title, you typically find a date and permalink icon. 

AKA bookmark [[posts]]. Perhaps a more common name and this should be moved to /bookmark?

Examples:
* [http://www.marco.org/ Marco.org]
* [http://daringfireball.net/ Daring Fireball]

Silos:
* del.icio.us
* bit.ly
