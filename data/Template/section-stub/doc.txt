This template is used to indicate a section is incomplete and encourages people to expand it. It should be placed at the top of the page.

If a section seems thorough to you (and you consider yourself fairly knowledgeable on the subject and thus capable of reasonably determining as much), feel free to remove the <nowiki>{{section-stub}}</nowiki> template code.

== Blank Template ==
Copy and paste:

<pre style="line-height: 1.4em; padding: 1em; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: black; background-color: #f9f9f9;">{{section-stub}}</pre>

== Parameters ==
This template has no parameters.

== Examples ==
Including this template code:

<pre style="line-height: 1.4em; padding: 1em; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: black; background-color: #f9f9f9;">{{section-stub}}</pre>

Will generate:
{{section-stub}}
