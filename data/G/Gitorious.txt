'''<dfn>[http://getgitorious.com/ Gitorious]</dfn>''' was open source [[git]] hosting and collaboration software which has since been [https://about.gitlab.com/2015/03/03/gitlab-acquires-gitorious/ acquired] by [[GitLab]]. Development seems to have stopped with the last release in [https://blog.gitorious.org/2014/12/04/announcing-gitorious-3-2/ December 2014]

Gitorious.org, a hosted service based on the software, closed in [https://blog.gitorious.org/2015/04/15/gitorious-org-is-dead-long-live-gitorious-org/ in May 2015], and users were encouraged to migrate the repositories to [[GitLab]].

When it shut down, [https://twitter.com/textfiles/status/588740015166861312 Archive Team took over] the gitorious.org domain and continues to host the public content in read-only form.
