'''<dfn>[https://gfycat.com/ Gfycat]</dfn>''' was a [[silo]] to search and share animated gifs.

Gfycat is being discontinued and content will become unavailable after September 1, 2023. Their announcement:

<blockquote>The Gfycat service is being discontinued. Please save or delete your Gfycat content by visiting https://www.gfycat.com and logging in to your account. After September 1, 2023, all Gfycat content and data will be deleted from gfycat.com</blockquote>

If you have content which [[hotlinking|hotlinks]] to images at gfycat.com, you should update your references.
