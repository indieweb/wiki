'''<dfn>Google Knowledge Graph</dfn>''' is an attempt by [[Google]] to provide summary information and context about anything, and in particular, displayed as part of a search result for a specific term or phrase. When Google's Knowledge Graph has information regarding a subject, it displays a knowledge box, such as the one below, showing information on the topic/individual.
