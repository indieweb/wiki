'''<dfn>[https://developers.google.com/identity/ Google Identity Platform]</dfn>''' is (formerly part of [[Google Plus|Google+]]) a proprietary identity provider for building [[Google Sign-In]] support into your applications that only provides Google-controlled identities, and does not accept externally provided identities.
