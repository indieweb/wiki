'''<dfn>Google Talk</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>GTalk</dfn>, <dfn>GChat</dfn>) is an instant messaging service and client application from [[Google]]. While the client application has been discontinued in favor of [[Hangouts]], third party applications can currently connect to the service over [[XMPP]].
