'''<dfn>Gemini</dfn>''' is a application-level internet protocol for the distribution of arbitrary files, with some special consideration for serving a lightweight hypertext format which facilitates linking between files. You may think of Gemini as "the web, stripped right back to its essence" or as "Gopher, souped up and modernised a little", depending upon your perspective.

== Concepts ==

Gemini sites are called "capsules" and are hosted by Gemini protocol servers.

To access Gemini sites with current web browsers an Web-to-Gemini proxy is necessary.

There are [https://portal.mozz.us/gemini/gemini.circumlunar.space/software/ dedicated browser clients] available for many platforms.

== Gemtext Format ==

Gemini capsules consists of files made up using the [https://portal.mozz.us/gemini/gemini.circumlunar.space/docs/gemtext.gmi Gemtext markup language], but the protocol serve any file format.

Capsule owners can keep a [[gemblog]] for posting a blog on Gemini.

== Publication ==

Aggregators will collect new [[gemblog]] posts, which can also be server as Atoms feeds.

The platform has search engines dedicated to finding content on Gemini sites.
