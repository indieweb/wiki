'''<dfn>[https://pagespeed.web.dev/ Google PageSpeed Insights]</dfn>''' is a web-based page analysis tool run by [[Google]] that loads a page, its necessary resources, analyzes its [[performance]] and other factors and provides a score (based on [[Google Lighthouse]] performance).
