'''<dfn>[https://developers.google.com/speed/pagespeed/insights/?url= Google PageSpeed Insights]</dfn>''' is a web-based page analysis tool run by [[Google]] that loads a page, its necessary resources, analyzes its [[performance]] and other factors and provides a score (based on [[Google Lighthouse]] performance).
