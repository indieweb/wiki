'''<dfn>Google One</dfn>''' is what Google summarily calls all of its online cloud storage hosting, including storage in [[Gmail]], [[Google Photos]], and [[Google Drive]]. You can review and permanently delete large files/enclosures etc. at https://one.google.com/storage/management.
