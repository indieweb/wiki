'''<dfn>[https://developers.google.com/web/tools/lighthouse Google Lighthouse]</dfn>''' is an open source website testing tool for improving “[[performance]], [[accessibility]], [[progressive web app]]s, [[SEO]]”, that can be run via a website or in [[Chrome]] Dev Tools, and used by several members of the IndieWeb community to improve their personal sites.

It rates each point on a scale of 0-100 based on certain metrics.

Web-based version:
* [[Google PageSpeed Insights]]

== Why ==
Google Lighthouse is a tool for developers to improve their websites, and users can also use it to view a coarse evaluation of websites that they use, to provide feedback and requests to the developers of said websites.

== Open Source ==
* https://github.com/GoogleChrome/lighthouse
