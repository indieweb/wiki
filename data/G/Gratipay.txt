'''<dfn>Gratipay</dfn>''' (formerly Gittip) was a payment silo that allowed individuals and organizations to receive [[tip]]s to their Gratipay account which was associated with a number of different silo accounts such as Github, Bitbucket, Twitter, and shut down 2017-12-29.

== History ==
* 2017-12-29 Gratipay shut down: https://gratipay.news/the-end-cbfba8f50981
* 2015-05-07 Gratipay posted on [[Medium]]: https://medium.com/gratipay-blog/gratipocalypse-42fd0ec0d9e8 explaining that due to switching payment processors and attempting to better comply with financial regulations, they would be skipping their weekly payment cycle. 
* 2014-06-07 {{aaronpk}} requested  "website" profile field with [[rel-me]] support: https://github.com/gratipay/gratipay.com/issues/2477
