'''<dfn>Gratipay</dfn>''' (formerly Gittip) was a payment silo that allowed individuals and organizations to receive [[tip]]s to their Gratipay account which is associated with a number of different silo accounts such as github, bitbucket, twitter, openstreetmap, etc.  

[https://gratipay.news/the-end-cbfba8f50981 Gratipay shut down] 2017-12-29.

== Issues ==
On 2015-05-07 Gratipay posted on [[Medium]] explaining that due to switching payment processors and attempting to better comply with financial regulations, they would be skipping their weekly payment cycle. [https://medium.com/gratipay-blog/gratipocalypse-42fd0ec0d9e8]
