'''<dfn>[https://www.certificate-transparency.org/ Certificate Transparency]</dfn>''' is an initiative to publicly and verifiably log new TLS certificates to help detect wrong or malicious certificates quickly.

== Tools ==
* https://crt.sh/ can be used to search for certificates
* https://sslmate.com/certspotter/ monitors the logs and can send you an e-mail if a new certificate for one of your domains is issued
* [[Facebook]] runs a [https://www.facebook.com/notes/protect-the-graph/introducing-our-certificate-transparency-monitoring-tool/1811919779048165 similar service] (Facebook login required)
