'''<dfn>CMoS</dfn>''' is short for [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Chicago_Manual_of_Style Chicago Manual of Style], a reference for "normal" use of text spacing and punctuation to convey semantics that several in the indieweb community often use and refer to.
