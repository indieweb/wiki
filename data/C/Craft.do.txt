'''<dfn>Craft.do</dfn>''' is a platform for creating and collaboration on documents, notes, and projects that you can use to edit pages on your personal site.

== Features ==
Craft documents can:
* contain various types of content, such as text, images, videos, links, tables, and more. 
* be organized into folders, starred for easy access, and shared with others via links or email. 

Support documentation can be found at 
* https://support.craft.do/ 
There is also an excellent in-app help search system along with a collection of slash commands (commands invoked by typing '/') as well.
