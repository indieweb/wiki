'''<dfn>[https://github.com/cliss/camel Camel]</dfn>''' is a part static/part dynamic blogging platform written in [[Node.js]] by [https://www.caseyliss.com/ Casey Liss].

The Camel [[project]] is open source and available on [https://github.com/cliss/camel GitHub].

== IndieWeb examples ==
=== Casey Liss ===
[https://www.caseyliss.com/ Casey Liss] is self-dogfooding Camel on his own site at https://www.caseyliss.com/ since 2014. Prior to that he was using [[Tumbler]] since 2008.
