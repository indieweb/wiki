'''<dfn>sleep</dfn>''' is a [[passive]] [[metrics]] [[post]] type that indicates how much time (and often a graph of how deeply) a person has slept.

== Tools ==
* People with diagnosed obstructive sleep apnoea syndrome who are using data-capable CPAP machines (as well as oximeters) can use [http://www.sleepfiles.com/SH2/ SleepyHead], an open source tool to extract data from the SD card and get detailed metrics about their sleep including duration and various sleep quality metrics.
