    '''<dfn>nofollow</dfn>''' is a rel value meant to stop search engines imputing endorsement from a link. It has been overused by silos to keep SEO power for themselves rather than linked-to posts.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== ProcessWire Webmention ===
The [[ProcessWire#ProcessWire_Webmention|ProcessWire Webmention]] plugin skips rel=nofollow links when processing referrer links as potential [[Vouch]] URLs.
