'''<dfn>newwwyear</dfn>''' is a hashtag (#NewwwYear for better readability) proposed at the [https://twitter.com/jamiemchale/status/943319060271398912 end of 2017 by Jamie McHale] and [https://twitter.com/jensimmons/status/943323088405581824 popularized by Jen Simmons] to encourage people to tweet their personal website plan for the upcoming new year, similar to the annual IndieWeb [[commitments]] wiki pages that began at the end of 2014 with [[2015-01-01-commitments]].

See:
* '''[[indieweb commitments]]''' for the upcoming year’s IndieWeb commitments
* '''[[commitments]]''' for all past year’s IndieWeb new year commitments
