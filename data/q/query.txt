'''<dfn>query</dfn>''' is a technical term ([[jargon]]) that usually means to look something up in a [[database]] (database query), and may also mean a request made to a web site or service to look something up (like search query), more specifically for the IndieWeb, asking a [[Webmention]] handling service for a list of Webmentions send to a particular target.
