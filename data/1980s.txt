The '''<dfn>1980s</dfn>''' was a decade when several IndieWeb community members were born and when a particularly colorful aesthetic was popularized that has subsequently seen adoption in [[nostalgia]] contexts, like the [[#Homebrew_Website_Club_logo|Homebrew Website Club logo]].

There’s a lot of web/technology history that began in the 1980s. For such general (not indieweb-specific) web/tech history, please see (and add to) respective [[#Wikipedia_articles|Wikipedia articles]].

=== VHS tapes ===
VHS tapes and their sleeves had a particular aesthetic design in the 1980s that captured the imagination, especially in retrospect and has inspired similar designs for modern products, services, and events!
