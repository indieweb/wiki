'''<dfn>[http://pump.io/ pump.io]</dfn>''' is an open source [[project]] for "a stream server that does most of what people really want from a social network."

* github: https://github.com/e14n/pump.io

See also, Evan explains pump.io on IRC: http://indiewebcamp.com/irc/2013-06-10#t1370892851

Evan also explains pump.io and federated social web on the [http://notapodcast.co.uk/post/57337280889/9-the-prince-of-aberystwyth TINAP podcast].

== IndieWeb Compatibility ==

pump.io uses its own protocol for communicating between servers, based on ActivityStreams. There are no known examples of communication to/from indieweb sites.

[https://e14n.com/evan Evan P] ''has'' [http://bret.io/2013/08/08/t5/ expressed interest] in pump.io communicating via indieweb style [[comment | comments]].

Evan has indicated he is interested in receiving contributions to pump.io that will implement indieweb standards:

* [[webmention]], etc.

* [https://github.com/e14n/pump.io/pull/871 Closed by Evan P] - 2014-06-22
