'''<dfn>[http://ytdl-org.github.io/youtube-dl/ youtube_dl]</dfn>''' is a command-line program to download videos from [[YouTube|YouTube.com]] and a [http://ytdl-org.github.io/youtube-dl/supportedsites.html few more sites] which some indie sites use to export their content from [[YouTube]]. It is released to the public domain, which means you can modify it, redistribute it or use it however you like.


== IndieWeb Examples == 
* IndieWeb [[friendly]] service [[Huffduffer]] has a bookmarklet that posts audio to one's account. {{snarfed}} [https://snarfed.org/2015-03-07_huffduff-video created] http://huffduff-video.snarfed.org/ using youtube_dl to extract audio from YouTube videos to send to Huffduffer from which one can also download and save to one's website.
* {{gwg}} has used how it looks at video pages for improving the [https://github.com/dshanske/parse-this Parse This] tool/plugin which is used in several places including for [[Post Kinds Plugin]] for [[WordPress]]
