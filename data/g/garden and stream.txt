'''<dfn>garden and stream</dfn>''' is a metaphor for different kinds and aspects of websites, contrasting growing & linking content slowly & incrementally by topic, versus posting & presenting time-based content chronologically, both discussed in blog posts and IndieWebCamp sessions.
