'''<dfn>gopost</dfn>''' is a tool to make posts to several social networks at once and also generates a web HTML snippet to embed in your (static) website.

This is work in progress, currently Mastodon toots and file snippets are working.

The project is on [https://github.com/Kulbartsch/gopost Github].

'''gopost''' were started by [[User:Wolkenbruch.net|Alexander Kulbartsch]].
