'''<dfn>goo.gl</dfn>''' is [[Google]]'s [[link shortener]] service that will stop redirecting short links on 2025-08-25. Google announced that starting 2018-04-13, no new short links could be created, but existing links would continue to redirect. On 2024-07-18, they announced that existing links would no longer redirect after 2025-08-25.
