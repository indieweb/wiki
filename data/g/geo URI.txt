    '''<dfn>geo URI</dfn>''' is a URI scheme for describing the latitude and longitude of a specific location, and is used by some [[Micropub]] clients as an easy way to specify the [[location]] of a [[post]]. e.g., <code>geo:45.51533,-122.64653</code>.
