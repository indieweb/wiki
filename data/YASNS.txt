'''<dfn>YASNS</dfn>''' is short for Yet Another Social Networking Service and refers to the ever repeating cycle of new social network [[silos]] emerging, inviting "key" users, perhaps with limited invitations, and people rebuilding their "friends" lists for the nth time.
