'''<dfn>[https://keybase.io/ Keybase]</dfn>''' is an unmaintained [[GPG]] key [[silo]] acquired by [[Zoom]] in 2020, though it does support the [[rel-me]] IndieWeb [[building block]], and was used by a few community members.

* 2020-05-07: Keybase app/site no longer maintained, Keybase team acquired by Zoom and working on Zoom security instead https://keybase.io/blog/keybase-joins-zoom
