'''<dfn>[https://kagi.com/ Kagi]</dfn>''' is a company best known for its [[search]] engine and its [[small web]] directory. It originally launched in 2018 as Kagi.ai,<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20190822195619/https://kagi.ai/about.html Web Archive: Kagi.ai - About Us]</ref><ref>[https://help.kagi.com/kagi/ai/kagi-ai.html Kagi Docs: Kagi AI]</ref> and it added [[LLM]] features to its search engine in 2023.<ref>[https://blog.kagi.com/kagi-ai-search Kagi Blog: Kagi's approach to AI in search]</ref>
