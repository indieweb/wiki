'''<dfn>[https://kapowski.schmarty.net/ Kapowski]</dfn>''' is a [[Micropub]] client for posting animated GIF [[photo]]s to your website using [[Micropub]], powered by [[Gfycat]] search.

Kapowski is written in [[PHP]] and hosted at https://kapowski.bayside.pub/

== Features ==

Kapowski supports:

* searching [[Gfycat]] for GIFs
* creating [[photo]] posts via Micropub
** a post can be a [[photo reply]] using [[u-in-reply-to]]
* without signing in, users are shown an HTML snippet they can copy/paste to their site.
