'''<dfn>iCloud</dfn>''' is what [[Apple]] summarily calls all of its various online services such as [[Apple ID]], online storage, syncing, as well as a [[silo]] of sorts with iCloud identifiers and small group photo sharing.

== Status ==
You can check the status of various Apple online services here:
* http://www.apple.com/support/systemstatus/

== Criticism ==

=== Backup ===
iOS 9.x has a UI in the Settings app (Storage option) for enabling various backup features, which use iCloud:

<img src="http://i.imgur.com/rtNm6k1.png" style="max-width:480px" alt="Screenshot of iOS Settings Storage options" />

Note: the "Photo Library" backup option is different from the "iCloud Photo Library" and "My Photo Stream" options in the explicit iCloud Photos preferences above.
