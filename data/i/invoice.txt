'''<dfn>invoice</dfn>''' is a request for payment for specific goods or services.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} has sent invoices as plaintext posts requesting reimbursement for things. The posts are in reply to a person's home page, are person-tagged, include a text description and amount of the request, and include a link to the [[pay]] page where payment can be sent from.
