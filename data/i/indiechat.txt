'''<dfn>#indieweb-chat</dfn>''' (formerly #indiechat) is an [[IRC]] channel on freenode.net for off-topic chat for the IndieWeb community. Unlike the main [[IRC|#indieweb]] channel, the #indieweb-chat channel is not publicly logged, though it is bridged with Slack.
