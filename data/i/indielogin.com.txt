'''<dfn>indielogin.com</dfn>''' is a service that developers use to log users in to a website. you create a web sign-in form that asks for their domain, and it directs people to indielogin.com to authenticate. users can authenticate by linking to Twitter/GitHub/email address/PGP key, and then they are asked to authenticate at one of those when signing in.
