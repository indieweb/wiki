'''<dfn>#indieweb-random</dfn>''' (formerly #indieweb-chat and before that #indiechat) is an [[IRC]] channel on Libera for off-topic chat for the IndieWeb community. Unlike the main [[IRC|#indieweb]] channel, the #indieweb-random channel is not publicly logged, though it is bridged with Slack and Discord.
