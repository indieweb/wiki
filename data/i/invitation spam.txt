'''<dfn>invitation spam</dfn>''' is a particular form of [[spam]] that has the form of offering to the recipient, access to a [[post]] or [[stream]], membership in a [[group]], participation in a [[event]], or some other capability that is typically not available to the public or just anyone, though there are exceptions like being invited to [[like]] a [[Facebook]] page.
