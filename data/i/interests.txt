[[Category:Slashpages]]

An '''<dfn>interests</dfn>''' page lists things people enjoy spending time exploring or doing, sometimes describing why, and typically published as a top level [[page]] on IndieWeb sites. 

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{artlung}} has had an [https://artlung.com/interests/ /interests page] since November 2025
* {{zachary.kai}} has had [https://zacharykai.net/interests an /interests page] since Jun 2024
* {{addyourself}}

== Services ==
=== The /interests Directory ===
[https://chrisburnell.github.io/interests-directory/ The /interests Directory] is a collection of sites that links to people's interests pages.

== Origin ==
The idea was proposed as an addition to [[now]] and [[about]] pages with the post ''[https://chrisburnell.com/note/slash-interests What are you interested in?]''
