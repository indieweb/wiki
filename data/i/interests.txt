[[Category:Slashpages]]

An '''<dfn>interests</dfn>''' page lists those things people are passionate about. A typical interests page expounds about them as a top level [[page]] on IndieWeb sites.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{artlung}} has had an [https://artlung.com/interests/ /interests page] since November 2025
* {{zachary.kai}} has had [https://zacharykai.net/interests an /interests page] since Jun 2024
* [[User:Peterspath.net|Peter's Path]] has an interests page at [https://peterspath.net/interests/ https://peterspath.net/interests/]

* {{addyourself}}

== Services ==
=== The /interests Directory ===
[https://chrisburnell.github.io/interests-directory/ The /interests Directory] is a collection of sites that links to people's interests pages.

== Origin ==
The idea was proposed as an addition to [[now]] and [[about]] pages in [https://chrisburnell.com Chris Burnell's] post ''[https://chrisburnell.com/note/slash-interests What are you interested in?]''
