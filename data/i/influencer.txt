'''<dfn>influencer</dfn>''' is a person with a large number of [[followers]], typically on social media [[silo]] profiles, but often also on their own independent blogs, and who receive a lot of [[responses]] on their posts, that brands sometimes seek out, and othertimes themselves seek out brands, to do deals for goods and/or services in exchange for posts. Being or becoming an influencer may be another motivation to [[POSSE]] your posts, to expand.
