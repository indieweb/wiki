An '''<dfn>indieweb comment hosting silo service</dfn>''' is a [[comment]] hosting [[silo]] that is indieweb [[friendly]], that is at a minimum allows you to create and host [[reply]] posts properly marked up with [[h-entry]] and [[in-reply-to]], and sends [[webmentions]] for them as well.

== Examples ==
* {{eliemichel}}'s http://tools.exppad.com/Replier/
* http://withknown.com/ can be used as an indieweb comment hosting silo service, since it supports replies and sends webmentions.
