'''<dfn>Indigenous for Android</dfn>''' is a native app for Android and currently in early development that supports posting your website using [[Micropub]] and a built-in [[reader]] that supports [[Microsub]].

The [[project]] is under active development by [https://indieweb.org/User:Realize.be Kristof de Jaeger] and can be found on [https://github.com/swentel/indigenous-android GitHub]. There is also an [https://indieweb.org/indigenous_for_ios iOS version].
