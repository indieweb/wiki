'''<dfn>[http://readmill.com/ Readmill]</dfn>''' was a [[silo]] for reading ebooks and making annotations. It is [[silo-deaths#Readmill|shutting down]] on 2014-07-01.

== Exports ==
Exports are available from Readmill and produce files in various formats.

[http://readmill.com/export Their data export] (requires login) outputs .zip archive with .txt .json .xml plus a bunch of assets.
