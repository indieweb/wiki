'''<dfn>multitag</dfn>''' is when you combine multiple tags in the same feed seperated by commas.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Eddie Hinkle ===
*{{eddiehinkle.com}} has added multitag support to his tag feed pages [https://eddiehinkle.com/2018/12/12/28/note/ as of 2018-12-12].
** Multitags are used in the html tag feed pages, the json feed pages and the rss feed pages. Examples listed below:
*** https://eddiehinkle.com/tag/fiona,jasmine,cat,cats/
*** https://eddiehinkle.com/tag/fiona,jasmine,cat,cats/feed.json
*** https://eddiehinkle.com/tag/fiona,jasmine,cat,cats/feed.xml
