'''<dfn>makko</dfn>''' is a single-binary [[static site generator]] written in Zig by the [http://starlightnet.work/ Starlight Network] community. It is configured in [[JSON]] and features [[markdown]] pages, Mustache templates, [[YAML]] [[frontmatter|frontmatters]] and feed generation in the [[RSS]] and [[Atom]] formats.
