'''<dfn>[http://mention-tech.appspot.com/ mention-tech]</dfn>''' is a service that can receive webmentions both via [[Webmention]] and a form on its home page, displays them there after verifying, and also has forms to send a webmention, ping Internet Archive to save a page, and send webmentions for all links on a page.
