'''<dfn>[http://microformats.org/wiki/microformats2-json microformats2 JSON]</dfn>''' is the canonical output format of the microformats2 parsing algorithm. As such it can be used to compare parsers and create test suites. It is also used as the official serialisation format of microformats objects, and relied upon by specifications such as Micropub.
