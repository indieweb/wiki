    '''<dfn>manual until it hurts</dfn>''' is an IndieWeb development practice of resisting automating something until you have manually done it enough times to really understand it, and know that it is worth doing and automating.

== Brainstorming ==

{{tantek}} [https://chat.indieweb.org/2018-04-05#t1522946439115700 proposes thinking in terms of cost/benefit]: manual as long as the expected cost of doing it manually is less than the cost of setting up and maintaining automation.
