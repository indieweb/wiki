'''<dfn>metacrap</dfn>''' is a general purpose derogatory term referring to the poor quality of meta information like those in meta tags category, description, [[OGP]], [[Twitter Cards]], and other [[DRY]] violations outside the primary content of a page, introduced in a [https://people.well.com/user/doctorow/metacrap.htm 2001 essay by Cory Doctorow].
