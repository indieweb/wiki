'''<dfn>microsyntax</dfn>''' refers to short text conventions for conveying specific semantic meaning, such as an "@" prefix indicating a (user)name, or "#" prefix indicating a [[hashtag]] (both conventions popularized by [[Twitter]]).

== Why ==
=== Why author ===
When writing/authoring, it‚Äôs often easier to use a little bit of inline punctuation like an '@' or a '#' as a microsyntax to express some additional meaning rather than adding explicit markup or even [[markdown]].
