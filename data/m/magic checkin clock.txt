A '''<dfn>magic checkin clock</dfn>''' is a physical analog-style clock where the hand(s) display the current location(s) of one or more persons who publish their location with [[checkins]], typically via Foursquare's [[Swarm]], based on the Weasley Clock.[http://harrypotter.wikia.com/wiki/Weasley_Clock]
