'''<dfn>marchitecture</dfn>''' (or <dfn>marketecture</dfn>) is a term for [[architecture]]-like diagrams that contain lots of jargon, buzzwords, or marketing phrases for sales or political reasons yet make little actual sense, especially if you look at the apparent or implied relationships therein.
