'''<dfn>microcaching</dfn>''' is a technique of caching a web server response for a very short period of time to reduce the effects of the [[thundering herd]] problem.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{aaronpk}} has configured his web server with a 30 second microcache using nginx.
