'''<dfn>microcaching</dfn>''' is a technique of caching web server responses for a very short period of time (seconds) to reduce the effects of the [[thundering herd]] problem, while also avoiding some of the pitfalls of correctly invalidating caches.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{aaronpk}} has configured his web server with a 30 second microcache using nginx.
