== My IndieWeb Setup ==
My web site is running Wordpress with the following plugins :
* [https://github.com/jihaisse/custom-feed-url Custom Feed URL], Customize your feed URL
* [https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-hashtags Hashtags], Adds twitter like hashtags to your blog-posts
* [https://github.com/willnorris/wordpress-hum Hum], Personal URL shortener for WordPress
* [https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-indieauth/ IndieAuth], IndieAuth for WordPress
* [http://wordpress.org/plugins/indieauth-links/ IndieAuth Links], enable sign in with your domain name.
* [https://github.com/jihaisse/social A fork of] [http://wordpress.org/plugins/social/ Social] POSSE to twitter and Facebook. Backfeed from twitter & Facebook
* [https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-webmention Webmention], Webmention support for WordPress posts/comments
* [http://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-facebook-open-graph-protocol/ WP Facebook Open Graph protocol], Adds proper Facebook Open Graph Meta tags and values to your site 
* [https://github.com/wp-plugins/jm-twitter-cards Twitter cards], Twitter cards integration on WordPress

And [http://wordpress.org/themes/sempress SemPress] mf2 theme with [https://github.com/jihaisse/SemPress-Lite Lite children theme].
