== my indie web setup ==
http://t37.net is my primary place where I post:
* Full articles (in French) I then POSSE on [[Twitter]] http://twitter.com/fdevillamil through abbreviated short statuses.
* Short (~ 140 characters) statuses (mostly in English) I POSSE on [[Twitter]] http://twitter.com/fdevillamil
* Shortened links using a built-in link shortener

== my indie web projects ==
[https://github.com/fdv/publify/ Publify] is a Ruby on Rails blogging platform started in 2006. It's currently evolving to an indie Web publication platform with:
* Blog posts (RSS/Atom, Blogger and MT API).
* Short statuses that POSSE on Twitter with link back.
* Self hosted URL shortener.
