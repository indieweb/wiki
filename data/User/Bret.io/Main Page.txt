== Welcome to the IndieWeb ==
<strong>Own your data.</strong> [[own_your_data| Create and publish content on your own site]], and optionally [[POSSE| syndicate copies to third-party silos]]. 

This is the basis of the <strong><dfn>Indie Web</dfn></strong>. 

For more, see [[principles]], [[why|why]] and what [[Distinguishing | distinguishes]] the IndieWeb from other projects. Interested? See [[User:Bret.io/Main_Page#How |how to get started]].
