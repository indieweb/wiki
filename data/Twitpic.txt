'''<dfn>[http://twitpic.com Twitpic]</dfn>''' was a [[photo]] [[silo]] designed for sharing photos on [[Twitter]]. It will be [http://blog.twitpic.com/2014/09/twitpic-is-shutting-down/ shut down] October 25, 2014.

== Usernames ==
Twitpic re-uses [[Twitter]] usernames.

== Feeds ==
Public [[feeds]] are available of Twitpic users' posts, e.g. for user @aplusk:
* http://twitpic.com/photos/aplusk/feed.rss
