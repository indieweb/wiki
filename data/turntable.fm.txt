<dfn>[http://turntable.fm Turntable.fm]</dfn> was a social music sharing website and mobile application that closed in 2014. In December 2013 it was announced they would shut down the service to focus on Turntable Live, allowing musicians to perform online concerts. By early 2014, Turntable Live had quietly disappeared. Turntable.fm does not resolve currently and turntable.com is listed as being for sale.
