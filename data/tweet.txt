A '''<dfn>tweet</dfn>''' is a [[note]] (or [[reply]]) posted on [[Twitter]].

== History ==
=== 2006 ===
2006-11-14 Before "tweet" meant a post on Twitter. The Twitter blog post [https://blog.twitter.com/2006/six-more-twitter-updates Six More Twitter Updates!] refers to Twitter posts by other terms (<strong>emphasis added</strong>)
* singular "Twitter update" as in <blockquote>When you come across a <strong>Twitter update</strong> that you really like you can save it by clicking the little star.</blockquote>
* plural "Twitters" as in <blockquote>[…] pick favorites we can put together fun pages like the Top 10 Favorite <strong>Twitters</strong>.(Note: The Top 10 […]</blockquote>

=== 2007 ===
2007: The term "tweet" to refer to Twitter [[posts]] was introduced in the Twitterific app[http://furbo.org/2011/03/11/twitterrific-firsts/][http://www.quora.com/What-is-the-origin-of-the-word-tweet][http://furbo.org/2013/06/28/the-origin-of-tweet/]
