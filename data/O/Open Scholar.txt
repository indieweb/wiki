'''<dfn>[https://osprojectsite.org/node/671 Open Scholar]</dfn>''' is a prepackaged [[Drupal]] install with preinstalled modules geared toward the higher education space for making running and maintaining a website easier.

The open source [https://github.com/openscholar/openscholar development version of Open Scholar] can be found on [[GitHub]].
