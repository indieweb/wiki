'''<dfn>Overland</dfn>''' is an [[iOS]] and [[Android]] app for location tracking that can send its data to a custom server. {{aaronpk}} uses Overland to send his location to his website to do things like automatically set the timezone of posts, track bike rides and train trips, display the local weather, and more.

* [https://overland.p3k.app/download Download from the App Store]
* [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.openhumans.app.overland Download from Google Play]

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{aaronpk}} stores data from Overland in [[Compass]]
** {{eddiehinkle.com}} has the same setup
* {{cleverdevil}} [https://micro.blog/cleverdevil/2200605 stores data] from Overland in Amazon [[S3]], and created a presentation about his setup ([https://cleverdevil.io/s/dlag1GnFrU.pdf PDF])
* [https://samwilson.id.au/ samwilson] uses Overland to geotag posts in his blog
