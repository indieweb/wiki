'''<dfn>[http://owntracks.org OwnTracks]</dfn>''' is an open source app for tracking your location and sharing with others, available on iOS and (unmaintained) Android.

The Android version has been declared unmaintained [https://github.com/owntracks/android 2017-10-14]. The developer [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.owntracks.android recommends] [http://www.barbaro.it/cms/index.php/it/android Zanzito] as a replacement.
