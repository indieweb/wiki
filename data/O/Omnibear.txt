'''<dfn>[https://omnibear.com/ Omnibear]</dfn>''' is an open source browser extension for posting text notes, replies, bookmarks, and likes to your website using [[Micropub]] created by [[User:Keithjgrant.com|Keith J. Grant]].

* Chrome: TBD <!-- previous link: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/omnibear/cjieakdeocmiimmphkfhdfbihhncoocn -->

== Features ==
IndieWeb features that Omnibear supports:
* publishing posts:
** [[note]]
** [[reply]]
** [[bookmark]]
** [[favorite]]
** [[repost]]
* along with
** [[tags]]
** [[slugs]]
** Identifying sites that support [[webmentions]] (icon when responding/reacting to posts)

You can right-click on an any [[h-entry]] to reply, like, or repost it.
