'''<dfn>October</dfn>''' is the 10th month of the Gregorian calendar year and a month of several blogging challenges such as [[Blogtober]], [[Inktober]], [[Mathober]], [[Weird Web October]], also a month where some sites display a seasonal [[Halloween]] theme, or may be in reference to [[October CMS]].
