'''<dfn>OpenSocial</dfn>''' was a "standards-based component model for cloud based social apps" formerly at <nowiki>http://opensocial.org/</nowiki> which now redirects (along with all URLs at that domain) to a W3C blog post: http://www.w3.org/blog/2014/12/opensocial-foundation-moves-standards-work-to-w3c-social-web-activity/

== 2014 ==
OpenSocial closed and gave everything to W3C.
* http://www.w3.org/2014/12/opensocial.html.en
* http://www.w3.org/blog/2014/12/opensocial-foundation-moves-standards-work-to-w3c-social-web-activity/

== 2013 == 
OpenSocial was discussed at the [[2013/osfw3c|2013 W3C Social Web workshop]].
