'''<dfn>blogiversary</dfn>''' is a portmanteau of blog plus anniversary and refers to both the anniversary of when a blog was started and a post (usually a note, sometimes an article) that commemorates it.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
Earliest first:
=== Kevin Marks ===
{{kevinmarks}} has been posting blogiversary posts since 2002.
* 2002: http://epeus.blogspot.com/2002/11/blogiversary.html
* 2006: https://epeus.blogspot.com/2006/11/my-blogs-fifth-birthday.html

=== Tantek ===
{{t}} used to post blogiversary posts, as recently as 2008:
* 2008: [http://tantek.com/log/2008/08.html#d08t1923 6th blogiversary]

=== Manton ===
{{manton}} usually has a quick microblog post to mark the anniversary of his blog, and sometimes longer posts like these:
* 2012: https://www.manton.org/2012/03/09/years-and-signals.html
* 2011: https://www.manton.org/2011/03/09/wayback.html
* 2007: https://www.manton.org/2007/03/10/weblog-th-year.html
* 2006: https://www.manton.org/2006/03/09/mediocrity-is-the.html
