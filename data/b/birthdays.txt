'''<dfn>birthdays</dfn>''' is a feature on some social media [[silos]] that shows a page of people with current birthdays (today, recently, or soon), sometimes with how old they are, and UI to interact with them, sometimes with a daily notification of whose / how many people's birthday it is. E.g. https://www.facebook.com/events/birthdays/ (needs screenshot).
