'''<dfn>blog archive format</dfn>''' is a data format proposed by {{manton}} for the export of a blog, based on a zip file and top level HTML [[h-feed]] inside, that is supported by [[micro.blog]]. Original proposal: https://www.manton.org/2017/11/24/blog-archive-format.html.
