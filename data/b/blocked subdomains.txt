https://indieweb.org/blocked_subdomains

<span class="p-summary">The '''<dfn>blocked subdomains</dfn>''' on the wiki are subdomains offered by hosting providers that cannot be used to login with.</span> People who use subdomains of these providers to host their [[FAQ#What_is_a_personal_website|personal website]] will have to get a [[personal domain]] before they can login.

They can currently only be found in [https://github.com/indieweb/mediawiki-extensions/blob/master/LassoAuth.php the LassoAuth extension code]. As of 2018-01-14 [https://github.com/indieweb/mediawiki-extensions/blob/196b02b6c688b1a026b51f3c80d4e912304899b8/LassoAuth.php#L17-L27 the list of domains] that cannot be used to login is:

* commentpara.de
* github.io
* wordpress.com
* blogspot.com
* livejournal.com
* indiewebcamp.com
* indieweb.org
* herokuapp.com
* jsbin.com
