'''<dfn>bridge all the things</dfn>''' is a nascent IndieWeb philosophy that prioritizes interoperability over ideology, NIH, historical disagreements, etc. ecosystems are valuable and powerful, and one easy way to extend an ecosystem is to bridge it with other existing ecosystems.
