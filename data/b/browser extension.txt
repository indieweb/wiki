'''<dfn>browser extension</dfn>''' (aka <dfn>add-on</dfn>) is a third-party addition to a [[browser]] enabling new features for users such as new ways to interact with websites.

You may be looking for [[WebExtensions]] for the cross-browser framework for building browser extensions, or [[Safari Extensions]] for the history of extension support in Safari.
