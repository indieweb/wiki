'''<dfn>backfill</dfn>''' is the action of importing all your past posts, typically from a social media silo, into your own site.

== Indieweb Examples ==
* {{chrisaldrich}} has used import to backfill posts from past silo sites including [[WordPress.com]], [[Posterous]], and [[Twitpic]] amongst others.
* [[User:petermolnar.eu|Peter Molnar]] has [https://petermolnar.net/personal-website-as-archiving-vault/ written about some of his backfill work] as well as his reasons why.
