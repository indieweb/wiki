'''<dfn>[https://b2evolution.net/ b2evolution]</dfn>''' is an open source blogging/[[CMS]] platform, one of the original surviving forks of the B2 blog engine, and added [[Webmention]] support 2019-03-22. [https://b2evolution.net/news/2019/03/22/b2evolution-6-11-beta-with]

== IndieWeb Support ==
* [[Webmention]]:  [https://ramblinggit.com/2019/01/webmentions-on-b2evolutions-todo-list/ Discussion on Ramblinggit.com] (in comments) where B2evolution maintainer noted that they "have added Webmentions support to the todo list".
* Update: 2019-03-22 [https://b2evolution.net/news/2019/03/22/b2evolution-6-11-beta-with B2evolution adds Webmentions support to version 6.11.0-beta]
* Update 2019-12-15 [https://ramblinggit.com/2019/12/15/i-just-set.html Brad Enslen confirms that it sends/receives Webmention].
