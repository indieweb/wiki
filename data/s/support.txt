'''<dfn>support</dfn>''' is related to all things regarding issues reported to a site or silo, like when you [[report abuse]], and receive a response in a support inbox.

== Silo Examples ==

=== Facebook ===

[[Facebook]] has a separate support inbox, accessible on the web at https://www.facebook.com/support/. They use this to report back on [[report_abuse#Facebook|reports]], among other things.
