'''<dfn>suggest location</dfn>''' is a [[web action]] for posting a location response to a post indicating location information for the post, like where a photo was taken, supported by [[Facebook]] on [[photo]] posts lacking location.

== Silo Examples ==
=== Facebook ===
[[Facebook]] supports the "Suggest Location" web action on some others's [[photo]] posts.
