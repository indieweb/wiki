'''<dfn>Small Web</dfn>''' is a confusing term recently introduced by Aral Balkan in [https://ar.al/2020/08/07/what-is-the-small-web/ a blog post]. It describes a subset of indie web [[principles]], but applies them in narrow way, and thus claims to be different. It's a good example of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narcissism_of_small_differences the narcissism of small differences].

[https://neustadt.fr/essays/the-small-web/ Rediscovering the Small Web], written by Parimal Satyal, is a post extolling the virtues of handmade HTML web pages
