'''<dfn>sleep</dfn>''' is a [[passive]] [[metrics]] [[post]] type that indicates how much time (and often a graph of how deeply) a person has slept.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} uses [[p3k]] to post sleep posts to aaronparecki.com since [https://aaronparecki.com/2011/11/27/1/ 2011-11-27]. The data is imported from [[Jawbone]] or [[FitBit]]. Examples:
* https://aaronparecki.com/2014/07/28/1/

Since 2016-07-06, some of the sleep posts include the indoor temperature:
* https://aaronparecki.com/2016/07/07/3/

Some details about how the sleep data is extracted from the FitBit API:
* https://aaronparecki.com/2017/01/31/8/day-42-fitbit-sleep

== Tools ==
* People with diagnosed obstructive sleep apnoea syndrome who are using data-capable CPAP machines (as well as oximeters) can use [http://www.sleepfiles.com/SH2/ SleepyHead], an open source tool to extract data from the SD card and get detailed metrics about their sleep including duration and various sleep quality metrics.
