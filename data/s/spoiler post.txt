'''<dfn>spoiler post</dfn>''' is a post that contains information that will spoil something such as a movie, in the IndieWeb this would be a post that communicated to a parser/reader that the content of the post might spoil info about a new book, tv show, movie, etc.

__TOC__

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Marty McGuire ===
{{martymcguire}} uses details and summary elements to provide text transcripts for each post of [[TWITIWAE|This Week in the IndieWeb Audio Edition]] that are hidden by default with a “Show/Hide Transcript” click target to reveal them.
* [https://martymcgui.re/2017/08/19/140447/ First use 2017-08-19]

=== Goodreads ===
Goodreads has the ability to mark a review as containing spoilers.
