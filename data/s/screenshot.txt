A <dfn>screenshot</dfn> is an image of a computer screen or a website.

Website screenshots can be shown with [[bookmark]]s in the [[reply-context]] or as a [[photo]].

== Indieweb Examples ==
* {{aaronpk}} shows screenshots of websites in [https://aaronparecki.com/bookmarks his bookmarks].
* {{aaronpk}} automatically uploads screenshots to his [[media endpoint]] to quickly share a URL [https://aaronparecki.com/2016/11/02/5/screenshots].
* {{cweiske}} uses [https://cweiske.de/phancap.htm phancap] to display small screenshots on his private bookmark service.
