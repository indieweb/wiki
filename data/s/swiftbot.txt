'''<dfn>swiftbot</dfn>''' is a [[bot]] in #indieweb-chat which returns the most related Taylor Swift lyric (from her first nine studio albums) to a query, made by {{capjamesg}} for [[joy]] in the community.

== Usage ==

<pre>
[tantek]: !ts December
swiftbot: I go back to December all the time All the time
</pre>
