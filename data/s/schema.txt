A '''<dfn>schema</dfn>''' in the context of the indieweb typically refers to an explicitly structured way of organizing the various aspects of information used to construct personal site posts and other content, either informally or specific to their own site like in a custom [[flat file]] storage format (perhaps [[JSON Schema]]) or a [[database]], or standard [[microformats]] vocabularies like [[h-card]], [[h-entry]], [[h-event]], and more. In some contexts (especially [[SEO]]) ''schema'' or especially capitalized ''Schema'' is used as a shorthand for [[schema.org]].
