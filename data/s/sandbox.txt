The '''<dfn>sandbox</dfn>''' attribute of the HTML5 <code>&lt;iframe&gt;</code> element can be used to greatly restrict what the [[embed|framed]] content can do.

== Why ==
Consider using "iframe sandbox" to restrict what embedded iframes (e.g. [[auto-embed]]s of [[YouTube]] or [[Vimeo]] or other [[video|videos]] [[silo|silos]]) can do.
