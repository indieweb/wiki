A '''<dfn>salary</dfn>''' is the periodic payment an employee receives from an employer, often kept private, however sometimes disclosed (even on personal sites) to improve visibility and transparency.

Salaries are seen as very personal, and very often not discussed, even with family and friends.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Jamie Tanna ===
{{jamietanna}} has started to track his salary on his [https://www.jvt.me/salary/ /salary/] page, [https://www.jvt.me/posts/2021/09/09/public-salary-history/ as per his announce blog post], as a means to improve visibility and transparency.
