A '''<dfn>stream</dfn>''' refers to a collection of [[posts]], typically time-ordered, similar to a [[feed]], and often on a [[homepage]] published as HTML, sometimes updated in real-time, with updates propagated via a notification protocol.

A [[composite stream]] includes multiple post types.

=== How to publish a stream ===

* [[h-feed]]
* [[h-entry]]

* [[WebSub]] notification protocol
