A '''<dfn>stream</dfn>''' refers to a collection of [[posts]], typically time-ordered, similar to a [[feed]], and often on a [[homepage]] published as HTML, sometimes updated in real-time, with updates propagated via a notification protocol, or may refer to the [[discuss#stream|#stream chat channel]].

A [[composite stream]] includes multiple post types.

If you are looking for the stream of indieweb related posts or channel, see:
* '''[[stream.indieweb.org]]'''

== Other Examples ==
=== Quartz ===
The news publication [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quartz_(publication) Quartz]'s mobile app announces stories in the form of a conversation: users "chat" with the app in order to find out more about stories they're interested in.

[[File:IMG_2036.PNG]]

In November 2016, {{benwerd}} announced that he was going to implement this style of publishing for his personal website.

Its website displays stories as a continuous scroll.
