A '''<dfn>system font stack</dfn>''' is a collection of fonts you can use in your website that match fonts that are available on most devices.

== Why ==
System fonts avoid the loading time & bandwidth of custom [[web font]]s and can make your site look more native to the system people are browsing on.

* https://systemfontstack.com
