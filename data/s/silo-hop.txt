'''<dfn>silo-hop</dfn>''' is when someone quits a silo and moves their content to another silo.

== Services ==
* [[Flickstagram]] was a service that allowed you to silo-hop from [[Instagram]] to [[Flickr]] (2012)
* [https://www.adweek.com/performance-marketing/moving-from-myspace-to-facebook-import-myspace-profile-to-facebook-with-spacelift/ SpaceLift] was a service that allows you to silo-hop from [[MySpace]] to [[Facebook]] (2007)
