'''<dfn>[https://silo.pub silo.pub]</dfn>''' is a [[Micropub]] endpoint for hosted blogs like [[Tumblr]], [[WordPress.com]], [[Blogger]], and [[Twitter]]. It aims to implement the ideas laid out in the post [https://snarfed.org/micropub-for-hosted-blogs Micropub for Hosted Blogs].

== History ==
silo.pub was temporary named "Feverdream" while it was being developed. And hosted at https://feverdream.herokuapp.com during that period.
