'''<dfn>Windows Live Spaces</dfn>''' was a blogging and social networking platform from Microsoft, also known as MSN Spaces. It was shut down in 2011 but unlike many others the offered a migration option to [[Wordpress.com]]. See https://en.blog.wordpress.com/2010/09/27/welcome-windows-live-spaces-bloggers/
