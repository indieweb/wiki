[[Category:HTTP status code]]

'''<dfn>404</dfn>''' is not found — or it is an HTTP/1.1 Status Code returned from a web server to signify that the requested URI was not found.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{lazcorp}}: https://www.lazaruscorporation.co.uk/404 has a choose-your-own-adventure story the user can follow.
* {{capjamesg}}: http://jamesg.blog/404 invites readers to explore other links on his website.
* {{cupparex}}: https://crystaltouchton.com/404 has a cute 404.
* {{aciccarello}}: https://www.ciccarello.me/404 has a fun gif and links to contact him or check the internet archive
* {{kupietz}}: accessing a nonexistent page like https://michaelkupietz.com/ekiekiekiptangzoomboinggonzowie will display a randomly selected 404 image, which was something of a technical challenge due to local and Cloudflare page caching. There's also a minor easter egg.
