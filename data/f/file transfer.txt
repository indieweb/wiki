Transferring files to and from friends, family, and across your devices is a necessary yet often time-consuming part of internet existence!

Here is a compiled list of methods to make it easier.

== Methods ==
* Air Drop for Apple Devices
* Dropbox, Google Drive, iCloud, Mega
* https://filegarden.com/
* https://localsend.org/
* Windows Link for Android + Windows
* https://wormhole.app/
* You can direct message yourself on Slack and WhatsApp. Not directly on Discord, but as ribo.zone pointed out, you can create a group DM and not add anyone.
