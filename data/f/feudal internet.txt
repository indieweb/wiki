'''<dfn>feudal internet</dfn>''' is a metaphor for the [[silo]]-dominated web introduced in Bruce Schneier’s 2013 article <cite>[https://hbr.org/2013/06/you-have-no-control-over-s You Have No Control Over Security on the Feudal Internet]</cite>.

Article timeline:
* (C) 2012-11-26 Bruce Schneier: [https://www.wired.com/2012/11/feudal-security/ When It Comes to Security, We're Back to Feudalism] — introduces using the concept of ''feudalism'' as a metaphor for the security of web and internet services.
* (T) 2013-06-06 Bruce Schneier: [https://hbr.org/2013/06/you-have-no-control-over-s You Have No Control Over Security on the Feudal Internet] — introduced the actual phrase "feudal internet".
* 2013-10-24 Bruce Schneier in The Atlantic: [https://www.theatlantic.com/technology/archive/2013/10/the-battle-for-power-on-the-internet/280824/ The Battle for Power on the Internet] — Mentions ”Internet feudalism” in comparison to ”Medieval feudalism”
* 2013-10-25 Glyn Moody: [https://www.techdirt.com/articles/20131025/08383625015/bruce-schneier-feudal-internet-how-to-fight-it.shtml Bruce Schneier On The Feudal Internet And How To Fight It]
* 2013-12-10 Bruce Schneier: [https://h2o.law.harvard.edu/text_blocks/2109 Power in the Age of the Feudal Internet]
* 2015-03-25 Paul Budde: [http://www.circleid.com/posts/20150325_consumers_are_the_serfs_of_the_feudal_internet_companies/ Consumers Are the Serfs of the Feudal Internet Companies]
* 2017-05-29 Michel Bauwens: [https://blog.p2pfoundation.net/fight-feudal-internet/2017/05/29 How to fight the feudal internet]
* 2017-11-25 Richard Chen: [https://medium.com/@rchen8/power-in-the-age-of-the-feudal-internet-20e106a2e2ce Power in the Age of the Feudal Internet]
* 2020-07-03 Neil Mather: [https://doubleloop.net/2020/07/03/resisting-the-feudal-internet/ Resisting the feudal Internet]
