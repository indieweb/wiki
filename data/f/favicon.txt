A '''<dfn>favicon</dfn>''' is a convention for websites to provide an [[icon]] for browsers to display in bookmarks, favorites, history, and other places they show or list URLs.

You can get favicons for most sites as PNGs using [[Google]]'s cache of them, e.g.:

* https://plus.google.com/_/favicon?domain=twitter.com&.png from <nowiki>https://plus.google.com/_/favicon?domain=twitter.com&.png</nowiki>
* https://plus.google.com/_/favicon?domain=facebook.com&.png from <nowiki>https://plus.google.com/_/favicon?domain=facebook.com&.png</nowiki>
* etc.
