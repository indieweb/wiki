'''<dfn>facebook permalink URL problem</dfn>''' is an issue that POSSE implementations may face, where it is difficult to guess a post permalink URL based on the ID that Facebook returns. The other direction is even more difficult (or impossible), IDs cannot be guessed based on a post URL.
