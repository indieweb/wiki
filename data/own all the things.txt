'''<dfn>Own all the things</dfn>''' is a memetic rallying cry for some of those who are part of the [[own your data]] and IndieWeb movements. It is a sub-category of the [[meme]] "[http://knowyourmeme.com/memes/all-the-things X All the Things]". Often it can be a strategy of social [[silos]] (and particularly their [https://chat.indieweb.org/2016-03-28/1459208323787000 legal departments]) to maintain an onerous and high level of ownership of the data input by their users.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* [http://dentedreality.com.au/ Beau Lebens]'s [[tagline]] for his [[WordPress]] based website is "Beau Lebens' All of the Things" where he primarily aggregates and thereby owns all of his social [[silo]] activity primarily via [[PESOS]] using his [https://wordpress.org/plugins/keyring-social-importers/ Keyring Social Importer plugin] ([https://github.com/beaulebens/keyring-social-importers/ dev version]).  See also: [https://beau.blog/2016/12/a-clean-slate/ 1] [http://dentedreality.com.au/2012/10/09/wordpress-is-your-digital-hub/ 2] [http://dentedreality.com.au/2012/10/07/where-is-your-digital-hub-home/ 3]
