=== What ===
Come enjoy breakfast and talk about the Indie Web! [[User:Aaronparecki.com|Aaron Parecki]] is organizing breakfast for folks in town for Open Source Bridge. Come to TILT around 7:30-8, order food or coffee at the counter, and join us at one of the large tables! Those of us attending Open Source Bridge will walk to the venue for the 9:00am keynote afterwards.
