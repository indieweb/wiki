'''<dfn>[https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Media/Guides/Formats/Image_types#avif_image AVIF]</dfn>''' is an alternative image format to [[GIF]] and is used on some community members' sites.

== Why ==
* Smaller file size:
** {{citation
| title = Switching my website images from WebP to AVIF
| url = https://stuffandnonsense.co.uk/blog/switching-my-website-images-from-webp-to-avif/
| author = [https://stuffandnonsense.co.uk/ Andy Clarke]
| published = 2026-07-12
| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20260712141547/https://stuffandnonsense.co.uk/blog/switching-my-website-images-from-webp-to-avif/
}}: <blockquote>The theoretical advantages of a format are one thing, but the results from my own website were more convincing. The first batch I converted came from my site’s general images folder. The WebP files totalled 6.58MB, whereas their AVIF replacements came to just 2.30MB. That reduced their combined size by 4.28MB—or 65.1%.</blockquote>
