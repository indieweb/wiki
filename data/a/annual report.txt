'''<dfn>Annual report</dfn>''' is a summary of an individual's activities throughout the year, often including statistics and charts, or narrative text.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Nicholas Felton ===
http://feltron.com/

Nicholas Felton published annual reports from 2005 to 2014, both on his personal website as well as in print.

=== Lillian Karabaic ===
* http://anomalily.net/the-year-2016/
* http://anomalily.net/birthday-statistics/
* http://anomalily.net/this-year-in-numbers-2014/

Lillian publishes annual reports (as well as [http://anomalily.net/category/monthly-expense-reports/ monthly]) as print zines, and puts a copy of the content on her personal website as well.

=== Aaron Parecki ===
* https://aaronparecki.com/2017/12/31/11/2017-in-numbers
* https://aaronparecki.com/2018/04/01/39/q1-review

Aaron publishes annual and quarterly reports on his website, but has not (yet) made a print version of them.

=== Jamie Tanna ===

{{jamietanna}} publishes reviews, an up to date list which can be found at https://www.jvt.me/series/year-in-review/

* https://www.jvt.me/posts/2018/02/14/2017-in-review/
* https://www.jvt.me/posts/2020/01/12/2018-in-review/
