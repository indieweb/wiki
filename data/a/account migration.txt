'''<dfn>account migration</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>account portability</dfn>, or the ability to <dfn>move your account</dfn>) is a specific kind of [[migration]] when a user switches from using one account on a particular server to using another account on another server, typically when both servers host multiple accounts, and has the common problem of [[losing followers]].

== Service Examples ==
Services that help with migrating an account from one server to another.

=== BridgyFed ===
[[BridgyFed]] now supports many combinations of account migration to/from web, [[Bluesky]], and [[fediverse]] services and identities. Read more: 2026-06-05 https://blog.anew.social/migrations-update-launch/

=== Bounce ===
[https://bounce.anew.social/ Bounce], [https://blog.anew.social/bounce-beta-now-live/ launched 2025-08-25]. Enables people migrate between Bluesky, Mastodon, and Pixelfed, while keeping all of their followers and many of their follows.

== Silo Examples ==
* [https://about.netflix.com/en/news/profile-transfer-keeps-netflix-experience-constant Announced in 2022], [[Netflix]] allows users on a Family plan (or otherwise shared account) to migrate their user history, preferences, and general data to a new account. See their docs for step-by-step instructions: [https://help.netflix.com/en/node/124844 How to transfer a Profile to a new account]
