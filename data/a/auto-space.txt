'''<dfn>auto-space</dfn>''' is space markup (like a br or nbsp) that was automatically added to some text to explicitly add markup linebreaks, line indenting, or sequential spacing.

== Implementations ==
=== CASSIS ===
Open source library [[CASSIS]] has an <code>auto_space</code> function (as of 2015-08-26 +0200) that implements this.
