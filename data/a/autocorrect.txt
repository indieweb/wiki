'''<dfn>autocorrect</dfn>''' is a feature of many text entry systems (especially [[mobile]]) that uses a built-in dictionary and simple grammar parser to fix errors while typing, yet often erroneously “corrects” [[jargon]] and proper nouns not in its dictionary, and is a source of errors in [[discuss|IndieWeb chat channels]] which can be found by [https://indiechat.search.cweiske.de/?q=autocorrect searching the chat for autocorrect].
