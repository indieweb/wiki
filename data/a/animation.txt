'''<dfn>animation</dfn>''' is the practice of making [[images]] appear to move. In a web and [[IndieWeb]] context, [[GIF]] and [[Flash]] are the most common formats, [[HTML]] can include animations large and small with [[CSS]] timelines and transitions. [[SVG]] can include [[JavaScript]] inline or externally to produce animation as well.
