'''<dfn>[http://indietech.rocks/ Indie Tech Rocks]</dfn>''' is a website that collects products "that don't screw us, the users".

The first version was created at Indie Web Summit in Portland, OR, June 2017.

It is hosted as a GitHub page, and accepts pull requests with new products that meet the criteria.
