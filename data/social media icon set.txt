'''<dfn>social media icon set</dfn>''' is a collection of icons for various services, typically [[silos]], that you can use on your [[homepage]] to link to your profiles on those sites.

== Examples ==
* https://paulrobertlloyd.com/2009/06/social_media_icons
** github: https://github.com/paulrobertlloyd/socialmediaicons
* [[OpenWeb Icons]]
* [[Genericons]]
* [[Simple Icons]]

=== Tantek ===
{{t}} on tantek.com uses Paul Robert Lloyd's social media icons on his homepage to link to his other profiles with [[rel=me]].
