'''<dfn>[https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Mozilla/Projects/Social_API Firefox Social API]</dfn>''' was a [[Firefox]] browser feature from [[Mozilla]] for interfacing with social APIs, on [[silos]], servers running open source installations like [[Known]] & [[WordPress]], and servers supporting open protocols like [[Firefox_Share#Micropub_share_provider|micropub]].

The last part of Social API, [[Firefox Share]], was removed in Firefox 57.
