'''<dfn>F5Bot</dfn>''' is https://f5bot.com/ a service for checking [[Reddit]], [[Hacker News]], and [[Lobsters]] for keywords and notifying by [[email]]s, which can be extracted for links to your personal site as a source of [[webmentions]].

== See Also ==

* IndieWeb Example: {{petermolnar}} is using F5Bot for [[backfeed]], with his own email hook to extract links to HTML pages, checking for any of his site domains, and sending them to [[webmention.io]].
