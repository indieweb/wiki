'''<dfn>[https://www.facebook.com/business/m/fan-subscriptions-onboarding Facebook Fan Subscriptions]</dfn>''' is a [[crowdfunding]] functionality within [[Facebook]] designed to help turn [[followers]] in to supporters.

Facebook began rolling out the feature in [https://www.recode.net/2018/3/19/17137446/facebook-video-subscription-creators-youtube-tip-jar 2018 March]. The feature lets creators offer $4.99 monthly subscriptions to their fans, which gets them access to exclusive content and an opt-in Supporter badge that appears next to the user’s comments on the Page.
