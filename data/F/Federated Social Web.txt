The phrase '''<dfn>Federated Social Web</dfn>''' is an early 2010s phrase that historically referred to several things, including [[federation]] with [[social standards]], the [[Federated Social Web Summit]] event(s), and 2010-era social web efforts. In particular:

* [[Federated Social Web Summit]] event (2010 Portland, 2011 Berlin, 2012 San Francisco)
* [http://www.w3.org/2005/Incubator/federatedsocialweb/wiki/Main_Page Federated Social Web IG] - W3C Interest Group formed after the 2010 Federated Social Web Summit.
* [http://www.w3.org/community/fedsocweb/ Federated Social Web CG] - W3C Community Group, established in early 2012 from the aforementioned Federated Social Web IG.
* 2010-era [[social standards]] / distributed social web standards with the goal of [[federation]]:
** [[OStatus]] ([[ActivityStreams]], [[PubSubHubbub]], [[Salmon Protocol]])
** [[PortableContacts]]
