'''<dfn>[https://simonrepp.com/faircamp/ Faircamp]</dfn>''' is an all-in-one [[SSG]] for producing a static music site hostable anywhere, with soft paywall functionality, and runs on Windows, macOS, or Linux.

An active [[webring]]  [https://webring.key13.uk/directory.html exists] for musicians with Faircamp sites.
