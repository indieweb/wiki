'''<dfn>[https://fastmail.com/ FastMail]</dfn>''' is a popular web-based paid email service launched in 1999. It was owned by Opera from 2010 to 2013, when the original developers bought the service back from Opera.[http://blog.fastmail.com/2013/09/25/exciting-news-fastmail-staff-purchase-the-business-from-opera/]
