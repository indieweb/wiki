'''<dfn>[http://ffffound.com/ FFFFound]</dfn>''' was an image collection [[silo]] used for [[bookmark|bookmarking]] and sharing images found elsewhere on the web.

FFFFound accounts have [[feeds]] of their posts, e.g.:
* http://ffffound.com/home/adactio/found/feed

Launched in June 2007 FFFFound created a community around the curation of art and other imagery. It was invite-only for its entire ten-year run with each user only receiving a single invite.

On 2017-05-12 founder Yugo Nakamura [https://twitter.com/yugop/status/852047586789806082 announced on twitter] that FFFFOUND would close on May 8, 2017.
