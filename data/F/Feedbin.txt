'''<dfn>[https://feedbin.com Feedbin]</dfn>''' is an open source [[feed reader]] written in [[Ruby]] that is also available as a paid, hosted service. 

Source:
* https://github.com/feedbin/feedbin

It is similar to [[Google Reader]] was, but offers a much improved and simplified interface compared to what Google Reader had.

== Issues ==
=== microformats2 support ===
Feedbin does not yet support subscribing to home pages marked up with [[h-feed]] and [[h-entry]] [[microformats]]. It has been requested:
* https://github.com/feedbin/feedbin/issues/132
** The Ruby and Node microformats parsers were tested and some problems occurred. The issue was closed on 2015-01-14

Please add your own opinion or help work on a pull request!
