'''<dfn>Facebook Master Algorithm</dfn>''' is the name given to [[Facebook]]'s algorithm for determining what content to show in Facebook's reader (AKA News Feed shown on their logged-in [[homepage]]), also adopted and tweaked by [[Instagram]] for their reader (app home screen).
