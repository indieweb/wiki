'''<dfn>[https://joinfirefish.org/ Firefish]</dfn>''' (formerly <dfn>Calckey</dfn>) is a fork of [[Misskey]], a microblogging  [[ActivityPub]] server implementation with an emphasis on features that people enjoy using. 

The new name was introduced with the 1.0 release in July 2023.
