== Features ==
=== Pingback ===
Quora appears to support sending pingbacks as of 2015-02-06. {{aaronpk}} received a pingback from Quora from a comment someone wrote that included a link to his post. The pingback source was the quora question URL with a fragment ID to the specific comment.

[[File:20150206-quora-pingback-status.png]]

== Criticism ==
<blockquote>Worth noting: Quora has no public [[API]], no backup/export tools, blocks anonymous display of answers, and banned the Wayback Machine entirely.
