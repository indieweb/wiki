'''<dfn>static maps</dfn>''' are a way to show a map as an image on a web page without requiring a javascript library.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} uses a PHP [https://github.com/esripdx/Static-Maps-API-PHP Static Maps library] to display static maps on his event posts as well as on walk/run/bike/drive posts when shown in the list view.
* http://aaronparecki.com/events/2015/07/15/1/homebrew-website-club
* http://aaronparecki.com/metrics?before=2015-07-25-194625

=== Jeena ===
{{jeena}} uses the the [http://staticmap.openstreetmap.de/ OSM static map service] to display static maps under pictures with geo data to show where they were taken. He also caches every picture to go easy on the free service.
* https://jeena.net/photos/161
