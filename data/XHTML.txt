'''<dfn>XHTML</dfn>''' is a markup standard (W3C) for [[HTML]] that has been superseded by HTML5.

If your use-cases require XML well-formedness, use XML-valid HTML5.[http://tantek.com/2010/302/b1/xhtml-dead-long-live-xml-valid-html5]
