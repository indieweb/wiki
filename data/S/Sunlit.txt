'''<dfn>[http://sunlit.io Sunlit]</dfn>''' is an iOS app to post photos to [[micro.blog]] and [[Micropub]] capable sites.

Sunlit 3.0 is open source and includes Swift code for IndieAuth, Micropub, and the WordPress XML-RPC API. The interface is designed around browsing the Micro.blog timeline, discovering new photos, and creating blog posts with photos and text. It can be signed in to both Micro.blog for the timeline, as well as an external blog for posting using Micropub.
