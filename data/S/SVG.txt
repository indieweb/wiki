'''<dfn>SVG</dfn>''' is Scalable Vector Graphics, a standard way to display vector images that integrates well into HTML and is implemented in all modern browsers. It is a great alternative to providing multiple bitmap images for different screen resolutions, and much better than using [[Flash]].
