'''<dfn>[https://searchmysite.net/ Search My Site]</dfn>''' provides simple [[search]] for personal and independent websites - to improve the [[discovery|ability to find]] independent and personal websites, and provide a search as a service for site owners.

The [[project]] is built and maintained by [[User:Michael-lewis.com|Michael Lewis]].


== Features ==
Provides the ability to: 
* Search independent websites
* Browse Sites
* see the Newest Pages
* visit a Random Page
* register your own personal site (including using [[IndieAuth]])
