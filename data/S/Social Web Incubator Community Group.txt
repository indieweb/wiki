The '''<dfn>[https://www.w3.org/wiki/SocialCG Social Web Incubator Community Group]</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn><abbr>SWICG</abbr></dfn>, <dfn>Social Web Community Group</dfn>, or <dfn><abbr>Social CG</abbr></dfn>) is a [[W3C]] Community Group established during the [[Social Web Working Group]] for broader community participation, and to maintain some of the specifications from the Social Web WG.

== How to ==
=== How to join ===
The Social Web Incubator Community Group, like all W3C community groups, is open to both W3C Member organization participation, and individuals.
* https://www.w3.org/community/socialcg/join

== Discussion ==

SWICG holds monthly discussions (potentially moving to bi-weekly meetings) and occasionally meetings on specific subjects. The Social CG uses the #social channel on the W3C IRC network (irc.w3.org) for chat communcation:

* https://chat.indieweb.org/social

As of [https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-swicg/2022Nov/0001.html November 2022], W3C opened up a mailing list for the SWICG, called public-swicg, with archives:

* https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-swicg/

You can join the CG (and thus get added to the mailing list), here:

* https://www.w3.org/community/socialcg/
