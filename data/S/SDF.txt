'''<dfn>SDF</dfn>''' is [https://sdf.org Super Dimension Fortress] (SDF), a free Unix shell. Amongst the many, many services offered by SDF are web hosting, git project hosting and a Mastodon server. Membership is free to all, and many more services can be enabled via a one-time donation. Others require an ongoing fee.

== IndieWeb examples ==

* {{mat.tl}} has been a longtime member of SDF. Trying in 2025 to use it more as an example of what can be done with SDF and how people may use it for personal projects.
