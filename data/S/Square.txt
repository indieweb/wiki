'''<dfn>[https://squareup.com/ Square]</dfn>''' is a company that offers financial services, including software and hardware to collect [[payments]] with mobile devices.

== Square Cash ==
[https://cash.me/ Square Cash] is a service for exchanging money using debit or credit card payments. Creates a simple-looking cash.me/$username URL to share for people to pay you. An amount can be pre-selected by adding it to the URL, e.g. https://cash.me/$username/10.
