'''<dfn>[https://www.slideshare.net/ Slideshare]</dfn>''' is a [[presentation]] hosting/sharing [[silo]], that was acquired by [[LinkedIn]], which is owned by [[Microsoft]]. [[Scribd]] purchased Slideshare from Microsoft and has run it since September 2020. The current iteration requires [[JavaScript]] just to navigate to next/previous slides and other site navigation. 

Spring 2026 Slideshare had a large rebuild to bring back functionality and fix a lot of bugs and cruft from years of inattention.

Slide decks can be uploaded, are converted to images and can then be viewed in the browser.

== Issues ==
=== js;dn ===
SlideShare heavily (and often unnecessarily) depends on Javascript, e.g. just to navigate to next/previous slides. Thus js;dn (Javascript Required; Didn't Navigate), related to but not quite as bad as [[js;dr]].
