'''<dfn>Steemit</dfn>''' is a blogging [[silo]] with gamification (badges and achievements to be unlocked) to encourage publishing [[posts]], [[reply]]ing, upvoting, and other interactions. Currently membership is not open, and the users have to go through an [https://steemit.com/steemit/@otisbrown/how-long-does-it-take-for-a-new-account-to-be-approved-to-get-on-steemit approval process].

See https://steemit.com/steemitboard/@steemitboard/steemitboard-the-steemit-gamification-experience for a description of their gamification and pictures of the badges, levels, etc.

There are also [http://altplatform.org/2017/07/24/the-problem-with-steem-and-all-altcoins/ skeptic views] on the Steemit business model.
