'''<dfn>Six Apart</dfn>''' is the software company that created [[Movable Type]], [[Vox]], and [[Typepad]], and was at one time the owner of [[Livejournal]] and [[Pownce]].

The current company named Six Apart is a spinoff that only maintains Movable Type; the core company became [[Say Media]], which was then acquired by TheMaven, which is now The Arena Group.
