'''<dfn>[https://statamic.com/ Statamic]</dfn>''' is a commercial [[flat-file]] [[CMS]] built on [[PHP]], with [https://docs.statamic.com/antlers Antlers] templating, and [[YAML]] + [[Markdown]] for storing articles and pages (YAML for metadata, Markdown for the content), and some IndieWeb [[building blocks]] support. 

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{vhbelvadi}} uses [[Statamic]] on [https://vhbelvadi.com VHBelvadi.com].
* {{straydogstrut}} uses [[Statamic]] on [https://straydogstrut.co.uk straydogstrut.co.uk].
