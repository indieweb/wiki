'''<dfn>[https://statamic.com/ Statamic]</dfn>''' is a commercial [[flat-file]] [[CMS]] built on [[PHP]], with [https://docs.statamic.com/antlers Antlers] templating, and [[YAML]] + [[Markdown]] for storing articles and pages (YAML for metadata, Markdown for the content), some IndieWeb [[building blocks]] support, but no known IndieWeb examples.
