'''<dfn>SNS</dfn>''' is an acronym used to refer to [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social%20networking%20service Social Networking Services].

[https://docs.aws.amazon.com/sns Amazon Simple Notification Service] ('''<dfn>Amazon SNS</dfn>''') is a web service that enables applications, end-users, and devices to instantly send and receive notifications from the cloud.
