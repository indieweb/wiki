'''<dfn>[http://www.eastgate.com/storyspace/ Storyspace]</dfn>''' is a tool made by [http://www.eastgate.com/ Eastgate] for complex, interlinked narrative, both fiction and nonfiction which predated and influenced the ideas behind the [[wiki]].

The underlying user interface is an interesting one which has some potential for design of websites and digital [[commonplace book]]s for idea generation.
