'''<dfn>[https://wordpress.org/themes/sempress/ SemPress]</dfn>''' is a highly semantic [[WordPress]] Theme with HTML5 templates, responsive and seo optimized. SemPress supports most of the new HTML5 tags, the new HTML5 input-types, [[microformats]], microformats v2, and microdata (Schema.org).

It is one of the most indie web friendly themes in the WordPress repository.

== Development ==
The development branch of SemPress is available on [https://github.com/pfefferle/SemPress GitHub].
