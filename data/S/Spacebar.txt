'''<dfn>[https://spacebar.chat Spacebar]</dfn>''' (formerly <dfn>Fosscord</dfn>) is a free and open source, full stack reverse engineering and reimplementation of [[Discord]], and some indieweb community members have experience with it.

== Why ==
It's a communication platform you're already familiar with.

In Spacebar you can use existing Discord bots and bridges
