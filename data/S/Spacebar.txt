'''<dfn>[https://spacebar.chat Spacebar]</dfn>''' (formerly <dfn>Fosscord</dfn>) is a free and open source, full stack reverse engineering and reimplementation of the [[Discord]] server, and some indieweb community members have experience with it.

== Why ==
It's a communication platform you're already familiar with.

In Spacebar you can use existing Discord bots and bridges

== Spacebar Clients ==

To connect to a Spacebar server you need a Spacebar client. 

=== Official Spacebar WIP client ===

* [https://github.com/spacebarchat/client Official Spacebar WIP client]

=== Fermi ===

* [https://github.com/MathMan05/Fermi Fermi] is a full-featured Spacebar client created by Mathium05 as a replacement of the official WIP client.
