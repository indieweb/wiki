'''<dfn>[https://ricks-apps.com/osx/sitesucker/index.html SiteSucker]</dfn>''' is a Macintosh application that downloads websites from the Internet and can be used to [[archive]] [[silo|siloed]] pages to one's own website. There is both a free and a paid version of the tool.
