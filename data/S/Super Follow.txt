'''<dfn>Super Follow</dfn>''' is an experimental [[Twitter]] feature for a form of paid subscriptions and special badge applied to your icon to indicate that you are a super follower.

== Silo Examples ==
Only Twitter for now, though Instagram is [https://techcrunch.com/2021/06/30/instagram-is-developing-its-own-version-of-twitters-super-follow-with-exclusive-stories/ developing something similar].
