'''<dfn>[https://dot.net .NET]</dfn>''' is a free and open-source, managed computer software framework created and maintained by [[Microsoft]] for Windows, Linux, and macOS operating systems, and used by at least one IndieWeb site.

.NET can be used for building:

* [https://dot.net/web Web apps]
* [https://dotnet.microsoft.com/apps/desktop Desktop] & [https://dotnet.microsoft.com/apps/maui mobile] apps
* [https://dotnet.microsoft.com/apps/games Games]
* [https://dotnet.microsoft.com/apps/iot Internet of Things (IoT)]
* [https://dot.net/ml Machine learning applications]
