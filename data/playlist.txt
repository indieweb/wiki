http://indiewebcamp.com/playlist

A playlist represents a collection of songs of some significance to the list's creator. These could then be syndicated to places like Spotify and Google Play All Access, as they tend to share a lot, if not most, of the same content.

The playlist differs from a Jam or Scrobble in that it acts as a sort of bookmark for a collection of songs. As opposed to Jams and Srobbles which a passive types.
