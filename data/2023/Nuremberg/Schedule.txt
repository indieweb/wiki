The session grid is developed on day 1, and is based on suggestions by the attendees.

All times are '''local''' times. If you are a remote attendee, make sure to [https://events.indieweb.org/local-time?date=20231028T1000&tz=Europe%2FBerlin convert to your timezone]. Be careful about Daylight Savings Time! Europe switches over between Saturday and Sunday. So [https://events.indieweb.org/local-time?date=20231029T1000&tz=Europe%2FBerlin start time on Sunday] might be different for you than Saturday.
