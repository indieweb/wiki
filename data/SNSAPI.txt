'''<dfn>SNSAPI</dfn>''' is a lightweight cross platform middleware [[project]] for Social Networking Services.

* GitHub: https://github.com/hupili/snsapi
* PyPI: https://pypi.python.org/pypi/snsapi/
* Maintainer: [[User:Hupili.net]]

== Summary ==
SNSAPI unifies the data structures and interfaces of different SNS so that developer users can programmatically operate those channels.

SNSAPI can be a building block of all three [[syndication-models]] termed on IndieWeb.
