<dfn>silo.pub</dfn> is a [[Micropub]] endpoint for hosted blogs like [[Tumblr]], [[WordPress.com]], [[Blogger]], and [[Twitter]]. It aims to implement the ideas laid out in the post [https://snarfed.org/micropub-for-hosted-blogs Micropub for Hosted Blogs].
