'''<dfn>joy</dfn>''' is a good motivator and focus for making things for your own website, and is something the IndieWeb community encourages people to prioritize and pursue with their IndieWeb [[projects]], and deprioritize (or [[manual until it hurts|automate]]) things that do not bring them joy.
