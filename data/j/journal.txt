<span class="p-summary">'''"journal"''' and '''"journaling"''' are words with multiple shades of [[IndieWeb]] meaning related to [[writing]], both public and private, [[blogging]] and [[Zettelk√§sten|note-taking]].</span>

* the idea of [[private posts]] in particular map to [[writing]] related to introspection and research
* some [[blogging|bloggers]] refer to their [[blog|blogs]] as "journals" ''(See journaling [[silo|silos]] [[LiveJournal]], [https://www.deadjournal.com DeadJournal])''
* "[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_journal Journal]" may refer to academic publishing by scholars and associated with educational institutions. Some journals are predatory in pricing causing some to resort to [[academic samizdat]]. [[open access|Open access]] is a model for academic publishing to make more data open and available.
* journal is the root word of "journalism," see ''[[Indieweb for Journalism]]''
