'''<dfn>Meta Platforms</dfn>''' (or <dfn>Meta</dfn> for short) is a corporation formerly named Facebook, that runs several [[silo]] services ([[Facebook]], [[Instagram]], [[Threads]], that IndieWeb folks have [[POSSE]]d to), a couple of [[messaging]] services ([[Facebook Messenger]], [[WhatsApp]]), and hosts [[Data Dialogues]] in-person events that invite and include outside experts including IndieWeb community participants.

== Services ==
Services and [[app]]s:

Content posting and hosting:
* [[Facebook]]
* [[Instagram]]
* [[Threads]]

Messaging:
* [[Facebook Messenger]]
* [[WhatsApp]]

== Events ==
* [[Data Dialogues]]
