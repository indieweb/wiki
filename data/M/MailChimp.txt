'''<dfn>[https://mailchimp.com MailChimp]</dfn>''' is an email sending service that some IndieWeb sites use to automatically send content to subscribers from a [[feed file]].

MailChimp is known for marketing services like email and SMS campaigns and provides website and form building features. It acquired [[TinyLetter]] in 2011 before shutting it down in 2024. It was acquired itself by [[Intuit]] in 2021.

== Features ==
=== Free tier ===
See their [https://mailchimp.com/pricing/marketing/ pricing page] for the latest limits.
* Up to 1,000 emails per month
* Forms and landing pages
* Integrations with external services
* [[RSS]]-to-email

== How ==

=== RSS-to-email ===

The [https://mailchimp.com/features/rss-to-email/ RSS-to-email feature] allows people without a [[reader]] to automatically get updates from your site in their email. See their [https://mailchimp.com/features/rss-to-email/ official help page] for instructions.
