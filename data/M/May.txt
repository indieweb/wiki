'''<dfn>May</dfn>''' is the 5th month of the Gregorian calendar year, when there is usually an IndieWebCamp [[Düsseldorf]] typically right before [[Beyond Tellerrand]].

== Events ==
Typical events in November:
* [[Beyond Tellerrand]] Düsseldorf
* IndieWebCamp [[Düsseldorf]]
** See: [[Planning#Düsseldorf]] to express your interest and help organize!
* [[Global Accessibility Awareness Day]] annually on third Thursday of the month
