An '''<dfn>MX</dfn>''' record is a [[DNS]] record that tells other servers where to send email to for the domain.

For example:
<pre>example.com.    1800    MX    mail.example.com.   10</pre>

Tells all mail servers that incoming mail for example.com is handled by the server mail.example.com.
