'''<dfn>[https://miraheze.org Miraheze]</dfn>''' is an [[advertising|ad]]-free non-profit [[wiki]] farm that operates on [[business-model|donations]]<ref>[https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/FAQ Miraheze FAQ]</ref> and allows you to use your own [[domain]].<ref>[https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/Custom_domains Miraheze Custom Domains]</ref> It allows users to [[export]] wiki [[data_portability|data]]<ref>[https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/Backups Miraheze Backups]</ref> and as well as [[import]] their wikis from other wiki farms, such as [[Wikia]].<ref>[https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/Moving_a_wiki_to_Miraheze Moving A Wiki To Miraheze]</ref> Miraheze is volunteer-run and was originally founded in 2015.<ref>[https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/Miraheze_history Miraheze History]</ref>
