'''<dfn>[https://mathober.com/ Mathober]</dfn>''' is an annual [[blogging challenge]] for the month of [[October]] with a theme of being a space for everyone to explore and express math creatively on their blogs. Use any medium—art, code, poetry, and more—to engage with the prompts in your own way. No rules, just fun and discovery. It takes place every October.
