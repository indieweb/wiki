'''<dfn>[https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/Spoke Mozilla Spoke]</dfn>''' is an [[open source]] project for an editor to create 3D scenes you can navigate in a web browser and was used by at least one IndieWeb community member to build and show a 3D version of their site.
