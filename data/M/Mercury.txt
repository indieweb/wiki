    '''<dfn>[https://mercury.postlight.com/ Mercury]</dfn>''' is a suite of reading related tools featuring an API that takes any web article and returns only the relevant content — headline, author, body text, relevant images and more — free from clutter and advertising. 

=== Web Parser ===
Make semantic sense out of any web article. Mercury Parser is a single API endpoint that takes a URL and gives you back the content reliably and easily. It took over the functionality of the [[Readability]] API when Readability shut down in late 2016.

=== AMP Converter ===
With just one line of code, Mercury AMP prepares your publication for Google [[AMP]]. Get lightning-fast load times and boosted search results on mobile.

=== Mercury Reader ===
Turn any article into a clean and readable page with one click using the [https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/mercury-reader/oknpjjbmpnndlpmnhmekjpocelpnlfdi?hl=en Chrome browser extension].

=== Open Source ===
Much of [https://postlight.com/trackchanges/mercury-goes-open-source the Mercury suite was open sourced by Postlight in February 2019].
