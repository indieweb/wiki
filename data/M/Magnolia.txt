'''<dfn>Magnolia</dfn>''' was a [[bookmark]] service that shut down in 2010. [http://web.archive.org/web/20101022063119/http://gnolia.com:80/pages/closing] One of the competitors to the, at the time,  dominant [[Delicious]]. 

== History ==

In 2009 the site had a complete outage and lost all user data. [https://web.archive.org/web/20140101162023/http://www.wired.com/business/2009/01/magnolia-suffer/]

{{adactio}} [https://adactio.com/journal/1552 blogged about the data loss] at the time and how that sparked him to start on a journey to avoid such a losses from happen to him in the future.
