'''<dfn>Marauders Map</dfn>''' was a Chrome browser extension that showed the real-time location of your [[Facebook]] Messenger friends; similar IndieWeb functionality can be found in the social reader [[Together]]’s map view of your followings. https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2015/may/28/marauders-map-chrome-app-tracks-facebook-messenger.
