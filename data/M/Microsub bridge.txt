'''<dfn>[https://snarfed.org/microsub-bridge Microsub bridge]</dfn>''' is a design doc for a [[Microsub]] server that bridges to other [[reader]] backends. {{snarfed}} wrote it in Feb 2018, then [https://snarfed.org/2018-07-16_introducing-baffle implemented and launched it in July 2018] as [[Baffle]].

{{sknebel}} also has a prototype working end to end with [[Inoreader]] and [[Monocle]].
