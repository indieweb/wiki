A '''<dfn>Manual of Me</dfn>''' is a page for communicating your working preferences, motivations and needs, so others can better understand how to work with you.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{jamietanna}} has his own version at [https://manual.jvt.me manual.jvt.me]

== Silo Examples ==
* [https://my.manualof.me/ Manual of Me] is a free hosted version, which provides a set of [https://my.manualof.me/questions questions] and a page that can be shared with others.
