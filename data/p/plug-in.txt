A '''<dfn>plug-in</dfn>''' / <dfn>plugin</dfn> or <dfn>add-on</dfn> is software that a user installs as an addition to their existing software that adds or modifies its features; the IndieWeb has plugins for making [[WordPress]] and [[Drupal]] more indieweb [[friendly]].
