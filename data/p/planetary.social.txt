'''<dfn>[https://planetary.social/ planetary.social]</dfn>''' is a [[Scuttlebutt]]-based distributed social network, privacy-focused, ad-free, and secure.

It is being built by a team that includes [https://twitter.com/rabble/status/1220078709589213184 @rabble], who previously worked on Odeo and [[Twitter]], and [https://twitter.com/tomcoates @tomcoates].
