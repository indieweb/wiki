'''<dfn>[https://github.com/bellebethcooper/pico Pico]</dfn>''' is an [[Android]] client for [[Micro.blog]] with support for [[Micropub]].

Development for Pico was done on [https://github.com/bellebethcooper/pico GitHub].

The app allowed one to 
* Create a new post including text and/or an image
* View your timeline
* Open the conversation view for a single post
* Reply to a post from the timeline

[http://bellebcooper.com Belle B. Cooper] [http://blog.bellebcooper.com/pico.html announced the project] on 2018-08-31. 

{{manton}} [https://chat.indieweb.org/meta/2020-09-21#t1600717797010200 has indicated that] it "Probably wouldn’t be a big stretch for an Android developer to update Pico since it’s already using Micropub, just currently hardcoded to Micro.blog’s endpoint."
