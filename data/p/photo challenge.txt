'''<dfn>photo challenge</dfn>''' is a particular kind of [[challenge]] focused on taking and/or posting a [[photo]] for some number of days like a week, 30 days (each day of a month), 365 days (each day of a year), or continuously.

== Service Examples ==
=== microblog ===
[[micro.blog]] frequently hosts photo challenges at https://challenges.micro.blog/:
* 2017-11-09 [http://micro.douglane.com/2017/11/09/microblog-photo-challenge.html Micro.blog Photo Challenge] (see also: https://www.manton.org/2017/11/5991.html)
* 2023-02-26: [https://challenges.micro.blog/2023/02/26/the-may-photoblogging.html The March 2023 Photoblogging Challenge Prompts]
* 2023-08-30: [https://challenges.micro.blog/2023/08/30/september-photoblogging-challenge.html September 2023 Photoblogging Challenge: FAQs]
* 2024-03-27: [https://challenges.micro.blog/2024/03/27/april-photoblogging-challenge.html April 2024 Photoblogging Challenge: FAQs]
* 2025 winter wonderland: https://challenges.micro.blog/2025/12/12/days-of-winter-wonder-photo.html
