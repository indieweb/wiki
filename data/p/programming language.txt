'''<dfn>Programming languages</dfn>''' are [[language]]s designed for humans to write for machines to interpret and execute, typically in the form of software, though sometimes in terminal commands.

Examples:
* [[JavaScript]]
* [[PHP]]
* [[Python]]
* [[Ruby]]
* [[Go]]
* [[Haskell]]
* [[Elixir]]
