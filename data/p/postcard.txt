A '''<dfn>postcard</dfn>''' is [[print|printed]] card with [[writing]] and/or [[images]] suitable for [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mail physical mailing].

== Brainstorming ==
* In 2026, {{artlung}} expressed an intent to create a card suitable for passing out at in-person [[IndieWebCamp|camps]] or other [[events]] ''(such as the upcoming  [https://www.comic-con.org San Diego Comic Con], [[HTML Day]])''.
** Card will be mailable, legible
** Should communicate the [[principles]] of the [[IndieWeb]]
** Open [[license]], translatable and localizable
** Suitable for readers with a wide range of reading levels and backgrounds
** includes a bit of [[HTML]] and [[CSS]] code and communicates [[fun]]
** uses IndieWeb [[graphics]], adhere's to [[CoC]] and does not misrepresent Indieweb.org
