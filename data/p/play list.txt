'''<dfn>play list</dfn>''' is a post containing a list of songs (or more broadly, any audio and/or video) meant to be played in a particular order; for an indieweb example see http://gregorlove.com/2007/11/happy-living-day-mix/

A <dfn>mix tape</dfn> is a recording of a play list onto a specific medium. Play lists sometimes link to online mix tapes, ibid.
