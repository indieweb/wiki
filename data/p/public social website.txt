A '''<dfn>public social website</dfn>''' is a <em>public website</em> (at a URL you can curl), that has <em>social</em> content like [[posts]] which have obvious visible [[authorship]] (even if pseudonymous), and mentions other people or other social web content, via URL reference, not just name.
