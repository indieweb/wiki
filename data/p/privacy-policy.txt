'''<dfn>privacy-policy</dfn>''' is a [[rel]] value for a link to the [[privacy policy]] of a site.

== How to ==
Add <code>rel="privacy-policy"</code> to any link from your site to your privacy policy.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== David Shanske ===
{{GWG}} has used <code>rel=privacy-policy</code> in the footer of every page of his site since 2022-08-06.

=== capjamesg ===

{{capjamesg}} has used the privacy-policy rel attribute in a link to his privacy page since 2022-08-07.

=== Ana Rodrigues ===
{{ana-r}} started using the <code>rel=privacy-policy</code> in the footer since 2024-03-10
