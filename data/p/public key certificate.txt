A '''<dfn>public key certificate</dfn>''' (often just <dfn>certificate</dfn> or <dfn>cert</dfn>) is a file used to prove the ownership of a public key, typically as part of [[Transport Layer Security]] for [[HTTPS]] for your indieweb site, that you must explicitly [[HTTPS#Obtain|obtain and manage]], or have your [[webhost]] do so for you.
