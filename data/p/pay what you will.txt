'''<dfn>Pay what you will</dfn>''' is [[business-model]] based on a donation button or other [[UX]] that allows and encourages the user of a service to pay what they think the service is worth.

== Examples ==
https://lightward.com/pricing

=== Flickstagram ===
flickstagram.org was a service which imports your Instagram photos to your Flickr account. People have paid anywhere from $1 to $10 after using this service.
