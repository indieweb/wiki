'''<dfn>performance</dfn>''' is a [[post type]] that indicates a performance of a musical piece, such as performing a song at karaoke.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} imported his karaoke performance history from Voicebox to his website. The performance posts indicate which song was sung and when.
* List of all performance posts
** https://aaronparecki.com/karaoke
* Posts in the context of the rest of the posts on a day
** https://aaronparecki.com/2019/06/30#post-id-80940 (IndieWebCamp 2019)
