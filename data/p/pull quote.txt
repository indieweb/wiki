'''<dfn>pull quote</dfn>''' is a short [[quotation]] from the article displayed in larger type to attract the eye of someone skimming the page.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== gRegor Morrill ===
{{gRegor}} has CSS styles for pull quotes and has used it a few times, as early as 2013-05-03:
* https://gregorlove.com/2013/05/the-long-web-haul/

However, my usage so far has been to highlight a quote or a song lyric that is ''not'' directly in the post text, but from a video or article, so they may not strictly be considered pull quotes. I really like the visual style of a pull quote, though am uncertain how often I would use them nowadays.
