'''<dfn>[http://www.google.com/picasa Picasa]</dfn>''' is software for organizing and sharing [[photo]]s. Google announced on 2016-02-12 that it will be retired in favor of [[Google Photos]]. The desktop software will no longer be maintained after 2016-03-15 and changes to the web albums will begin 2016-05-01. [http://googlephotos.blogspot.com/2016/02/moving-on-from-picasa.html]
