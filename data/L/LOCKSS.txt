'''<dfn>LOCKSS</dfn>''' is an acronym for <dfn>'''L'''ots '''O'''f '''C'''opies '''K'''eeps '''S'''tuff '''S'''afe</dfn>, a [http://www.lockss.org project] and collection of [https://www.lockss.org/community/networks/ networks for libraries] to keep copies of stuff on the web, but often used as a generic mnemonic for the technique of keeping and encouraging many copies of things as a way of improving their [[longevity]].
