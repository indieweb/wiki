'''<dfn>London</dfn>''' is a city in the [[UK]] that has been holding regular [[Homebrew Website Club]] meetups since [[events/2016-08-24-homebrew-website-club#London|2016]]. 

== HWC London / Europe == 

HWC London / Europe is a [[Homebrew Website Club]] hosted online every second Wednesday at 7pm UK time. The event was started by {{calumryan}} and {{barryfrost}} and is currently organised by {{marksuth}} and {{capjamesg}}.
