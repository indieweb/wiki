'''<dfn>[https://developer.twitter.com/en/docs/authentication/guides/log-in-with-twitter Log in with Twitter]</dfn>''' is (AKA <dfn>Sign in with Twitter</dfn>) [[Twitter]]'s [[silo sign-in]] service. 

== How not to ==
You should use and support [[IndieAuth]] instead, or if you want to provide fallback for Twitter profiles, support [[RelMeAuth]] and Twitter log-in as a means for that.
