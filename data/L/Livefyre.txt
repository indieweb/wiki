'''<dfn>[http://livefyre.com Livefyre]</dfn>''' is an Enterprise Social Web Platform used by hundreds of Publishers and Brands for commenting, chat, reviews, and social content marketing.

Products (Social APIs + Social Web Components) include:
* Real-time Commenting and Chat
* Ratings and Reviews
* User/Topic Following and Aggregation
* Content Collection Hosting and tools for curating social Content from SNS like twitter.com, instagram.com, facebook.com, and many more
* A JavaScript SDK for building custom social web components
* An [http://api.livefyre.com/app-service/v4/ App Web Service] that models application lifecycle management for social web components
* User Management and Social Authentication
* https://storify.com/

For more answers, visit [http://answers.livefyre.com/ Livefyre Answer Central].
