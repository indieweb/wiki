'''<dfn>[https://twitter.com/hashtag/letsfixthis?f=tweets&vertical=default LetsFixThis]</dfn>''' is a hashtag (<dfn>#LetsFixThis</dfn>) proposed by Jeffrey Zeldman at the end of his 2019-04-11 A List Apart article [https://alistapart.com/article/nothing-fails-like-success/ Nothing Fails Like Success] as a rallying point to share thoughts about the IndieWeb as an approach to solving the problems of “the internet, 2019” that has garnered some notable responses.

* 2019-04-30 Wences García: [https://www.marketgoo.com/blog/2019/04/30/why-support-indieweb/ Why I’m supporting the IndieWeb (and you should too)]
