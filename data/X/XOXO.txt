'''<dfn>XOXO</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>XOXOfest</dfn>) was an annual festival and conference in Portland, Oregon that ran from 2012-2016 2018-2019, with the final event [https://2024.xoxofest.com August 22-24, 2024], and has held many IndieWeb-related events and meetups.

Hashtag: <kbd>#xoxofest</kbd>

See each year for IndieWeb related sessions that year.
