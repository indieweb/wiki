'''The XMPP Software Foundation (<dfn>XSF</dfn>)''' is an organization with the primary mission of creating and maintaining the standards required to implement software that uses the XMPP protocols.

The XSF is an independent nonprofit with the following mission:
* define open protocols for presence and instant messaging -- [https://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc6122.txt IETF RFC6121 - Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP): Instant Messaging and Presence]
* define open protocols for realtime communication and collaboration -- [https://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc6120.txt IETF RFC6120 - Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP): Core]
* thru it's membership define new extensions to XMPP and ensure interoperability between Client and Server implementations
