'''<dfn>XFN</dfn>''' ('''XHTML Friends Network''') is the network of visible links across blogs that claim various XFN relationships with/to each other. The name is a tongue-in-cheek self-deprecating parody of the "Psychic Friends Network".

See also:
* http://gmpg.org/xfn/
* http://microformats.org/wiki/xfn
