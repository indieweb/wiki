= How to Contribute =

Here are a couple of ways to participate in IndieWebCamp, before, during, after, and even if you can't make it:

# join/hangout on the [[IRC]] channel: irc://irc.freenode.net/indiewebcamp
# post links on your IndieWebCamp.com user profile page to the open source projects which you're contributing to and using as part of your own domain/identity on the web.
# help out with the [[2014]] IndieWebCamp!
