The '''<dfn>[[2019/NYC|IndieWebCamp NYC 2019]] session grid</dfn>''' is where notes and videos from all sessions can be found.

The session grid is developed on day 1, and is based on suggestions by the attendees.

All times are <strong>local</strong> times. If you are a remote attendee, make sure to [https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html?iso=20191005T150000&p1=179 convert to your timezone].

== Saturday ==
Day 1: Share, Learn, and Discuss!

'''163 William Street, 2nd Floor, New York, NY 10038'''

Session topics will be proposed and chosen on the day of the event and organized into a grid.

Sessions are facilitated by the participant who proposed the topic. Learn more at [[session facilitating]].

== Sunday ==
Day 2: Create day. Build stuff and demo it!

'''One Pace Plaza, 3rd Floor'''
<br>(''Enter using the 3 Spruce Street / Michael Schimmel Center for The Arts entrance'')

* 08:30 - Doors Open
* 09:00 - Welcome and Kickoff
** 9:00-12:00 CIVIC E314 (Capacity: 16) remote or group hacking
** 9:00-12:00 CIVIC E315 (Capacity: 30) quiet/silent hacking room
* 12:00 Lunch
* 13:00 Creating Continued
* 16:30 [[2019/NYC/Demos|Demos]]
* 15:15 Clean Up
* 15:30 Camp Closes
