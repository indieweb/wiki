== Travel and Lodging ==
Local travel by 🚃 rail, 🚗 car, ⛵️ boat, 🚲 bicycle:
* '''[http://68middle.st/contact 68 Middle St address, map, and 🚃🚗⛵️ directions]'''

=== Airport ===
* ✈️ '''LGW Airport.''' London Gatwick tends to have more European connections and is the better airport to fly into as it has a quick direct train connection to Brighton.
* ✈️ '''LHR Airport.''' London Heathrow has more international connections and may be more convenient to fly into from overseas.

=== Trains ===
* 🚃 '''From London St. Pancras International''': If you are arriving via Eurostar, the easiest and most direct way of getting to Brighton is via Thameslink trains, which depart regularly from Platform A. It costs £10-20 for a ticket. [https://www.nationalrail.co.uk/ National Rail] has all the timetables and costs. Advance tickets are cheaper, but require you to take a specific train.

* 🚃 '''From LGW airport''': Go to the South Terminal which is connected to the train station where you can buy a ticket to Brighton. The train will drop you off in downtown Brighton about a 15 minute walk from the venue. It costs £10-20 for a ticket. [https://www.nationalrail.co.uk/ National Rail] has all the timetables and costs. Advance tickets are cheaper, but require you to take a specific train.

* 🚄🚈🚃 '''From LHR airport''': There are a couple of options
** 🚄 [https://www.heathrowexpress.com Heathrow Express] to London Paddington Station (fastest ~15-20min, has wifi, pricier), OR
** 🚃 [https://tfl.gov.uk/modes/tfl-rail/ TfL Rail] to London Paddington Station (fast ~30min), OR
** 🚈 London Underground [https://tfl.gov.uk/tube/route/piccadilly/ Piccadilly line] to London Hammersmith (slower ~80-90min, cheaper)
** Then:
** 🚈 London Underground to Victoria, THEN
** 🚃 Train from Victoria train station to Brighton.

For more information about routes etc.:
* 🚃 [https://traintimes.org.uk/ Accessible UK Train Timetables]
* 🚈 [https://tfl.gov.uk/plan-a-journey/ Transport For London (TFL): Plan A Journey] for London Underground status and trip planning

=== Taxi ===
🚕 There are local cabs in Brighton, which typically only take cash (£).

=== Cycling ===
🚲 There are three National Cycle Routes within less than 1km of 68 Middle St. Bicycle parking is located inside the venue. [https://www.cyclestreets.net/journey/ Plan your cycle route] on CycleStreets.

* Route 2 follows adjacent to the seafront from locations West/East
* Route 20 follows London Road and Preston Road from the North 
* Route 90 follows Lewes Road from the North East
