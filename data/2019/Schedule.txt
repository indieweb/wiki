The '''<dfn>[[2019|IndieWeb Summit 2019]] schedule and session grid</dfn>''' is where notes and videos from all sessions can be found.

The session grid is developed on day 1, and is based on suggestions by the attendees.

All times are <strong>local</strong> times. If you are a remote attendee, make sure to [https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html?iso=20190629T140000&p1=419 convert to your timezone].
