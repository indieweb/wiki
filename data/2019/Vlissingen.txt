<div class="e-content">
The importance of owning your data is getting more awareness.
<br>
Apart from an ordinary IndieWebCamp we want to create sustainable FLOSS communities and raise awareness in politics and media.
Let's create awesome OpenSource stuff around the federated- and indieweb. 
We will tinker in a water tower, the base for the event.

Join us for '''five days''' of brainstorming, working, helping.
At least 24 spots are available for now incl. 8 cost-free sleeping rooms.
