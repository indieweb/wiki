'''<dfn>451</dfn>''' is the proposed HTTP status code for legal reasons preventing access of a web resource. It refers to Ray Bradbury's dystopian novel Fahrenheit 451 featuring a society that has banned, and burns, books. See also [https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc7725 RFC 7725].
