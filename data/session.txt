A '''<dfn>session</dfn>''' is a type of [[post]] that is an [[event]] likely with a [[presentation]] and speakers. Sessions are also often part of larger events (like conferences) that are typically a whole day or span several days.

== IndieWeb Implementations ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
Aaron Parecki has been posting sessions for quite some time here:
* http://aaronparecki.com/presentations

Example:
* http://aaronparecki.com/presentations/2011/05/27/1/ambient-location-and-the-future-of-the-interface

=== Lanyrd ===
[[Lanyrd]] has pages for both conferences and sessions in conferences.

Example:
* http://lanyrd.com/2013/eia2013/sckykt/ is a session at [http://lanyrd.com/2013/eia2013/ a conference].
