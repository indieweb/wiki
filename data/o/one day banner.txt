'''<dfn>one day banner</dfn>''' is a feature on some sites where they show a banner at or near the top of the page as a call to action for something for that day in particular, like a protest or an election.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Tantek ===
{{t}} on tantek.com for 2016-313 US Election day.
* http://tantek.com/2016/313/t3/new-site-feature-one-day-banner-electionday
* 2017-07-12 for the [[Battle for the Net]] campaign.
