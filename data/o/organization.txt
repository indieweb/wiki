'''<dfn>organization</dfn>''' is a special [[account]] that is a [[group]] of individual members with their own accounts who can join, leave, and often have different abilities to alter the organization and its membership, supported by some [[silo]]s like [[GitHub]].

== GitHub ==
* GitHub supports organization accounts with varying permission levels for individual members https://help.github.com/articles/permission-levels-for-an-organization/ and allows for transferring [[repo]]s from one organization to another https://help.github.com/articles/transferring-a-repository-owned-by-your-organization/ .
