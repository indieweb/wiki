'''<dfn>otp</dfn>''' (or OTP) is an acronym for <dfn>on the phone</dfn>, sometimes appended to an [[IRC]] nickname to indicate that their attention is currently divided (preoccupied) with a [[phone]] call and they might not respond to queries; OTP can also mean <dfn>[[one time password]]</dfn>, sometimes used with [[IndieAuth]].
