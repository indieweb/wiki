'''<dfn>#osfw3c</dfn>''' is a hashtag for the [[2013/osfw3c|W3C Workshop on Social Standards: The Future of Business]] that took place 2013-08-07..08 in San Francisco, CA, USA, which led directly to the creation of the [[socialwg|W3C Social Web Working Group]] where several IndieWeb specifications were standardized.

* [[2013/osfw3c]] - wiki page archive of the [https://etherpad.mozilla.org/osfw3c #osfw3c Etherpad], links to IRC archives of the days of the event, and posts about.
