'''<dfn>own your replies</dfn>''' is the IndieWeb approach of posting your [[reply]] posts on your own site first and using [[Webmention]] to automatically send them to show up as [[comments]] on the original post, and/or automatically having your site [[POSSE]] them to silo (copies of) posts.
