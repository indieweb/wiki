'''<dfn>[https://orgmode.org Org mode]</dfn>''' is for keeping notes, maintaining [[todo]] lists, planning projects, and authoring documents with a fast and effective plain-text system. Primarily used and designed for use in the emacs editor, Org mode extensions are available for other editors and Operating Systems.
