'''<dfn>Big Boring System</dfn>''' is an open source text-only social network [[project]] that uses phone numbers for both sign-up and sign-in, and was also a site running it by the same name at bigboringsystem.com from 2013-2015.

* Open source: https://github.com/bigboringsystem/bigboringsystem
