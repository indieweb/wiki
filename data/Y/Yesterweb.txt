'''<dfn>[https://yesterweb.org/ Yesterweb]</dfn>''' is an archived website and was a community ([[Discord]], [[Mastodon]], [[Hubs]], [[Gemini]], [[Minecraft]] instances) that “focused on nurturing creativity and diversity in web pages and digital social infrastructure”, with a number of [https://yesterweb.org/manifesto member contributed manifestos], some of which referenced IndieWeb.

== Shutdown ==
* 2024-04-05 [https://forum.yesterweb.org/viewtopic.php?t=392 The forum is shutting down]
* shutdown of community forum(s) per https://suboptimalism.neocities.org/writings/yesterweb (via https://toot.garden/@moonview/110667602359898976) and https://forum.yesterweb.org/viewtopic.php?t=392 and https://yesterweb.org/#summary

== Culture ==
* [https://yesterweb.org/about/ What role does nostalgia play in the Yesterweb?]
<blockquote cite="https://yesterweb.org/about/">It's fun to be nostalgic about old design choices and aesthetics. There’s a lot of comfort in nostalgia, but’s more of an 'icing' than a 'cake'. We aren't blind to the shortcomings of the early web, instead we see the potential that was lost. And sometimes we just want to dress up our websites!</blockquote>
