'''<dfn>[https://yesterweb.org/ Yesterweb]</dfn>''' is a website and community ([[Discord]], [[Mastodon]], [[Hubs]], [[Gemini]], [[Minecraft]] instances) with a [[1990s]] design aesthetic that is “focused on nurturing creativity and diversity in web pages and digital social infrastructure”, with a number of [https://yesterweb.org/manifesto member contributed manifestos] that reference the IndieWeb.
