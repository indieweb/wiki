'''<dfn>[http://www.yelp.com Yelp]</dfn>''' is a [[review]] [[silo]] that also allows for [[checkins]].

The service also allows one to add compliments, [[recommendation|tips]], [[bookmark|bookmarks]], [[collection|collections]], [[events]], [[acquisition|order history]], [[followers]] and [[following]] functionalities.

== Data Export ==
Yelp provides some data export at https://www.yelp.com/profile_privacy by clicking on "Download a copy of your Yelp data". They then export your data and email it to the email address associated with your account.
