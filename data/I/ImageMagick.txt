'''<dfn>[https://www.imagemagick.org/ ImageMagick]</dfn>''' is open source software for editing [[photo|images]], typically on the [[command line interface|command line]], often for [[post|posting]] to ones website.

== How ==
See ImageMagick's [https://imagemagick.org/script/command-line-processing.php Command-line Processing] documentation.

Example: to create an [[AVIF]] from a [[PNG]]:

<code>magick images/my-photo.png images/my-photo.avif</code>
