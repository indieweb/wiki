The '''<dfn>IndieWeb</dfn>''' is a community of independent and personal websites based on the '''[[principles|principles]]''' of '''[[personal-domain|owning your domain]]''' and using it as '''[[How to set up web sign-in on your own domain|your primary online  identity]]''', '''[[POSSE|publishing on your own site first (optionally elsewhere)]]''', and '''[[ownyourdata|owning your content]]'''.

Want to get started on the IndieWeb? See '''[[Getting Started]]'''.

=== IndieWeb Standards ===
* [[IndieAuth]] - to authenticate with your website
* [[Webmention]] - to notify other websites, for peer-to-peer comments, likes, and other [[responses]]
* [[Micropub]] - to publish to your site using a variety of clients
* [[WebSub]] - to get real time updates on other sites' new content
* [[Microsub]] - (experimental) to follow others, collect posts, read, and interact with them

=== Syndication and Cross-posting ===
* [[POSSE]] - Publish (on your) Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere
* [[PESOS]] - Publish Elsewhere, Syndicate (to your) Own Site
* other methods of [[cross-posting]]
