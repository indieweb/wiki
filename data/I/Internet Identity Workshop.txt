The '''<dfn>[https://internetidentityworkshop.com/ Internet Identity Workshop]</dfn>''' is a workshop held twice a year at the [[Computer History Museum]] in [[Mountain View]] that focuses on [[identity]] issues where several IndieWeb related sessions have been held over the years.
