'''<dfn>Indy web</dfn>''' is a spider web encountered by the character Indiana Jones (AKA Indy), the web of Indiana Jones fan sites which link to each other, personal site features that make use of aesthetics from the Indiana Jones movies, or a misspelling of [[IndieWeb]].

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Jeremy Keith ===
{{adactio}}’s monthly [[archive]] pages show travel maps for the locations he’s been that can animate (with soundtrack!) in the theme of the maps shown in Indiana Jones movies when he flies from city to city.
* Example: https://adactio.com/archive/2019/08/map (requires JS, click "Play" button top left of map)
