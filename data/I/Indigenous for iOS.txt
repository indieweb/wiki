'''<dfn>Indigenous for iOS</dfn>''' is a native app for iOS that supports posting your website using [[Micropub]] and a built-in [[reader]] that supports [[Microsub]].

The [[project]] was initially developed by {{eddiehinkle.com}} and is available on the [https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/indigenous-social-timeline/id1271004584?mt=8 iOS App Store] with the source code on [https://github.com/EdwardHinkle/indigenous-ios GitHub]. There is also an [https://indieweb.org/indigenous_for_android Android version].

On May 8 2019, {{ eddiehinkle.com }} announced that it was [https://eddiehinkle.com/2019/05/08/5/article/ no longer maintained]; development was transferred to {{realize.be}} who subsequently announced on 27 Jan 2021 that they were [https://github.com/marksuth/indigenous-ios/issues/252 searching for new maintainer]; on 28 Sep 2021 {{marksuth}} became [https://marksuth.dev/posts/indigenous-desktop-ios/ the new maintainer].

= V1 Features =
All '''Bold Text''' is currently available in the beta.

* '''Share Sheet Support'''
** '''Share URL'''
*** '''Note in [[reply]] to URL''', Note quoting URL, '''[[Like]] URL''', '''[[Repost]] URL''', '''[[Bookmark]] URL''', [[reacji]] shortcut options
** Share Photo
*** Post photo with caption
*** Add [[person-tag]]s (using micropub query)
* '''Posting'''
** '''Posting note'''
*** Add location, '''Add photo using a micropub media endpoint''', '''Add categories''', '''Add syndication targets''', Add [[person-tag]]s (using micropub query)
** '''Voice posting for notes using Siri'''
* '''Reader'''
** '''Displays all [[Microsub]] channels'''
** '''Preview of all posts in a channel'''
*** Filter posts by different attributes
*** Set specific views for different channels
**** Gallery View, Map View, News View, Stream View
** '''View original source URL'''
** '''Respond to posts''', Like, Bookmark, Reacji Shortcuts, '''Reply'''
* '''Supporting multiple micropub accounts with a default one selected'''
* Notifications
** Forward mf2 json to your endpoint with a secret key to receive a push notification
*** Example: https://indigenous.abode.pub/eddiehinkle.com/notification would send a push notification to any app authenticated as eddiehinkle.com.
