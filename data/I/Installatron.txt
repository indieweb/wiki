'''<dfn>[https://installatron.com/ Installatron]</dfn>''' is a proprietary software used by numerous [[shared hosting]] providers that allows for one-click installs of many popular applications such as [[WordPress]], [[Drupal]], and [[Known]].

The service can be used on individual domains, by website providers or large shared hosting providers. 

The installation process sets up a database, installs an applications, and can allow for automatic updates and back ups.
