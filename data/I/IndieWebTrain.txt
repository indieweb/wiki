'''<dfn>IndieWebTrain</dfn>''' was a train from [[Düsseldorf]] to [[Nürnberg]] on 2017-05-17 where some participants in [[IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2017]] decided to take the same train to [[IndieWebCamp Nürnberg]] and discuss & create IndieWeb things on the ride.
