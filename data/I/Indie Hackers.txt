'''<dfn>[https://www.indiehackers.com/ Indie Hackers]</dfn>''' is an online forum (no relation to IndieWeb or IndieWebCamp) of founders working on profitable businesses and side projects, that was notably mentioned in [https://mtlynch.io/why-i-quit-google/ Why I Quit Google to Work for Myself], a blog post detailing why a 4 year [[Google]] employee quit to pursue independent ventures.
