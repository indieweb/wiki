'''<dfn>Indigenous for Android</dfn>''' is a native app for Android that supports posting your website using [[Micropub]] and a built-in [[reader]] that supports [[Microsub]].

The [[project]] is under active development by [https://indieweb.org/User:Realize.be Kristof de Jaeger] and is available on [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.indieweb.indigenous Google Play] and [https://f-droid.org/en/packages/com.indieweb.indigenous/ F-Droid]. The source code can be found on [https://github.com/swentel/indigenous-android GitHub]. There is also an [https://indieweb.org/Indigenous_for_iOS iOS version].

== Features ==

These features are currently available

<ul>
<li>Multiple accounts</li>
<li>Posting content to your site: note, article, reply, like, bookmark, repost, event, rsvp, issue, checkin, geocache, venue, trip</li>
<li>Send image, video and audio to the media endpoint (if available)</li>
<li>Save as draft to finish later</li>
<li>Upload multiples images, videos and audio, add tags where relevant</li>
<li>Get / refresh syndication targets</li>
<li>Send location on note, article and event</li>
<li>Read posts from your site and optionally update or delete them</li>
<li>Share (direct) from other applications to Indigenous</li>
<li>Reader (optional): Overview of channels, reading the timeline and direct actions like reply, like ..</li>
<li>Manage channels and feeds directly from the app</li>
<li>Push notifications via https://indigenous.realize.be</li>
<li>Minimal GPS tracker to log your itineraries when walking, running, flying etc. When posting to your site, the data will be send using the micropub endpoint as a trip.</li>
<li>Manage contacts and use autocomplete of contacts in content - see https://realize.be/notes/1970</li>
</ul>
