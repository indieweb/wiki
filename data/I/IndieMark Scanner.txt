'''<dfn>IndieMark Scanner</dfn>''' is a project started by {{JackyAlcine}} to give IndieWeb participants a way to determine exactly where they fall on the scale of marks. It's like https://indiewebify.me/ but more automated.

== Currently Supported ==
* [[Web sign-in]] detection (level 1)
* robots.txt checking (level 1)
