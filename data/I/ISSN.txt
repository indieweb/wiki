International Standard Serial Number ('''<dfn>ISSN</dfn>''') is an identification number for periodicals that at least two IndieWeb community members have on their websites.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* Terence Eden as an ISSN in his website footer.
** https://shkspr.mobi/blog/
* Jeffrey Zeldman has an ISSN on his website about page.
** https://www.zeldman.com/about/
