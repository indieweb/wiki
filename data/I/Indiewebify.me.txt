'''<dfn>[https://indiewebify.me/ Indiewebify.me]</dfn>''' is a service that checks how indieweb-compatible your site is and reports back its results. It's roughly based on [[IndieMark]] and is available as open source:
* https://github.com/indieweb/indiewebify-me
