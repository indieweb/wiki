'''<dfn>[https://indiewebify.me/ Indiewebify.me]</dfn>''' is a service that checks how indieweb-compatible your site is and reports back its results; the verb <dfn>indiewebify</dfn> also means the act of making your site more IndieWeb [[friendly]]. 

Indiewebify.me is roughly based on [[IndieMark]] and is available as open source:
* https://github.com/indieweb/indiewebify-me

== Tools ==
* https://monocle.p3k.io/preview - preview feeds or posts to see what they will look like in [[Monocle]]
