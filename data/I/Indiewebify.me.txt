'''<dfn>[https://indiewebify.me/ Indiewebify.me]</dfn>''' is a service that checks how IndieWeb-compatible your site by checking for feature support like [[Web sign-in]], homepage [[h-card]], &amp; post [[h-entry]]; the verb <dfn>indiewebify</dfn> also means the act of making your site more IndieWeb [[friendly]].

Indiewebify.me is roughly based on [[IndieMark]] and is available as open source:
* https://github.com/indieweb/indiewebify-me

== Tools ==
* https://monocle.p3k.io/preview - preview feeds or posts to see what they will look like in [[Monocle]], a good way to check:
** [[h-feed]] on [[home]] page, [[stream]], or [[archive]] page
** [[h-entry]] on multiple posts in a page like the above or in a [[composite stream]]
