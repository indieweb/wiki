'''<dfn>[https://vero.co Vero]</dfn>''' is an Instagram-like social network gaining some publicity in 2018-02, which claims to go for a model sustained by yearly fees and fees from in-app product sales instead of normal advertising models ("claims to" since it is for now free).

It supports [[bookmarks]]/links, [[listen|listens]] for music, [[watch|watches]] for movie/tv, [[read]] related posts for books, [[location]], and [[photos]] as post kinds in addition to being able to share with a variety of predefined [[audience|audiences]].
