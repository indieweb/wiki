A '''<dfn>Virtual Private Server</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>VPS</dfn>) is a level of [[webhosting]] service where you get root access to a virtual system and can install whatever you want.

For examples and more details, see [[webhosting#Virtual_Private_Server]]
