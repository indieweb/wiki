'''<dfn>[https://json-ld.org/ JSON-LD]</dfn>''' is a method of encoding [[RDF]] (aka "Linked Data") as [[JSON]]. It makes special points of IDing resources with URIs, and of including the URIs of schema-like "contexts" for vocabularies defined by a variety sources.
