'''<dfn>Janrain</dfn>''' was an Identity Provider (as a service) company based out of [[Portland]], Oregon that was acquired by [[Akamai]] in 2019 and subsequently shutdown.

Many enterprises used them to store user data, help with [[social login]], and proxy requests to Silo APIs.

Their home page was at: <nowiki>https://janrain.com/</nowiki>

API Docs were at: <nowiki>http://developers.janrain.com/rest-api</nowiki>
