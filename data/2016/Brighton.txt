= <span class="p-name summary">IndieWebCamp Brighton 2016</span> =
<div class="p-description description">
There is a growing awareness of the importance of owning your data. To help people get started, we're meeting for a bar-camp like collaboration in Brighton for '''two days''' of brainstorming, working, teaching, and helping.

Join us at the fifth annual IndieWebCamp Brighton and learn how to use open web technologies to empower and ‚Äì maybe more importantly ‚Äì encourage users to own their own content and identities.
</div>
