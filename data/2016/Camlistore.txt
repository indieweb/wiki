'''<dfn>Camlistore</dfn>''' was a session at [[2016|IndieWeb Summit 2016]].

No notes were taken during this session. Eric gave a demo of his existing [[Camlistore]] and setting up a dev version locally. Briefly discussed using camlistore to cache people's avatars for received webmentions.
