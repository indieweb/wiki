'''<dfn>[http://news.indiewebcamp.com IndieNews]</dfn>''' is an indieweb news aggregator, similar to [[Hacker News]], which only accepts submissions via webmention.

How to use IndieNews:
* [http://news.indiewebcamp.com/how-to-submit-a-post submit a post] - post something on your own site and send [[webmention]] 
* [http://news.indiewebcamp.com/how-to-comment comment on a post] - post a comment on your own site [[in-reply-to]] the original post, with a syndication URL to the IndieNews post.
* up-vote a post - sign-in with [[IndieAuth]] to enable the up-vote UI.
