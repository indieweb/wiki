'''<dfn>remote participation</dfn>''' refers to good practices for enabling people to take part in IndieWebCamp [[events]] at a different location and collaboration across locations.

See Barnaby Walters's blog post on [http://waterpigs.co.uk/articles/remote-participation Pros, Cons and a formula for Remote Participation at events].

For IndieWebCamps:
* Setup a talky.io channel for the camp, e.g. talky.io/indiewebcampnyc

For Homebrew Website Club:
* Setup and share a Vidyo URL

== Equipment ==
Minimum:
* laptop with a camera

Helpful for a site/room with multiple people:
* HD Webcam, e.g. [http://www.logitech.com/en-us/product/hd-pro-webcam-c920?crid=34 Logitech HD Pro Webcam C920]

Helpful for an individual
* Headphones/headset with microphone (helps reduce/eliminate echoes/feedback)
