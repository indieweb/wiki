'''<dfn>[http://tt-rss.org/redmine/projects/tt-rss/wiki tt-rss]</dfn>''' is a traditional [[Google]] Reader style [[feed reader]] that you have to self host that also supports consuming [[PuSH]] notifications.


== IndieWeb Examples ==
Individuals self-hosting tt-rss on their own domain:
* [[User:cweiske.de|cweiske.de]] at https://feeds.cweiske.de/
* [[User:Jorgee.net|JorgeE.net]] at http://feed.jorgee.net/
* [[User:david.shanske.com|David Shanske]] hosting it on a low end VPS
