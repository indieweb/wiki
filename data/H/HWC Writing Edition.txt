'''<dfn>HWC Writing Edition</dfn>''' is an online meetup about writing on the web, typically hosted on a weekend every month or two.

== Upcoming events ==

Check out the #writing category on the IndieWeb Events calendar for upcoming events: https://events.indieweb.org/tag/writing

== What we talk about ==

We talk about all things writing on the web! In the past, we have discussed topics like:

* Where we find motivation
* What the web is as a medium for writing
* How we choose what to write about next
* What the idea of "balance" means to us in writing.

== Note taking ==

We take notes because our discussions regularly inspire new ideas and it can be helpful to have a point of reference for both participants and people who couldn't join.

If you join an event and don't want any notes to be taken about what you say, please let the event host know.
