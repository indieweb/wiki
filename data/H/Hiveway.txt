'''<dfn>Hiveway</dfn>''' (AKA Etherhive<ref>https://twitter.com/EtherHive</ref>) was perhaps the shortest lived [[silo]] (~7d), [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Initial_coin_offering ICO] (~4d)<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20180307092055/https:/twitter.com/hivewaynet</ref>, and supposed use of [[blockchain]] ever; as a fork of [[Mastodon]]<ref>https://github.com/hiveway/hiveway</ref> that attempted to hide the fact<ref>https://twitter.com/nightpool/status/970392782903959552</ref><ref>https://medium.com/we-distribute/hiveway-io-shamelessly-rips-off-of-mastodon-and-slaps-a-blockchain-on-top-for-some-reason-57b7aba3e84f</ref>, later admitting it<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20180307104235/https://medium.com/@hiveway/hiveway-ceo-we-are-the-biggest-mastodon-fans-c55a67e4f217</ref><ref>https://twitter.com/espectalll/status/970599307941380096</ref>, then shutdown within two days<ref>https://twitter.com/todonnl/status/971024161010315264</ref>.
