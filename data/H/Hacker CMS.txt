'''<dfn>[https://github.com/kentonv/ssjekyll Hacker CMS]</dfn>''' is a [[Markdown]]-based web site editing and publishing app [[project]] built on top [[Jekyll]], [[Ace]], and [[jsTree]] that can be installed on [[Sandstorm]].

Author:
* This app was stiched together over the course of one day by Kenton Varda.[https://sandstorm.io/apps/]

Open source:
* https://github.com/kentonv/ssjekyll

== IndieWeb support ==
There doesn't appear to be any support (yet) of various IndieWeb [[building blocks]] such as [[microformats]] and [[webmention]].
* Issue 3: [https://github.com/kentonv/ssjekyll/issues/3 feature request: webmention receiving (and display of comments, likes, reposts) support]
