'''<dfn>Here be dragons</dfn>''' is in general a warning of potential dangers or dangerous areas, originally on physical maps, on the IndieWeb it refers to particularly problematic [[silos]] that usually formed in response to entire sets of abusive behaviors and accounts being banned from Twitter. Examples: Gab, Parler.
