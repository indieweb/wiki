'''<dfn>[https://www.hetzner.de/en/ Hetzner]</dfn>''' is a large German hosting provider, providing root servers and VPS, and is also popular as a colocation provider for smaller providers.

Hetzner runs on [https://www.hetzner.com/unternehmen/nachhaltigkeit/ 100% green electricity].
