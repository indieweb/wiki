An '''<dfn>endpoint</dfn>''' is a [[URL]] for an application to communicate with to make something happen.

Examples of endpoints on the IndieWeb are:

* [[Webmention]] endpoint where a website receives their Webmentions.
* [[IndieAuth]]:
** [[authorization-endpoint|Authorization endpoint]] for logins, and
** [[token-endpoint|token endpoint]] so a long-lived session can be created.
* [[micropub-endpoint|Micropub endpoint]] where [[Micropub]] [[Micropub/Clients|clients]] can send new posts.
