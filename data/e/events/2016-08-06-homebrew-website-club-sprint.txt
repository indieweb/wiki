<h1><span class="p-name summary">Summer Homebrew Website Club Sprint</span></h1>

<div class="p-summary">
Full day Homebrew Website Club meetup. Code/writing sprint. No talks. Come and work on your IndieWeb project, be it your personal website, IndieWeb tool, or anything else. Or write a blog post, or two.

One day, 10:00 to 18:00. Come anytime, leave anytime. Lunch in the middle. Drinks afterwards. 

Join a community with like-minded interests. Bring friends that want a personal site!

Any questions? Ask '''[http://indiewebcamp.com/irc/today?beta#bottom in the chatroom]'''!
</div>

Exchange information, swap ideas, talk shop, help work on a project...
