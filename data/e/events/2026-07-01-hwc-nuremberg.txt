'''<dfn>[https://events.indieweb.org/2026/07/homebrew-website-club-nuremberg-germany-nuedigital-uE7fdezRQSA4 Homebrew Website Club Nürnberg]</dfn>''' was an IndieWeb meetup in real life that took place on 2026-07-01. It was a special issue of HWC Nürnberg that took place as part of the [https://nuernberg.digital Nürnberg Digital Festival]. The last event before this one was held on [https://indieweb.org/events/2026-06-10-hwc-nuremberg 2026-06-10].

Thanks to {{tilman}} for hosting us at the [https://d.th-nuernberg.de/en/ design faculty of Technische Hochschule Nürnberg Georg Simon Ohm].

See also the matching entry on the [https://homebrew-website.club/t/homebrew-website-club-10-juni-2026/ HWC Nuremberg Discourse].

== Notes ==

[[File:hwc-nuremberg-2026-07.jpg|thumb|alt=All participants of HWC Nuremberg 2026/07 Special Nürnberg Digital Festival Edition|All participants of HWC Nuremberg 2026/07 Special Nürnberg Digital Festival Edition]]

For this special edition of Homebrew Website Club Nürnberg we were honored to welcome 21 lovely people who were interested in the ideas of the IndieWeb. We were excited for having such an international audience with guests originating from the US, Iran, Hongkong and many other countries, so we switched the meeting language to English.

After a short introduction to HWC by {{tilman}} we continued by showing our websites and telling about some features we build recently. Some references to the IndieWebCamp on the weekend before the meeting were dropped as well.

{{bjoern}} showed a new feature for his site (that is not yet published) which changes the header image and the intro text according to his daily fitness. The data for this is collected by his Google Fitbit Air fitness wristband and later on gathered using the Google Health API during the website build process.

After the intro round with inspiring websites from all over the world it was time to mingle. The participants grabbed some drinks and started lively discussions with each other.

Invitations were sent out to attend the next regular meeting in the [https://indieweb.org/events/2026-06-10-hwc-nuremberg upcoming week]. We hope to see many people from today there as well.
