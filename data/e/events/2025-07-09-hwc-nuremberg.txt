Wolfgang showed a HTML workshop which will take at a [https://digital-vereint.de/digitalconvention/2025/ local convention in October]. He also showed us the web site for a hiking community he works on in his spare time. The site is many years old and we discussed some improvements to it.

Max then showed us his new [https://frame.work/de/en/laptop13 Framework 13] laptop he got yesterday.
