== Notes ==

Matt showed up as he is currently in Nuremberg during an Europe trip. As he's interested in the whole IndieWeb movement, we made a quick round of introduction and showed him what we do in the IndieWeb space with our websites.

Max also showed the latest changes he did on his website. He (finally 😉) updated his /now page and he also removed the third-party dependency to videos he hosted on [[Vimeo]] up until now. He re-encoded all the content and added the Plyr player to his website. His site is now free of third-party dependencies.

René also created a new (yet unpublished) site called 'Sketchbook' where he uses PaperJS to create sketches for his self built 3D plotter.
