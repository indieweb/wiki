== Notes ==

[[File:HWC Nuremberg 03-2026.jpg|thumb|alt=The participants of HWC Nuremberg 03/2026|The participants of HWC Nuremberg 03/2026]]

Matt showed up as he is currently in Nuremberg during an Europe trip. He's interested in the whole [[IndieWeb]] movement, so we made a quick round of introduction and showed him what we do in the IndieWeb space with our websites. He took a lot of notes.

Max showed the latest changes he did on his website. He (finally 😉) updated his [https://maxhaesslein.de/now /now] page and he also removed the third-party dependency to videos he hosted on [[Vimeo]] up until now. He re-encoded all the content and added the Plyr player to his website. His site is now free of third-party dependencies.

René also created a new (yet unpublished) site called 'Sketchbook' where he uses PaperJS to create sketches for his self built 3D plotter. A really cool tool.

{{bjoern}} opened up the engine room and showed how his infrastructure based on Docker containers and Portainer works behind the scenes. He also talked about a rootkit attack on his new VPS which was exposed due to an open Portainer agent.

{{bjoern}} and Tilman plan to take part in the [[IndieWebCamp]] [[2026/Düsseldorf|Düsseldorf]], René and {{bjoern}} will be present at beyond tellerrand Düsseldorf in the following days as well.
