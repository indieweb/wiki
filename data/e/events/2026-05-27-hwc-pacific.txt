== Naty's Zensical demo ==
* Decided to migrate her secondary site from WordPress to documentation generator 
** https://zensical.org/
** By the creators of Materials for MkDocs [[mkdocs]]** Spun off due to conflict: https://fpgmaas.com/blog/collapse-of-mkdocs/
* Naty chose it because she wanted something simpler to Hugo and try a new SSG
* Search is very snappy, but had to do workarounds because it wasn't designed for blogs (e.g. dates, archive pages)
* To convert WordPress content to Markdown, Naty used https://github.com/lonekorean/wordpress-export-to-markdown
* Ross asked how this site is different to https://burgeonlab.com
** Naty plans to put content that is more casual/personal (not so technical) and take a stab at maintaining a digital garden on this domain
** The decision for having multiple websites or consolidating all content into one central website is something that needs to be mulled over
