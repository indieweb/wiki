Distant Past —
An archive of posts for events that happened at some point, but the exact time frame of when they took place is not known. e.g. A film watched at somepoint, or a book you've read previously, but don't know when.

Retro posts —
Posts that are added retro-actively to a site, for activities that took place in the past. Examples would include posts for places visited, events previously attended etc.

Near future —
A collection of posts for activities and events that you intend to do at some point "in the near future". Examples would be things like reading lists, films you want to watch, or places you want to visit.
