'''<dfn>[https://events.indieweb.org/2025/04/homebrew-website-club-nuremberg-germany-JVnsbe2yMg56 Homebrew Website Club Nürnberg]</dfn>''' was an IndieWeb meetup in real life that took place on 2025-05-14.

It was our first time at the [https://d.th-nuernberg.de/en/ design faculty of Technische Hochschule Nürnberg Georg Simon Ohm], thanks Tilman for hosting us!.

See also the matching entry on the [https://homebrew-website.club/t/homebrew-website-club-9-april-2025/68 HWC Nuremberg Discourse].

== Notes ==

Tilman gave a short introduction about the design faculty and all the options that are available to study here. This place will be our homebase for now as LEONARDO will move to a new location.

Philip showed us some improvements he made to his website. There was a short discussion at on what point you have to make clear that you link to external content like YouTube or Facebook.

René showed his blog nugget29.com and the backend behind it he built. We also talked about the projects he showed on his website (like picoTracker).
