'''<dfn>Monthly Guiding Compass Homebrew Website Club</dfn>''' was an IndieWeb meetup on Zoom, and this page is an archive of the Etherpad notes.

This was the first meetup with the “guiding compass” format, with an emphasis on community collaboration.
