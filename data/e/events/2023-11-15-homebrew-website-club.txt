== Notes ==

As there were some new participants, Sebastian and {{bjoern}} did an intro to the IndieWeb and the idea of the [[hwc]]. We then made an introduction round and showed our websites and/or struggles with Social Media.

* {{bjoern}} - [[digital garden]] based on [[Obsidian]] (IndieAuth/h-card supported)
* Sebastian - static website on [[Nikola]] (no IndieWeb features yet)
* Max - static website on [[Kirby]], works with Tilman on https://www.maxhaesslein.de/web/knot-system/
* Tilman - website runs on Knot (see above), IndieWeb features built-in
* Alex - static website, no CMS
* Saskia - Wordpress blog, no IndieWeb features yet
* Klaus - no current website, managed some before
* Simone - is overwhelmed with Social Media

[[File:HWC Nuremberg 2023-11-15 Group Shot.jpg|thumb|alt=HWC Nuremberg 2023-11-15 Group Shot|HWC Nuremberg 2023-11-15 Group Shot]]

We then discussed the schedule for HWC Nuremberg. We settle for a monthly in-person meeting, so the next meetings will be 2023-12-13 and 2024-01-10.
