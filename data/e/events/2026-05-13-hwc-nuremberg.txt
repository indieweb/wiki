== Notes ==

[[File:hwc-nuremberg-2026-05.jpg|thumb|alt=The participants of Homebrew Website Club Nuremberg 05/2026|The participants of Homebrew Website Club Nuremberg 05/2026]]

We started with a round of introduction by the HWC regulars. After that, it was time for new new participants.

Nora heard of HWC at IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf and immediately decided to take part. She studied design and now works at a local web agency. Her homepage at https://noracudazzo.com/ is a portfolio page of all the projects she has done recently.

Fabi is a skilled portrait artist with a web presence at https://fabi-zeichnet.de. He studied at the Technische Hochschule Nürnberg. He also has a website at https://www.mixedmartinarts.com/ with his other projects and things of upgrading this site as it is a little outdated in his opinion.

After the intro, Philip showed some changes on his Speedjungle e.V. site. He created a 'web crate' plugin for [[Vite]] that uses Markdown as a base for dynamic content managemnent. He also showed the layout changes he did on his MC Scrpt web site.

René demoed the backend for his website based on Keystatic. He is used to dynamic website backends like Astro and talked about a different kind of thinking when working with static site generators. He also showed the business cards he made for [[Beyond Tellerrand]] Düsseldorf and Superbooth and the process behind them.

Björn demoed his new webmention setup (including the connection to [[Mastodon]] via [[Bridgy]]), the new lightbox feature for images on his site and talked, together with Tilman and Nora, about the experience at [[2026/Düsseldorf|IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2026]]. The usage of [[XSLT]] to render the [[RSS]] feed {{jo}} presented in Düsseldorf was discussed and the older ones in the crowd remembered the days when [[XHTML]] and XSLT were the new hot stuff. :)

The participants of [[Beyond Tellerrand]] Düsseldorf 2026 handed out some recommendations for talks to watch once they are online. In no particular order:

* https://beyondtellerrand.com/events/dusseldorf-2026/speakers/niels-leenheer
* https://beyondtellerrand.com/events/dusseldorf-2026/speakers/annie-atkins
* https://beyondtellerrand.com/events/dusseldorf-2026/speakers/andre-michelle
* https://beyondtellerrand.com/events/dusseldorf-2026/speakers/james-white

Everybody is also looking forward to IndieWebCamp Nürnberg 2026. This will be a blast!
