== Notes ==

[[File:HWC-Nuremberg-04-2026.jpg|thumb]]

The crew of HWC Nuremberg gathered again for another round of personal web projects and mutual inspiration.

Wolfgang was in troubleshooting mode, working through CSS layout issues in his image detail view inside WebEdition CMS — one of those satisfying debugging sessions where the problem is specific enough to actually make progress on.

René arrived with a freshly redesigned personal site at [https://renebreckner.de renebreckner.de]: new CSS throughout, including a proper dark mode implementation. He also added a ''now'' page, an updated ''about me'' page, and a new posts section — all generated with [https://astro.build Astro] and edited via [https://keystatic.com Keystatic], a Git-based CMS that keeps content close to the code.

Philipp showed progress on [https://github.com/speedjungle-ev/monstersofjungle.de monstersofjungle.de], the web presence for Speedjungle e.V. — the project is moving along nicely, as the [https://speedjungle-ev.github.io/monstersofjungle.de/ live preview] already demonstrates.

{{bjoern}} and {{tilman}} plan to take part in the [[IndieWebCamp]] [[2026/Düsseldorf|Düsseldorf]], René and {{bjoern}} will be present at beyond tellerrand Düsseldorf in the following days as well.
