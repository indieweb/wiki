<div class="p-content description">
As part of the 2020 Open Data Day Celebration in Ghana, Global Open Initiative in collaboration with i Watch Africa, Linux Accra User Group and Open Street Map Ghana will organize series of workshops to train people on how to contribute to Wikidata and Open Street Map. The workshop will also introduce participants to IndieWeb and how they can curate OERs using IndieWeb building blocks.
