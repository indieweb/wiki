== Question: How to integrate a calendar on your personal site? == 
* {{gwg}} is thinking of replacing Nextcloud, specifically the calendar
* It can get complicated between if you've RSVP'd or just want to hear about it
* RSVP webmention is a good way to keep track of events you have gone to if you want to create a public list of those things
** specific example: indicate he's attending an HWC and make it compelling for other people finding the event
** {{gRegor}} has used RSVP posts to add some additional context before, example: https://gregorlove.com/2022/01/im-attending-homebrew-website-club/ specifically with the intent of sharing on Twitter and perhaps getting someone's attention
* https://tantek.com/ has a calendar on his website and has a link to a webcal
* You could also write a blog post about an event you're going to
* You have to think about what you are comfortable sharing

* How do you find your own angle on a specific kind of post like photos/events/RSVPs/notes, etc.?
* Be gentle with yourself about not being sure about how to approach something!
