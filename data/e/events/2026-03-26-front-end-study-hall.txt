== Question: Why did we end up with React? ==
** History of React?
* React Documentary: https://youtu.be/8pDqJVdNa44?si=6Fer8fDnvMWPk9lr
*** Facebook? Facebooks motivations
** Andy Bell has an article "if it works for you that's great" http://piccalil.li/blog/if-it-works-its-right
* Rails pitch
* React won the battle royale of SPA frameworks (Ember.js, Angular.js, etc.)
* Cargo cult effect in web framework/library adoption (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cargo_cult_programming)
* non-dev managers asking for React experience in job descriptions -> more people learning react
* coding bootcamps arising to meet the demand of new web devs wanting to quickly learn react to apply to jobs
* more jobs asking for React experience because of the greater supply of React web devs, feedback loop
