A '''<dfn>Share Sheet</dfn>''' is a sharing dialog listing many different sharing options for something.

In Apple iOS the share sheets [https://developer.apple.com/library/ios/documentation/General/Conceptual/ExtensibilityPG/ became extendable by third party apps with iOS 8] and thus became the first Apple provided cross-app sharing method.
