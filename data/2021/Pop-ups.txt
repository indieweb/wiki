<span class="p-summary description">'''<dfn class="p-name summary">IndieWebCamp Pop-ups 2021</dfn>''' are stand alone single topic sessions originally started in 2020, partly in response to the coronavirus pandemic that prevented the long history of regularly occurring in-person camps in various cities.</span>

While not designed to be the traditional two day camps of sessions and creating, they are meant to foster ongoing discussion and development of topics of interest to the IndieWeb community.

=== Remote Participation ===
IndieWebCamp has a strong tradition of remote participation, typically using chat, Zoom and Etherpad during as well as after the event. More details and links for remote participation will be posted here on the days of the event. Also feel free to join us on [[discuss|chat]] to get realtime updates and conversation before, during, and after the sessions. ([https://chat.indieweb.org/ Direct web chat link here.])
