'''<dfn>rel-subscribe</dfn>''' is an experimental rel value for linking from your home page to your subscription endpoint, and is currently prototyped by {{aaronpk}} on aaronparecki.com; try the Follow button in the right side of the footer of his home page or any permalink.

{{cleverdevil}} has implemented the basics of the client-side interaction on his website to enable subscribing via [[Nextcloud News]].
