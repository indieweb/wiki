'''<dfn>[https://microformats.org/wiki/rel-edit rel-edit]</dfn>''' is a microformat for linking a page to a URL that lets you edit a page.

== How to ==

You can add <code>rel=edit</code> to any <code>a</code> link, or as a <code>link</code> tag on a web page:

<pre>
<link rel="edit" href="https://example.com/edit/page-name" />
<a rel="edit" href="https://example.com/edit/page-name">Edit this page</a>
</pre>
