'''<dfn>rssCloud</dfn>''' is part of the [http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/rss/rss.html#ltcloudgtSubelementOfLtchannelgt RSS specification], it allows publishers to notify subscribers when there are updates to a feed.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
[[User:Unicyclic.com_mal|Malcolm]]'s site https://unicyclic.com provides updates to subscribers, and will also subscribe to notifications from other sites that support rssCloud.

== [[Wordpress]] ==
Wordpress.com supports rssCloud. See: https://josephscott.org/archives/2009/09/rsscloud-for-wordpress/ and https://josephscott.org/archives/2009/10/rsscloud-wordpress-plugin-update-0-4-1/

== Scripting.com ==
Dave Winer supports rssCloud and has provided some documentation at: http://walkthrough.rsscloud.co/
