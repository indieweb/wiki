'''<dfn>recent photos</dfn>''' is a summary or filtered display of the most recently taken and posted [[photos]] on a site, typically a smaller subset of a [[photo stream]] page, supported on various silos as boxes on profile pages.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
None so far, but there are some itches (in rough date order of itch capture)
* {{aaronpk}}: https://github.com/aaronpk/p3k/issues/22
* {{t}}: [[Falcon#mobile_friendly_recent_photos_box]]

== Silo Examples ==
* [[Facebook]] - in the left column of a profile page
* [[Twitter]] - as part of recent photos & videos section of a profile
