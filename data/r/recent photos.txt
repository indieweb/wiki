'''<dfn>recent photos</dfn>''' is a summary or filtered display of the most recently taken and posted [[photo]] or [[photos]] on a site, typically a smaller subset of a [[photo stream]] page, supported on various silos as boxes on profile pages.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{aaronpk}}: most recent photo (singular) in the top part of his [https://aaronparecki.com/ home page] since [https://github.com/aaronpk/p3k/issues/22 since 2018]
* {{t}}: most recent 12 photos [https://tantek.com/2020/054/t2/added-recent-photos-embed since 2020-02-23] in a 3x4 grid in sidebar
** more thoughts for improvements: [[Falcon#mobile_friendly_recent_photos_box]]
* {{jo}}: most recent photo (singular) in footer area of [https://dead.garden/ home page] since ~2024(?)
* {{addyourself}}

== Silo Examples ==
* [[Facebook]] - in the left column of a profile page
* [[Twitter]] - as part of recent photos & videos section of a profile
