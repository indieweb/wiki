'''<dfn>rel</dfn>''' is an HTML attribute that has a space separated set of values defined by the [[rel registry]] used in the IndieWeb for various [[discovery]] purposes such as [[identity]] ([[rel-me]]), [[authorship]] ([[rel-author]]), [[Webmention]] endpoint, [[Micropub]] endpoint, [[IndieAuth]] endpoints, etc.
