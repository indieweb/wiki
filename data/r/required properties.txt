'''<dfn>required properties</dfn>''' are an [[anti-pattern]] that has been solved with [[microformats2]] - originally the result of unfortunate [[parser|parsing]]/consuming code centric assumptions, often captured in [[IETF]] [[RFC|RFCs]] (like [[vCard]], [[Atom]]) and propagated (like into classic [[microformats]]) that put undue burdens on publishers and result in a worse [[UI]], poor data, and publisher confusion.
