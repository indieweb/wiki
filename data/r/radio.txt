'''<dfn>radio</dfn>''' is broadcast audio transmitted over radio waves or internet streams.

== Tools ==
Tools for hosting your own online radio station

* [http://icecast.org icecast2] - a free, open source, high performance audio streaming server
* [https://www.rogueamoeba.com/nicecast/ nicecast] - a paid, closed source audio streaming client

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{barnabywalters}} has written about [https://waterpigs.co.uk/articles/how-to-stream-live-audio-over-the-web-using-icecast2-and-puredata/ streaming audio using Icecast2 and Puredata] which then also archives the stream as a post. In [https://indieweb.org/2016/Nuremberg/Demos#Barnaby_Walters 2016] he demo'd turning that into a Micropub client.
* {{aaronpk}} has experimented with streaming actual radio over the internet: https://w7apk.com/streaming-radio
* {{jo}} streams public domain music and audio books using icecast2 and ices at https://dead.garden/radio/
