'''<dfn>[http://wunderlist.com Wunderlist]</dfn>''' is an app for managing todo lists, is available for many desktop and mobile platforms, and has an API. [[Microsoft]] acquired Wunderlist. On 2017-04-19, they announced a preview of [[Microsoft To-Do]] and that they would eventually retire Wunderlist.[https://blogs.office.com/2017/04/19/introducing-microsoft-to-do-now-available-in-preview/]
