'''<dfn>Wrong on the internet</dfn>''' is the last phrase of [https://xkcd.com/386/ XKCD #386], “Duty Calls”, a satirical comic expressing the difficulty of not arguing with someone making a mistaken statement on the internet, often on [[social media]], however also on [[blog]]s or other independent websites.
