'''<dfn>Wiggles</dfn>''' (also known as ''3D shakes'', ''3D photos/images'' or ''jittergrams'') are a type of [[photo]]/[[video]] [[post]] consisting of animated photos consisting of typically two (sometimes more) alternating frames taken from slightly different viewpoints, giving the impression of a 3D photo when animated.

They utilise [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiggle_stereoscopy Wiggle Stereoscopy], from which they take their name.

== How ==
It should be fairly easy to create wiggles with any camera and application which makes animated GIFs.

'''Previously:'''
* The [http://web.archive.org/web/20140209023059/http://www.jittergramapp.com/ Jittergram iOS app] (no longer available) made it easy to quickly create wiggles, with the option of exporting them as regular 3D photos requiring 3D glasses.
