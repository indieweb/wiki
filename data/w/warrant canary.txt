'''<dfn>warrant canary</dfn>''' is a regularly published statement that the publisher has not received a legal request for private user information (often accompanied by a requirement not to disclose said request).

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* As of 2016-02-17, withknown.com publishes the number of "Government Information Requests" received as part of their privacy policy: https://withknown.com/privacy/#Government+Information+Requests
* ...
