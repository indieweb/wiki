'''<dfn>water</dfn>''' is a common [[drink]] with variants like sparkling or flavored like coconut, and though rarely posted on the indieweb, there are smart water bottles (like Hidrate Spark) that will track your water drinking automatically and then sync to a [[fitness]] silo.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

{{aaronpk}} has posted water [[drink]] posts [https://aaronparecki.com/2013/11/02/11/ since 2013-11-02], for everything except plain water:
* 2013-11-02 [https://aaronparecki.com/2013/11/02/11/ Coconut Water]
* 2015-10-03 [https://aaronparecki.com.dev/2015/10/03/23/ Tonic Water]
* 2016-10-25 [https://aaronparecki.com/2016/10/25/12/ Sparkling Water]

== Silo Examples ==
=== Hidrate Spark ===
[https://hidratespark.com/ Hidrate Spark] ([https://twitter.com/hidratespark @hidratespark]) water bottles sync over bluetooth with a mobile [[app]] which tracks your water drinking automatically and can further sync to several [[fitness]] silos.

Reviews:
* 2016-11-04 sensors and sensibility: [https://sensorsandsensibility.com/posts/2016/4/11/hidrate-spark-smart-bottle Hidrate Spark smart bottle review] <blockquote>Taking dangerous steps into the land of We Put a Chip In It, I backed and just received the Hidrate Spark - a "smart" water bottle that tracks how much you're drinking.</blockquote>
