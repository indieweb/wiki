'''<dfn>water</dfn>''' is a common [[drink]] with variants like sparkling or flavored like coconut, though rarely posted on the indieweb, there are smart water bottles (like Hidrate Spark) that will track your water drinking automatically and possibly sync with several [[fitness]] silos.
