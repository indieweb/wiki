The '''<dfn>[[2020/West|IndieWebCamp 2020 West]] schedule and session grid</dfn>''' is where notes and videos from all sessions can be found.

This page will update frequently on the days of Camp, so please refresh it regularly on those days to make sure you have the most recent information.

The session grid is developed on day 1, and is based on suggestions by the attendees.

All times are <strong>local</strong> (Pacific) times. If you are a remote attendee, make sure to [https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html?iso=20200627T160000&p1=137&p2=179&p3=136&p4=195&p5=37&p6=141&p7=215&p8=176&p9=47&p10=196&p11=248 convert to your timezone].

== <span class="p-name">Friday Pre-Party</span> ==
* On [https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html?iso=20200627T030000&p1=137&p2=179&p3=136&p4=195&p5=37&p6=141&p7=215&p8=176&p9=47&p10=196&p11=248 Friday, June 26 from 20:00-22:00] Eat Your Own Cooking Pre-Party

* [https://events.indieweb.org/2020/06/indiewebcamp-west-eat-your-own-cooking-pre-party-DH5F8SRjSHff Get the Zoom link]

The night before camp, we're continuing our tradition of a pre-camp dinner...except we will be eating together apart. So, set a place setting in front of your webcam and join us for some distance socializing. In respect to other timezones, you need not bring dinner. Other meals, snacks, drinks, etc. are welcome. We will set up some Zoom break out rooms for people to pop into for some conversations.
