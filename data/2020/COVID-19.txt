<span class="p-summary">The [[2020|IndieWeb Summit 2020]] organizers have made the difficult decision to cancel IndieWeb Summit for 2020.</span>

If you have registered through the ticket system, you will be getting an email to give you the option of either getting a refund on your ticket or a credit for next year.

We will be organizing an Online IndieWebCamp during the June 27-28 weekend in the West Coast timezone. For more information, visit https://events.indieweb.org/2020/06/indiewebcamp-west-2020-ZB8zoAAu6sdN
