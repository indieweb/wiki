'''<dfn>ThinkUp</dfn>''' was a [[silo]] (at thinkup.com) that analyzed activity on social networks like [[Facebook]], [[Instagram]], and [[Twitter]]. It was closed 2016-07-18 and refunds were issued for the balance of member subscriptions. [https://medium.com/@anildash/the-end-of-thinkup-e600bc46cc56 2016-06-13 The end of ThinkUp]

The source code is available at https://github.com/ThinkUpLLC/ThinkUp

As of 2017-11-25, the thinkup.com domain is a coupon/discount tracking site.
