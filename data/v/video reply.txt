'''<dfn>video reply</dfn>''' is a reply with a video, a [[video]] post that is in-reply-to another post. Platforms such as [[TikTok]] and [[Instagram]] may allow for collaborative remix [[reply]] posts, and [[reaction]] [[posts]] of many types are common across [[silos]]. 

YouTube video replies were dropped per https://techcrunch.com/2013/08/27/google-dumps-video-responses-from-youtube-due-to-dismal-0004-click-through-rate/.
