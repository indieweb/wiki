'''<dfn>viral</dfn>''' is an adjective used to describe posts that are shared very broadly very rapidly, usually on [[silo]]s, often due to some timely aspect of the post, like humor related to current events, or a well explained summary of an otherwise complex issue, or both, almost always eliciting a strong emotional response which motivates rapid sharing.
