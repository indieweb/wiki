'''<dfn>Visualization</dfn>''' is display of [[data]] with visual representations, such as with [[graphics]] and charts, beyond raw spreadsheet entries or [[writing|prose descriptions]].

Writer [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Tufte Edward Tufte] has written classic books describing the history and practice of graphic visualization. He coined the term [[sparkline]] and remains influential in the field.
