'''<dfn>Visualization</dfn>''' is display of [[data]] with visual representations, such as with [[graphics]] and charts, beyond raw spreadsheet entries or [[writing|prose descriptions]].

Writer [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Tufte Edward Tufte] [https://www.edwardtufte.com] has written classic books describing the history and practice of graphic visualization. He coined the term ''"sparkline"''. 

Many [[IndieWeb]] members include [[sparklines]] to represent activity data on their websites.

== Why? ==
{{section stub}}
Graphical representations of activity and data are useful for understanding trends over time such as for [[streak|streaks]]. [[Maps]] with overlaid data can tell a story as well.
