'''<dfn>[https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-crosspost/ WordPress Crosspost]</dfn>''' is a [[POSSE]] plugin for [[WordPress]] that allows one to send posts from a self-hosted WordPress install to [[WordPress.com]] or [[JetPack]] enabled self-hosted WordPress sites.
