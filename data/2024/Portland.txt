'''<dfn>IndieWebCamp Portland 2024</dfn>''' was a one day discussion, create, and demo camp from 08:00-17:00 on 2024-08-25, the Sunday after [[XOXO]] 2024, at the Grand Stark Study Hall.

This was the 10th IndieWebCamp in [[Portland]], Oregon!
