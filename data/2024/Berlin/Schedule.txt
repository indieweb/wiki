'''<dfn>2024/Berlin/Schedule</dfn>''' is an hour-by-hour schedule and table of 14 sessions held across three rooms during [[2024/Berlin|IndieWebCamp Berlin 2024]].

== Saturday ==

Day 1 is about discussing in a [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BarCamp BarCamp]-like environment. Bring a topic you’d like to discuss or join in on topics as they are added to the board.

Session topics will be proposed and chosen on the day of the event and organized into a grid.

Sessions are facilitated by the participant who proposed the topic. Learn more at [[session facilitating]].

Times listed are in <abbr title="Central European Standard Time">CEST</abbr>.

== Sunday ==

Day 2 is about making things! Direct your inspiration from what we discussed day 1 into something on your site. Work with others or on your own. We’ll have demos at the end of the day to see what everyone worked on!

* 09:30 – Doors open
* 10:00 – Day 2 kick-off
* 10:30 – Hack sessions
* 12:30 – Group photo and lunch
* 13:30 – Hack sessions continue
* 15:00 – Demos
* 15:45 – Clean-up
* 16:00 – Camp closed
