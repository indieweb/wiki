'''<dfn>[https://getnikola.com/ Nikola]</dfn>''' is an open source [[static site generator]] written in Python. It supports [http://plugins.getnikola.com/v7/continuous_import importing RSS feeds as posts] and it handles data files, like [[Jekyll]] and [[Hugo]]. It uses [[h-entry]] for posts.
