'''<dfn>[https://nitter.net/ Nitter]</dfn>''' is a free and open source alternative [[Twitter]] front-end focused on privacy.

== Features ==
* [[RSS]] feeds for Twitter user accounts
* Alternative profile URLs for Twitter profiles, e.g. https://nitter.net/t
