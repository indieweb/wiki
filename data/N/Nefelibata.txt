'''<dfn>[https://github.com/betodealmeida/nefelibata Nefelibata]</dfn>''' is an open source [[static site generator]] written in [[Python]].

It does [[POSSE]] to:

* [[Mastodon]]
* [[Twitter]]
* [[Medium]]
* [[WT.Social]]
* [[FAWM]]

In addition to supporting [[webmention]]. The generated static website can be automatically published to [[Amazon S3]], [[Neocities]] and [[IPFS]].
