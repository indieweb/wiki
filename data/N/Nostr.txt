'''<dfn>[https://nostr.com/ Nostr]</dfn>''' is another [[decentralized]] social web protocol, developed as an alternative to [[ActivityPub]] and [[Secure Scuttlebutt]], that is supported by [[granary]] and a [https://github.com/snarfed/bridgy-fed/issues/446 proposed feature] for [[Bridgy Fed]].
