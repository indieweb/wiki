'''<dfn>NFC</dfn>''' is a wireless system that can transmit small amounts of data over short distances, typically used in contactless [[payments]] or to create tags that can open a website when scanned with a phone.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} left space on his [https://aaronparecki.com/2020/03/15/15/business-cards business cards] to be able to attach an NFC sticker, which when scanned, launches a URL to an event-specific landing page with additional contact info such as:

* https://aaronparecki.com/profile/?oauth
