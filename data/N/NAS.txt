    '''<dfn>NAS</dfn>''' is a Network Attached Storage device, either a purpose-built computer or an embedded system which shares storage over a network..

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{gwg}} uses his purpose built NAS to store the primary copy of his personal photos, videos, and documents as well as digitized copies of his physical media(movies, TV shows, and music).
