'''<dfn>Network News Transfer Protocol</dfn>''' ('''NNTP''') is used to connect [[Usenet]] news servers and end user client applications.

== Running your own NNTP instance for your site ==

[http://www.methodize.org/nntprss/ NNTPRSS] is a tiny java program that bridges RSS feeds to NNTP by providing a local NNTP server.
