'''<dfn>Network News Transfer Protocol</dfn>''' ('''NNTP''') is used to connect [[Usenet]] news servers and end user client applications.

== Bridging Web with NNTP ==

* [https://www.methodize.org/nntprss/ NNTPRSS] is a [[feed]] (RSS & ATOM) subscription server that maps [[posts]] to news items, making any NNTP client a [[feed reader]].
* [https://koldfront.dk/git/illuminant/about/ Illuminant] is an [[ActivityPub]] server that maps the [[fediverse]] to newsgroups, making any NNTP client a [[social reader]].
