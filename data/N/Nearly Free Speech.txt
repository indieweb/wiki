Nearly Free Speech is a hosting provider with servers in the NYC region. For hosting static websites, they charge as little as $0.01 per day, making them ideally suited for very small sites. PHP and other languages are supported, MySQL databases are also supported. 

* They have a pricing estimator on their website: https://www.nearlyfreespeech.net/estimate
* {{mat.tl}} is using them to host a static copy of a defunct local newspaper's WordPress website
