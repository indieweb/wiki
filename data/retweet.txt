A '''<dfn>retweet</dfn>''' is a [[silo]]-native [[repost]] of a [[tweet]] on [[Twitter]].

== History ==
Before Twitter implemented a native way to retweet, it was common to manually retweet by copying the full text of the tweet, including the @username, and prefixing with the text "RT"
