'''<dfn>statistics</dfn>''' are numbers that show measurements of various IndieWeb things like sites, posts, usage of protocols like Webmention, deployments of projects and plugins.

== See Also ==

* [https://brid.gy/ Bridgy homepage] shows stats of number of accounts, responses handled, links analyzed, webmentions sent, posts published, and blog posts processed.
* 2016-06-06 [https://snarfed.org/2016-06-06_bridgy-stats-update-2 Bridgy stats update] with charts, methodology, and links to previous stats updates.
* [https://the-federation.info/ The Federation dot info] is a site that shows stats for [[Diaspora]], [[Friendica]], and [[HubZilla]].
* [https://donp.org/graph/dashboard/db/mastodon?orgId=1&from=now-30d&to=now Last 30 days of Mastodon stats] - number of instances, toots per second, users.
