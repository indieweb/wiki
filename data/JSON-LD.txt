'''<dfn>JSON-LD</dfn>''' is a method of encoding [[RDF]] (aka "Linked Data") as [[JSON]]. It makes special points of IDing resources with URIs, and of including the URIs of schema-like "contexts" for resources (generally, existing RDF types such as [[FOAF]]).
