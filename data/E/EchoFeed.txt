'''<dfn>[https://echofeed.app EchoFeed]</dfn>''' is a service to cross post from [[feeds]] to other services and includes support for sending [[Webmention]]s.

== Features ==
It supports cross posting to:

* [[Mastodon]]
* [[Micro.blog]]
* [[Bluesky]]
* [[GitHub]]
* [[Discord]]

It can also send notifications via:
* [[Webmention]]
* [[webhook]]
