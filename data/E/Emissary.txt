'''<dfn>[https://github.com/EmissarySocial/emissary Emissary]</dfn>''' is an open source server [[project]] for “a different kind of distributed social medium that is friendly, safe, and welcoming to everyone” that supports IndieWeb [[building blocks]].

== Features ==
* https://mastodon.social/@benpate/109748374505300820
** "It's slow progress, but I'm pretty stoked.  In addition to RSS, Atom, JSONFeed, #IndieWeb WebMentions, and WebSub, Emissary now supports "Remote Follows" on the #Fediverse, which makes it tons easier to follow cool people and sites from around the web.Next Steps: ActivityPub inbox/outbox and RSSCloud" [https://mastodon.social/@benpate @benpate] January 25, 2023

== IndieWeb Friendly ==
Emissary is IndieWeb friendly with support for the following building blocks:
* [[Webmention]]
* [[WebSub]]
* feeds: RSS, Atom, JSONFeed

=== Planned ===
