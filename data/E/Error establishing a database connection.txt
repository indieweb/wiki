'''<dfn>Error establishing a database connection</dfn>''' is a common error message often shown as big bold text on sites (often [[WordPress]], [https://twitter.com/search?q=%22Error%20establishing%20a%20database%20connection%22&src=typed_query&f=live nearly daily across sites]) that have experienced a [[database-antipattern#DB_connection_loss|loss of connection between their web server (software) and their database server (software)]].
