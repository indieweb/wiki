'''<dfn>[http://emoj.li/ Emojli]</dfn>''' was a note hosting silo based entirely on [[emoji]], like [[Twitter]], but with emoji-only usernames & content, launched 2014-06-30 as a joke, shutdown 2015-07-31 due to maintenance costs rising too quickly.

Launch: https://twitter.com/Emojli/status/483603510056198145.
