'''<dfn>EyeEm</dfn>''' is a [[photo]] hosting [[silo]] that unilaterally changed its [[terms of service]] in 2024 to license any photos users have contributed to train generative AI models, including by external companies, which may be enough reason to avoid [[POSSE]]ing your posts there. 

* 2024-04-26 TechCrunch: [https://techcrunch.com/2024/04/26/photo-sharing-community-eyeem-will-license-users-photos-to-train-ai-if-they-dont-delete-them/ Photo-sharing community EyeEm will license users’ photos to train AI if they don’t delete them]
