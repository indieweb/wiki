'''<dfn>Edinburgh</dfn>''' is a city in Scotland that held an [[2015/Edinburgh|IndieWebCamp in 2015]], and a [[Homebrew Website Club]] meetup from [[events/2015-08-13-homebrew-website-club|2015-08-13]] to [[events/2016-02-09-homebrew-website-club|2016-02-09]] which restarted in 2024 and runs monthly.

See the [https://events.indieweb.org/tag/edinburgh #Edinburgh IndieWeb events page] for a list of upcoming events in the city.
