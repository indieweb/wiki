'''<dfn>[https://elgg.org/ Elgg]</dfn>''' is a open source social networking engine that is IndieWeb [[friendly]]. IndieWeb features can be added with the [https://github.com/RiverVanRain/indieweb indieweb plugin].

The Elgg project was started in 2004 by {{benwerd}} and [https://twitter.com/davetosh Dave Tosh]. It focuses on educational environments, but can be used by any organization/group.
