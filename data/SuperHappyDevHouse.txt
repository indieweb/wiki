'''<dfn>[http://superhappydevhouse.org/ SuperHappyDevHouse]</dfn>''' (often abbreviated as <abbr>SHDH</abbr>) is an irregularly scheduled serial gathering of hackers, developers, tinkerers that started in 2005 inspired by SuperHappyFunHouse and LAN parties. It itself was one of the inspirations for [[BarCamp]] later that same year, the structure of which was used for the first IndieWebCamp in [[2011]].
