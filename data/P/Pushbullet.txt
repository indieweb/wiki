'''<dfn>[https://www.pushbullet.com Pushbullet]</dfn>''' is a free service that sends rich notifications to your Android or iOS device, as well as various web browsers.

The service is currently free for all users and developers and offers an [https://docs.pushbullet.com/ API] for development. Premium services are planned for the future.
