'''<dfn>Paris Web</dfn>''' is an annual cycle of conferences and workshops spread over three days, in Paris, France.

The topics covered are around the design, implementation and maintenance of web projects.

The conferences are aimed at an audience of web professionals: technicians, creative people, decision-makers, agency directors, seniors or juniors.

Paris-Web's objective being to promote good practices among all decision-making levels, the topics covered are distributed over each day in a sufficiently diversified way to maximize the levels of approach and allow each participant to make the most of his or her visit.
