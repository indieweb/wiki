'''<dfn>Peach</dfn>''' ([https://web.archive.org/web/20221222120606/http://peach.cool/ archived] <nowiki>peach.cool</nowiki>) was a social network app that allowed you to share [[notes]], [[photos]], [[GIF]]s, [[chat]] with friends, and several other things by way of magic words ([https://web.archive.org/web/20230108131301/http://peach.cool/support archived documentation]). It was only available via the iOS and Android apps, without a web interface, but people reverse-engineered API access: [https://web.archive.org/web/20190406060723/https://gist.github.com/ummjackson/4db1da44c509576c1d1b archived gist]
