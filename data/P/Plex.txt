'''<dfn>[https://www.plex.tv Plex]</dfn>''' is a self hosted server for indexing, converting, playing, sharing, and streaming your media from local or cloud storage. It has a companion app for consuming media and can be accessed via a [[web browser]].

== Integrations ==
* {{cleverdevil}} has a [[Known]] plugin called [https://github.com/cleverdevil/Known-Watching Known-Watching] that includes a Plex webhook.
