'''<dfn>PDS</dfn>''' is an abbreviation for <dfn>Personal Data Server</dfn>, a part of federation in [[Bluesky]]'s [[AT Protocol]] network; [[Bridgy Fed]] acts as a PDS. Personal Data Servers store account information and posts. For more information see:
* https://bsky.social/about/blog/5-5-2023-federation-architecture

https://blue.mackuba.eu/directory/pdses is a directory of third-party PDSes not run by the Bluesky team.

== Examples ==
* [[Bluesky]] runs a PDS
* [[Bridgy Fed]] acts as a PDS
* [https://selfhosted.social/ selfhosted.social] runs an open PDS ([https://bsky.app/profile/selfhosted.social/post/3m742iefj7c27 since 2025-12-03])
* ...
