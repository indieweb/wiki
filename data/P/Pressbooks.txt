'''<dfn>[https://github.com/pressbooks/pressbooks Pressbooks]</dfn>''' is a [[book]] [[content management system]] which produces multiple formats: ebooks, webbooks, print-ready PDF, and various XML flavours. Pressbooks is built on top of [[WordPress]] Multisite.

The open source version of the plugin is available on [[GitHub]] at https://github.com/pressbooks/pressbooks.

Various hosts will maintain and manage versions or even subsets of the service including:
* https://pressbooks.com/
* https://reclaimhosting.com/pressbooks/
