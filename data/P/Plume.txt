'''<dfn>[https://joinplu.me Plume]</dfn>''' is an open source blogging tool that has some [[microformats2]] support and is based on [[ActivityPub]]. Plume users and blogs can be followed by other ActivityPub software, though the display will differ depending on the respective project's focus. [https://indieweb.org/pleroma Pleroma] displays the title, summary, and content, which may trigger its collapsed view for longer content. The post contains a link to the post's permalink and the author's profile page. In [[Mastodon]], the post summary is used as a CW with the title and permalink hidden under the fold. There is an open [https://github.com/tootsuite/mastodon/issues/8609 issue] to improve the display within Mastodon.
