'''<dfn>[https://prosemirror.net/ ProseMirror]</dfn>''' is an open source toolkit for writing rich-text [[editor|editors]] on the web that stores data as [[JSON]] objects instead of markup.


The [[project]] is maintained on [https://github.com/prosemirror/ GitHub].
