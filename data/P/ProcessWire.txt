<dfn>'''[http://processwire.com/ ProcessWire]'''</dfn> is an open source [[PHP]] content management system/content management framework.

== Plugins ==
=== ProcessWire Webmention ===
'''[http://modules.processwire.com/modules/webmention/ ProcessWire Webmention]''' adds webmention support to your ProcessWire site. Includes support for [[Vouch]] extension and an admin interface to view, process, delete, or change the visibility of received webmentions. It can also monitor a blogroll or whitelist and add new links it finds to the list of approved vouch domains. {{gRegor}} maintains and uses this plugin on gregorlove.com since 2015-07-12.
* v1.1.3 released 2017-03-27
* v1.1.2 released 2016-04-11
* v1.1.1 released 2016-04-10
* v1.1.0 released 2016-02-25 in the ProcessWire Modules Directory.
* Issues, pull requests: https://github.com/gRegorLove/ProcessWire-Webmention

=== ProcessWire IndieAuth ===
'''[https://github.com/gRegorLove/ProcessWire-IndieAuth ProcessWire IndieAuth]''' allows users to sign into your site using [[IndieAuth]]. {{gRegor}} maintains and uses this plugin on gregorlove.com since 2016-03-17.
