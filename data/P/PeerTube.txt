'''<dfn>[https://joinpeertube.org/ PeerTube]</dfn>''' is a free, decentralized, and federated video platform that supports both [[streaming]] and [[live streaming]].

* Open source: https://github.com/Chocobozzz/PeerTube

== Features ==
* https://github.com/novage/p2p-media-loader
* [[ActivityPub]]
* [[Mastodon]]
* formerly supported [https://github.com/webtorrent/webtorrent webtorrent], [https://github.com/Chocobozzz/PeerTube/issues/5465 removed in 2023]
