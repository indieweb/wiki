'''<dfn>[http://picturelife.com Picturelife]</dfn>''' was a photo hosting service that shut down 2016-08-22[https://www.theverge.com/2016/8/22/12587656/picturelife-shutdown-streamnation-jonathan-benassaya]. In July 2016, Reply All reported that users' photos had been offline for three months, but they were working to recover them. In August 2016, [[SmugMug]] worked with Picturelife to rescue photos and make them available again.[https://news.smugmug.com/smugmug-rescues-nearly-200-million-priceless-memories-from-picturelife-74a0f7b65dff] As of 2017-07-13, the SmugMug offer to Picturelife customers has ended.[https://www.smugmug.com/picturelife]
