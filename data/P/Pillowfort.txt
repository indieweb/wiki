'''<dfn>[https://www.pillowfort.social/ Pillowfort]</dfn>''' is a [[business-models|user-funded]] social media [[silo]] that supports [[blog|blogging]], [[comments]], and [[communities|community discussions]] with a [[chronological feed]]. It combines features from [[Tumblr]] and [[LiveJournal]], such as [[repost|reblogs]] and [[private posts]],<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20250409154827/https://www.dailydot.com/parsec/pillowfort-kickstarter/ DailyDot: Pillowfort wants to revive the art of blogging]</ref> while distinguishing itself by allowing LGBT and NSFW posts.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20250408235110/https://www.dailydot.com/debug/pillowfort-tumblr/ DailyDot: What is Pillowfort, and how is it different from Tumblr? ]</ref><ref>[https://www.fastcompany.com/90358305/six-months-after-tumblrs-nsfw-ban-these-kink-communities-are-coming-out-on-top Fast Company: After Tumblr’s NSFW ban, these adult communities have come out on top]</ref>

Pillowfort was crowdfunded into existence in 2016<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20160708083601/https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/pillowfort-io-beta-launch/#/ Indiegogo - Pillowfort Beta Launch]</ref> and has been supported with additional fundraisers in 2019<ref>[https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/562544287/pillowfortio-a-better-social-media-platform Kickstarter - Pillowfort]</ref> and 2023.<ref>[https://www.pillowfort.social/posts/4121787 End of Year Fundraiser Update]</ref> The site launched its options for paid premium features in April 2023.<ref>[https://www.pillowfort.social/posts/3286912 Pillowfort Premium Launches April 17, 2023]</ref><ref>[https://www.pillowfort.social/posts/3292044 Site Update - 4/17/2023]</ref>

The site started out on the [[.io]] [[top-level_domain|domain]] but moved to .social in 2019.<ref>[https://www.pillowfort.social/posts/601852 April 2019 Domain Move FAQ]</ref>
