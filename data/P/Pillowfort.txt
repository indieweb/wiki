'''<dfn>[https://www.pillowfort.social/ Pillowfort]</dfn>''' is a social media [[silo]] that supports blogging and community discussions.

Crowdfunded into existence in 2016, [https://www.pillowfort.social/ Pillowfort] “aims to be a hybrid of Tumblr, Twitter, and LiveJournal–keeping the strengths of these sites while compensating for their weaknesses.”
