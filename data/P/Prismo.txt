'''<dfn>[https://gitlab.com/mbajur/prismo Prismo]</dfn>''' is an in development, open source, link sharing tool. It supports partial [[ActivityPub]] federation and [[microformats2]]. Prismo users can be followed by Prismo users and by other ActivityPub software users. Federation between different Prismo instances is still in development.
