'''<dfn>[https://pine.blog Pine.blog]</dfn>''' was a [[reader]] and blog hosting service that supports [[Webmention]], and was [https://brianschrader.com/archive/pine-blog-deprecation/ shut down as of September 2025].

The service was run by [https://brianschrader.com/ Brian Schrader] who was [[self-dogfooding]] the service with an account at [https://web.archive.org/web/20240214092654/https://pine.blog/u/sonicrocketman| https://pine.blog/u/sonicrocketman] ''(archived)''

== Features == 
Pine.blog had a list of [https://web.archive.org/web/20240214092654/https://pine.blog/in-depth features] and a [https://pine.blog/kb/roadmapproduct roadmap].
