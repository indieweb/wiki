'''<dfn>[https://padd.crowdersoup.com/ PADD]</dfn>''' is an IndieWeb-enabled [[Microsub]] [[reader]] / client built by {{crowdersoup}}. Login is gated by [[IndieAuth]]. The design is heavily influenced by Star Trek.

Source Code:
* ''coming soon!''

== Features ==

* Login with IndieAuth
* Manage your Channels / Feeds
* Add new Feeds
* Remove Feeds
* Add new Channels
* Remove Channels
* Remove a post from a feed
* Like, Repost, or Reply to a post (makes a [[Micropub]] request to post interactions directly to your own site!)
* New Post editor: capable of creating article, note, photo, or check-in post types.
