The '''<dfn>[http://webwewant.org/ Web We Want Festival]</dfn>''' is a 2014 conference in London (official hashtag: #webwewantfestival) about improving the state of the Web. <blockquote>We focus on using innovative approaches to build support for national and regional campaigns to create a world where everyone, everywhere is online and able to participate in a free flow of knowledge, ideas, collaboration and creativity over the open Web."  We’ve set up and funded projects and campaigns around the world. And we’re just getting started. You could help us make a difference.</blockquote>
