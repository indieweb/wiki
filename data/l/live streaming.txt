<dfn>'''live streaming'''</dfn> is the practice of [[streaming]] media (mostly [[audio]] or [[video]]) live as the captured event is happening.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* [[2020/Austin|IndieWebCamp Austin 2020]] was livestreamed to indieweb.org
* [[User:WaterPigs.co.uk|Barnaby Walters]] posts audio live streams on his website since ???, as demoed at [[2016/Nuremberg/Demos#Barnaby_Walters|IndieWebCamp Nuremberg 2016]].
* {{TheGillies}} has streamed a feed of his living room at http://static.tyler.cat/live
* [https://kmikeym.com/ kmikeym] occasionally streams while working, and [https://news.kmikeym.com/k5m-con-2020/ streamed a conference in 2020], all streamed to https://live.kmikeym.com

== Silo Examples ==
* [[YouTube]] has a live streaming feature called "YouTube Live". It has been used to stream IndieWebCamps
