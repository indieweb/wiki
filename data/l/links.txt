'''<dfn>links</dfn>''' is a top level [[page]] on many indieweb sites that can be a [[bookmarks]] page, a [[link in bio|bio links]] page, or a [[linkblog]] page. It may also be considered an IndieWeb method for replacing [[Linktree]] or other such services.
