'''<dfn>[https://libre.fm/ libre.fm]</dfn>''' is a free and open source creative workspace that is IndieWeb [[friendly]]. Previously (2009-present) it was also a [[scrobbling]] service but as of May 2025 it  ''[https://github.com/foocorp/hacienda/discussions/117 that part is now firmly in maintenance mode]''

In response to the AI-slop enshittificiation of the web, Libre.fm is pivoting to a private creative workspace, requiring a login to access the majority of published content. See https://1800www.com

== IndieWeb Friendly ==
Libre.fm supports several IndieWeb building blocks, e.g. on profiles:
* [[rel-me]]
* [[h-card]] — e.g. https://indiewebify.me/validate-h-card/?url=https%3A%2F%2Flibre.fm%2Fuser%2Fmattl
