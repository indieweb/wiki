A '''<dfn>linkblog</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>linklog</dfn>) is a [[blog]] dedicated to posting links ([[bookmark]] posts) to other sites, with perhaps minimal commentary, tags, or sometimes [[quote]]d excerpts.

<dfn>linkblogging</dfn> is the practice of posting to a linkblog.
