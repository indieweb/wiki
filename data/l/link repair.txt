'''<dfn>link repair</dfn>''' is the practice of repairing broken hyperlinks on your personal site, especially on older posts where the links were valid as of the time of the post but have stopped working since.

== Why ==
{{section-stub}}
Repair your links on old posts so when people find them, they still have a good experience (what you intended when you posted it) when they click on links in your posts.
