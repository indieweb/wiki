'''<dfn>Overland</dfn>''' is an [[iOS]] app for location tracking that can send its data to an custom server. {{aaronpk}} uses Overland to send his location to his website to do things like automatically set the timezone of posts, track bike rides and train trips, display the local weather, and more.
