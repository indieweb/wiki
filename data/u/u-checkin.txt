'''<dfn>u-checkin</dfn>''' is a draft [[h-entry]] property for indicating a [[checkin]] [[venue]] for a post, usually with an embedded h-card for the venue, with at least a URL to a venue, which may have a more extensive h-card there. A simple p-checkin with name of venue may also be used.
