    '''<dfn>[https://uberspace.de/ uberspace]</dfn>''' is an advanced shared hosting provider from Germany with pay-as-much-as-you-want plans.


== good things ==
* advanced hosting options for various programming languages and databases and custom services like Jabber (everything that can work on random nonstandard ports)
* HTTPS for every domain, [https://manual.uberspace.de/web-https.htm HTTPS only]
* No [https://manual.uberspace.de/web-logs.html webserver logs by default]
* Changelog for [https://manual.uberspace.de/changelog.html new features]

== things to know ==
* website and support in [https://uberspace.de/de german] and [https://uberspace.de/en english], [https://manual.uberspace.de documentation] in english
* don't provide domains -> has to be bought and managed externally
