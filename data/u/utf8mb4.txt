'''<dfn>utf8mb4</dfn>''' is the MySQL character encoding which includes full Unicode coverage, including emoji symbols. The MySQL character encoding <code>utf8</code> only supports one-to-three byte Unicode symbols; four-byte symbols are not supported. [https://mathiasbynens.be/notes/mysql-utf8mb4]
