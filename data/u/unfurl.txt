'''<dfn>unfurl</dfn>''' is a [[jargon]] verb usually applied to URLs, meaning to retrieve (or create or both) some minimum amount of information from or about the contents of a URL, for the purpose of displaying a preview (like a [[link-preview]]) or context (like a [[reply-context]]).
