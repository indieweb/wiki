== IndieWeb wiki username ==
Your IndieWeb wiki username is the [[web address]] you used to sign-into the wiki using [[IndieLogin]].

How do you change your IndieWeb wiki username? 

You don't because it is directly tied to the web address ([[domain name]]) you used to login.

== IndieWeb chat username ==
On various IndieWeb [[discuss]]ion services, you might be using a short name other than your web address / domain name.

You should add this short chat name to:
* '''[[chat-names]]'''
and link an icon so your chats show up with your icon in the chat archives.
