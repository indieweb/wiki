http://indiewebcamp.com/mentions

“Mentions” may refer to:
* Webmentions, [[notifications]] of linking sent using the [[Webmention]] protocol
* [[mention]] posts, a type of response created implicitly by linking

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} shows a list of mentions of his posts and home page since 2013-10-12 at
* http://aaronparecki.com/mentions

=== Pelle Wessman ===
{{voxpelli}} shows a list of mentions of his posts and homepage since 2015-07-29 at:
* http://voxpelli.com/mentions/

=== Amy Guy ===
{{rhiaro}} shows a list of mentions of her posts (and homepage?) since 2015-08-13 at:
* http://rhiaro.co.uk/mentions

=== ... add yourself if you have a mentions page ===

== Silo Examples ==
=== Twitter ===
[[Twitter]] shows a list of recent mentions for the currently logged in user here:
* https://twitter.com/mentions?filter=all
* https://twitter.com/mentions?filter=following - much more useful
