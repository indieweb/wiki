A '''<dfn>mentions feed</dfn>''' is a page that lists webmentions sent to the website.

== Why ==
A mentions feed could be used to send [[notifications]] to a mobile app such as [[mention-app]]. 

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* http://aaronparecki.com/mentions
* https://waterpigs.co.uk/mentions/
* https://kylewm.com/mentions
* http://pfefferle.github.io/openwebicons/contact/
