The '''<dfn>hamburger icon</dfn>''' is a triple bar symbol (≡) commonly used to indicate a hidden navigation menu. Google has referred to this icon as "hotdog."

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Chat archives ===
The IndieWeb [[discuss]]ion chat archives use a hamburger menu in the top left for narrower windows (including "typical" mobile web views).
* https://chat.indieweb.org/
