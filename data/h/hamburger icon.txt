The '''<dfn>hamburger icon</dfn>''' is a triple bar symbol commonly used to indicate a hidden navigation menu. Google has referred to this icon as "hotdog." [http://flatfrogblog.com/2014/01/27/ui-icons-that-drive-me-crazy/]
It was first used on the [http://blog.placeit.net/history-of-the-hamburger-icon/ Xerox Star in the early 1980s]
