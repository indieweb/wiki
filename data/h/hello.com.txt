'''<dfn>[http://hello.com hello.com]</dfn>''' is an app-based social silo founded by Orkut Buyukkokten ([[Orkut]]) and a small group of ex-Google engineers.

== History ==
''More precise dates and citations for this section are welcomed.''
* 2000-2001: hello.com (idealab) offered downloadable software "hello freephone" for making calls to other hello users. [http://web.archive.org/web/20000817022555/http://www.hello.com/]
* 2003: hello from [[Picasa]] (Lifescape Solutions, Inc) offered downloadable software for peer-to-peer sharing photos with friends and family, including chat functionality. [http://web.archive.org/web/20030803064902/http://www.hello.com/]
* 2004-07-13: [[Google]] acquires Picasa. [http://googlepress.blogspot.com/2004/07/google-acquires-picasa.html]
* 2009-06-11: Google shuts down hello from Picasa, encouraging the use of other Google sharing products Picasa, Picasa Web Albums, and Google Talk. [http://web.archive.org/web/20090118064137/http://hello.com/]
* 2014: hello (Hello Network, Inc.) re-appeared as early as April 2014 in its current form, per Internet Archive. [http://web.archive.org/web/20140425075022/http://hello.com/]
