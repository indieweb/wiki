'''<dfn>hydrate</dfn>''' is web developer [[jargon]] for the process of using [[JS]] to add various elements to the DOM of a page such as additional images, navigation, or other UI elements; in the context of the IndieWeb, some sites use such [[JS]] to add the display of [[comments]], or other such secondary or third-party content.
