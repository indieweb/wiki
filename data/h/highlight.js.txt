<strong><dfn>[https://github.com/capjamesg/highlight.js highlight.js]</dfn></strong> is a script made by {{capjamesg}} that lets you interactively highlight different pieces of text on a web page.

== Use the Extension ==

* [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-GB/firefox/addon/highlight-js/ Firefox]

== IndieWeb Examples ==

=== capjamesg ===

{{capjamesg}} uses highlight.js on his personal blog and wiki.

Examples:

* https://jamesg.blog/2022/12/01/indieweb-chatgpt/#w=t,#text=ChatGPT%20what%20coffee%20is
* https://wiki.jamesg.blog/index.php?title=TV_Shows#w=t,The%20Simpsons%20Season%2033%20Finale,#text=Seinfeld,#text=Coronation%20Street,#text=Silicon%20Valley
