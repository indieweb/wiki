The '''<dfn>[[2022/Düsseldorf|IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2022]] schedule</dfn>''' is where you will find day 1’s session grid and day 2’s schedule. The session grid will contain links to notes and videos from all sessions.

The session grid is developed on day 1, and is based on suggestions by the attendees.

All times are <strong>local</strong> times. If you are a remote attendee, make sure to [https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html?iso=20220430T070000&p1=79 convert to your timezone].

== Saturday ==
Day 1: Share, Learn, and Discuss!

Session topics will be proposed and chosen on the day of the event and organized into a grid.

Sessions are facilitated by the participant who proposed the topic. Learn more at [[session facilitating]].
