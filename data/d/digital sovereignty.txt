'''<dfn>digital sovereignty</dfn>''' is in the context of the indieweb a reference to having control over your digital existence, especially online, such as your [[identity]] and [[own your data|data]], and in a broader context also known as <dfn>network sovereignty</dfn>, referring to nation-states asserting governance and control over their networks and Internet traffic at their borders.
