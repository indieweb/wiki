== Silo examples ==

[[Blogger]] has a field titled "description" under the title of the blog, as does [[Tumblr]]; [[Wordpress]] calls it "tagline".

In the html, tumblr uses <code> id="description"</code>, Wordpress uses <code>class="site-description"</code> and Blogger uses <code>class="description"</code>

This maps to the [https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc4287#section-4.2.12 Atom subtitle element] and the [http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/rss/rss.html RSS channel:description].
