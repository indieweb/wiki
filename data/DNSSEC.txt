DNSSEC is a security extension to [[DNS]] to allow verification and authentication of DNS records and domain names to prevent tampering with domain records and DNS-based man-in-the-middle attacks.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
* {{myfreeweb}} has DNSSEC signing working for https://unrelenting.technology using the [https://www.knot-dns.cz Knot DNS server] and Google Domains as the registrar
