'''<dfn>Blaugust</dfn>''' is a community led [[blogging challenge]] to post 31 times during the month of [[August]] (optionally using the eponymous hashtag #blaugust), described in [https://nerdgirlthoughts.game.blog/2025/07/10/blaugust-2025-is-coming/ Blaugust 2025 is Coming!] and has awards & badges you can earn by participating.
