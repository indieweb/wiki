'''<dfn>Bridgetown</dfn>'''  is an open source static site generator for modern web development built with [[Ruby]]. It is the spiritual successor to [[Jekyll]].

The source code for Bridgetown is available [https://github.com/bridgetownrb/bridgetown on GitHub] and is licensed under the [https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT MIT License].
