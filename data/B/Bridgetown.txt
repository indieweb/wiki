'''<dfn>Bridgetown</dfn>''' is an open source [[static site generator]] for modern web development and written in [[Ruby]]. [https://www.bridgetownrb.com Bridgetown] is the spiritual successor to [[Jekyll]]. It is maintained by [https://jaredwhite.com Jared White]. 

The source code for Bridgetown is available [https://github.com/bridgetownrb/bridgetown on GitHub] and is licensed under the [https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT MIT License].
