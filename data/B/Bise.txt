'''<dfn>[https://github.com/jmacdotorg/bise Bise]</dfn>''' is a webserver log analytics script written in [[Perl]]. It offers a simpler view than the other big stats packages.  

See [https://fogknife.com/2018-01-03-announcing-bise-a-blog-readership-reporter.html Announcing Bise, a blog-readership reporter].

== Features ==
Bise will search logs for whatever targets you specify and return the results as either a human-readable table or as JSON. It offers searches for "path", "path_regex", "referer_regex", or "agent_regex".
