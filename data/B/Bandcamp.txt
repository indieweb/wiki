'''<dfn>[http://bandcamp.com Bandcamp]</dfn>''' is a [[silo]] for artists and [[musician]]s to promote their music, including streaming and customizable pricing for purchasing music.

There is a modicum of data export possible via JSON-LD embedded into the HTML, although the data quality leaves much to be desired.
