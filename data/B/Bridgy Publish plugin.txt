'''<dfn>[https://wordpress.org/plugins/bridgy-publish/ Bridgy Publish plugin]</dfn>''' is an open source [[WordPress]] plugin that adds [[Bridgy]] Publish functionality to your WordPress install.

Development of the plugin [https://github.com/dshanske/bridgy-publish occurs on GitHub].

As of [https://github.com/dshanske/bridgy-publish/issues/56#issuecomment-368283029 2018-02-24], the plugin supports [[POSSE]] of posts from [[WordPress]] to [[GitHub]].
