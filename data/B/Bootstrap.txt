'''<dfn><a href="http://getbootstrap.com">Bootstrap</a></dfn>''' is a large collection of boilerplate HTML and CSS styles and components originally developed by Twitter. It is a very fast way to create a basically good-looking and mobile-friendly site, though it can be a challenge when doing lots of detailed or custom styling.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

* {{aaronpk}} uses Bootstrap in many of his projects including [[IndieAuth.com]], [[webmention.io]], [[Quill]], [[Teacup]], and [[Monocle]].
* [[Known]] uses Bootstrap, and is currently in the process of moving to Bootstrap 3 (as of 2015-05).
* {{snarfed}}'s [[Bridgy]] uses a style based on Bootstrap.
* {{kylewm}} used Bootstrap as a quick way to get up-and-running with his [[Feverdream]] prototype.
