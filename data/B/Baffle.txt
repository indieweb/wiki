[https://github.com/snarfed/baffle '''<dfn>Baffle</dfn>'''] was a bridge that made traditional feed readers like [[NewsBlur]] and [[Feedly]] support [[Microsub]]. It [https://snarfed.org/2018-07-16_introducing-baffle launched on 2018-07-16] and was turned down on 2018-07-04 due to lack of use.
