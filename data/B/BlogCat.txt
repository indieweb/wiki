'''<dfn>[https://blogcat.org BlogCat]</dfn>''' is a Firefox browser add-on that lets you manage and read [[feed]]s as well post to your site using [[Micropub]], or post to [[Bluesky]] and [[Mastodon]]. The documentation indicates it was inspired in part by [[Fraidycat]].

* Open source: https://github.com/soapdog/webextension-blogcat
