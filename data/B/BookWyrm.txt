'''<dfn>[https://bookwyrm.social/ BookWyrm]</dfn>''' is a social [[personal library|library]] platform and open source software built on [[ActivityPub]]. The source code is available at https://github.com/bookwyrm-social/bookwyrm.

The project was previously known as [https://github.com/mouse-reeve/fedireads FediReads] - an [[ActivityPub]] based [[Goodreads]] like service with https://openlibrary.org/; [https://fedireads-test.glitch.me/ Demo prviously available on Glitch]; (see also [https://web.archive.org/web/20200315235453/https://twitter.com/tripofmice/status/1230235138341998592 https://twitter.com/tripofmice/status/1230235138341998592])
