'''<dfn>[https://blogmesh.org/ Blogmesh]</dfn>''' is a proposal and prototype [[WordPress]] plugin to connect existing blogs in a way that resembles other [[social network]]s, using standards like [[RSS]] to follow your friends and create a timeline view of their posts.

The [[project]] is being developed on [https://github.com/roytanck/wp-blogmesh GitHub].
