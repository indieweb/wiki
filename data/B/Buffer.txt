'''<dfn>[http://bufferapp.com/ Buffer]</dfn>''' is a tool for [[scheduling]] the publishing of [[posts]] to [[silos]].

When creating a post, Buffer provides the option to syndicate the post to one or more services. The destination is indicated by a composite icon consisting of a large version of the profile photo of the account, with a small icon of the service overlaid.
