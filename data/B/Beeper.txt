'''<dfn>Beeper</dfn>''' is an open-source "universal chat app". It uses [[Matrix]] to bridge to a variety of private chats, including, for example, [[Instagram]], [[Discord]], [[WhatsApp]], [[Slack]], [[IRC]] and more. 

According to the Beeper FAQ, the team "hate(s) private, [[silo|siloed]] chat networks and do(es) not want to contribute to the problem."
