'''<dfn>IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2026</dfn>''', the 10th IndieWebCamp in [[Düsseldorf]] 🥳, takes place April 25-26, 2026, the weekend before [https://beyondtellerrand.com/events/dusseldorf-2026 beyond tellerrand conference Düsseldorf 2026].

Add the event to your calendar!
* [https://events.indieweb.org/2026/04/-indiewebcamp-d%C3%BCsseldorf-ewHCZehNA3gg '''🗓 IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf April 25 - 26, 2026'''], from '''10AM to 5PM'''.

🎪 '''IndieWebCamp''' is a two-day collaborative workshop with interactive discussions, brainstorming, creating, hacking, and teaching & helping each other make more and better use of our personal websites for a more fulfilling experience on the web.

1️⃣ '''Day one, 25 April''' we’ll do introductions and facilitate discussions in a [[BarCamp]] like environment. Bring a topic you'd like to discuss or join on topics as they are added to the schedule grid.

2️⃣ '''Day two, 26 April''' is our [[Create Day]] when we make, design, build, and code what we discussed. Work in groups with others or on your own. We do demos at the end of the day for everyone to show off what they worked on and made!

See '''[[IndieWebCamps/Attending|Attending an IndieWebCamp]]''' for more details if this is your first time!

== Health and Safety ==
The venue has plenty of space to sit distanced, and windows which we can and will open to provide fresh airflow.

If you are feeling sick or experiencing any respiratory (Covid, RSV, flu, cold) symptoms, we require that you please participate remotely instead of in-person.
{{emojicon|😷}}
Masking 😷 recommended (but not required) both in-person inside the venue, and in transit (airports, flights, trains) to the venue.

Plan to bring N95 (or equivalent) respirator or KN95 (or equivalent like FFP2) masks to wear.

Neither the venue nor the subsequent beyond tellerrand conference has any explicit health and safety rules, however we are recommending masking to help mitigate risks, both for IndieWebCamp participants, and for those we may interact with at beyond tellerrand.

'''Updates:''' similar to the [[2025/Düsseldorf|IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2025]] Health and Safety policy, this policy is a living policy, and may receive clarifying edits between now and the IndieWebCamp. However, we will not remove or downgrade any aspect of this policy between now and IndieWebCamp Düsseldorf 2026.

== Directions ==

'''⚠️ Important ⚠️:''' Do note that [https://www.uniper-duesseldorfmarathon.de/ Düsseldorf Marathon] is happening on '''Sunday''' and most public transport (and car) routes above the ground will be closed off! On Sunday you should therefore stick to the underground lines, called "U-Bahn", whose line numbers start with a '''"U"''' (e.g. '''U78 or U79'''). The closest underground stop is '''ERGO-Platz/Klever Str. U'''.

* public transportation cost: ~€3.80 for a one way adult subway/tram/bus.
** easiest and cheapest way to get around in Düsseldorf and the wider surroundings is to register for and use the [https://www.rheinbahn.com/eezy Eezy.NRW system in the Rheinbahn app] to check in and out. If you you forget to check out it will do so for you after a certain time. 
** if you prefer to buy a ticket, always pick fare category A. That allows you to travel to any destination within the city.

* 🚊 / 🚌 public transportation '''stop close by'''
** subway stop '''ERGO-Platz/Klever Str. U'''

* 🚊 Directions '''coming from Holiday Inn/NIU Tab hotels'''
** cross the IHZ park adjacent to the hotels to get to '''Oberbilker Markt U''' (650 meters, 9 mins. walk)
** [https://maps.app.goo.gl/7un5SauhEmdBPSBY6 go to platform 1 ("Steig 1") and get into subway U79 to '''Duisburg Duissern U-Bf''']
** get off at stop '''ERGO-Platz/Klever Str. U''' (5th stop, 8 mins. ride)
** use exit "Cordobastraße"
** from there it's a 300 meters to the venue (5 mins. walk, see photos)

* 🚊 Directions '''coming from Düsseldorf main station''' (aka Düsseldorf Hbf) 
** Inside Düsseldorf main station, find the stairs leading down to the underground ('''"U-Bahn"''')
** Go to '''platform 1''' ("Steig 1") and get into either subway [https://www.google.com/maps/dir/D%C3%BCsseldorf+Hauptbahnhof,+Konrad-Adenauer-Platz+14,+40210+D%C3%BCsseldorf/D-ERGO-Platz%2FKlever+Str.+U,+40477+D%C3%BCsseldorf/@51.2295943,6.7647465,14z/am=t/data=!4m14!4m13!1m5!1m1!1s0x47b8ca30fdd4112f:0xc3dc51f56b427a12!2m2!1d6.7927281!2d51.220115!1m5!1m1!1s0x47b8c9f451f59879:0xb2f2503a6acea1bd!2m2!1d6.7753963!2d51.2381401!3e3?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI2MDQyMC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D '''U78''' to '''D-MERKUR SPIEL-ARENA/Messe Nord''' or '''U79''' to '''Duisburg Duissern U-Bf''']
** get off at stop '''ERGO-Platz/Klever Str. U''' (4th stop, 6 mins. ride)
** use exit "Cordobastraße"
** from there it's a 300 meters to the venue (5 mins. walk, see photos)

=== Coming by Car ===
* The venue does not offer parking spots
* 400 meters away there is [https://maps.app.goo.gl/chHegDBMHacgQtPB6 a public parking] that costs €3 per hour and is free on Sundays
* There is a [https://maps.app.goo.gl/UWjZ768oqpSVKxkG8 good and free P+R (Park & Ride) place in the South of Düsseldorf] to drop off the car and switch into the underground line (which, at that point, is going over ground).

⚠️ Remember that Düsseldorf Marathon happens on Sunday and it might be hard, if not impossible, to get close to the venue.
