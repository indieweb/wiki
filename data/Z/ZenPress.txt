'''<dfn>[https://github.com/pfefferle/ZenPress ZenPress]</dfn>''' is a clean [[WordPress]] theme inspired by [[Medium|Medium.com]]. It is SEO optimized, accessibility ready and highly semantic. The theme is made with HTML5 (POSH) and flavoured with [[Microformats|Microformats(2)]] and [[Microdata]]/Schema.org.

It is based on the [[SemPress]] theme code.
