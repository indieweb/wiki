'''<dfn>[https://www.getzola.org/ Zola]</dfn>''' is a [[static site generator]] written in [[Rust]] that has been used by some community members on their personal site. It uses the [https://tera.netlify.app/ Tera] template engine which is very similar to [[Jinja]]. Content files are written in [[CommonMark]] ([[markdown]]) with [[TOML]] for [[frontmatter]].
