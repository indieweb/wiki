'''<dfn>[https://www.getzola.org/ Zola]</dfn>''' is a [[static site generator]] written in Rust. It uses the [https://tera.netlify.app/ Tera] template engine which is very similar to [[Jinja]]. Content files are written in [[CommonMark]] ([[markdown]]) with [[TOML]] for [[frontmatter]].

== IndieWeb Examples ==
IndieWeb community members using Zola on their own primary sites:
