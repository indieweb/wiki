ZipperHost is a hosting company run by a member of the IndieWeb community.

----

ZipperHost supports the IndieWeb movement and wants everyone to have access to affordable hosting options where they can own their content, whether that is their blog, email, app, or other content.

ZipperHost is run by [[User:jeannieindigital.com]], a member of the community and supporter of online identity ownership.  She [[selfdogfood]]s the services she offers in partnership with the providers she has used for years.

ZipperHost plans and services can be found at [http://zipperhost.com zipperhost.com] and include an unlimited cPanel hosting plan for $5 per month.
