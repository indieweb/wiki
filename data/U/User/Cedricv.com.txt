== Cédric Vonlanthen ==

'''Cédric Vonlanthen''' is a strategic marketing and operational fluidity consultant, founder of Fluance Pro and Fluance. He helps entrepreneurs and independents regain strategic clarity and operational fluidity in their professional activity.

=== Websites ===
* '''[https://cedricv.com/ cedricv.com]''' — Fluance Pro: Individual coaching for entrepreneurs and independents. Bilingual site (French/English) with blog on entrepreneurship, business development, and simplicity in business.
* '''[https://fluance.io/ fluance.io]''' — Fluance: Movement and well-being approach based on movement, breath, and play.

=== Activity ===
Cédric accompanies heart-centered entrepreneurs to:
* Get out of stress, overload, and scattered focus
* (Re)find a business model aligned with their values and aspirations
* Identify and activate the smallest changes generating the greatest transformation
* Align strategic marketing with operational fluidity for serene and sustainable growth

Entrepreneur since 2012, he combines engineering rigor with intuition to help entrepreneurs redefine success through fluidity.
