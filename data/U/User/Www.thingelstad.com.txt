[https://www.thingelstad.com/about/ Jamie Thingelstad] is an experienced technology executive with a successful track record of product and platform innovation and technology leadership for large-scale, financial services, media, retail, and software-as-a-service technology companies. He is Senior Vice President, Chief Technology Officer of [https://www.spscommerce.com/ SPS Commerce] ([https://finance.google.com/finance?q=spsc NASDAQ: SPSC]), the leading provider of retail cloud services powering the largest retail business network connecting 90,000 companies in the retail industry to efficiently and quickly conduct business. Mr. Thingelstad has served on the Board of Directors for the [https://mntech.org/ Minnesota Technology Association], [https://minnestar.org/ Minnestar], and [https://www.caringbridge.org/ CaringBridge]. He is the recipient of numerous awards including Forty Under 40, Titans of Technology, and the 2021 ORBIE CIO of the Year award. He lives in Minneapolis, Minnesota with his wife, two children, and their dog.
