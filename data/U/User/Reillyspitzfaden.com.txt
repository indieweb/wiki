== Intro ==
I'm a composer, electronic musician, and programmer in Rochester, NY. I teach music technology and film/game scoring at SUNY Geneseo, and I like doing sound design with [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_(software) Max/MSP]. I recently started a [https://reillyspitzfaden.com/blog blog] where I talk about this sound design as well as IndieWeb topics and [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JUCE JUCE] audio plugin development.

I first learned about the IndieWeb in 2024 when I was reading about the [https://indieweb.org/Gemini_protocol Gemini] protocol and the [https://indieweb.org/small_web small web].
