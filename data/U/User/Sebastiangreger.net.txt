= <span class="p-name">Sebastian Greger</span> =

My site <span class="p-url">https://sebastiangreger.net</span> is running on WordPress, with IndieAuth and Microformats set up manually.

The site sends and accepts Webmentions using the [https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-webmention wordpress-webmention] and [https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-semantic-linkbacks semantic-linkbacks] plugins. The backfeed is served by [[brid.gy]].

I POSSE to Twitter at [http://twitter.com/sebastiangreger @sebastiangreger] and occasionally to LinkedIn, using my own [https://sebastiangreger.net/2014/03/audience-context-conscious-posse-syndication-plugin-wordpress/ audience/context-conscious POSSE plugin] (in development).
