An '''<dfn>accessibility statement</dfn>''' is a statement posted on a website that details the commitments to making content and physical spaces equally accessible to those with disabilities, including any known limitations and who to contact in case there are any problems or concerns.

== Why ==
Accessibility statements demonstrate your care and commitment to equal access. They also help alleviate the pressure on people with disabilities needing to inquire about accessibility.

In some situations, there may be legal requirements to provide accessibility statements and offer certain [[accessibility]] features.

== How ==
* The W3 Web Accessibility Initiative has a page on [https://www.w3.org/WAI/planning/statements/ Developing an Accessibility Statement] that includes a tool to generate a statement.
