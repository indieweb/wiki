'''Joshua P. Steele''' is a software engineer and blogger based in Ohio. His primary website is [https://joshuapsteele.com joshuapsteele.com].

== About ==
I'm a pastor-turned-programmer who solves problems with a heart for people and an eye for detail. I work mainly on backend development in Java, SQL, and Python for a cybersecurity company.

== IndieWeb Features ==

My site supports the following IndieWeb features:

=== Identity ===
* [[h-card]] on homepage with profile information
* [[rel-me]] links to verify distributed identity across social platforms
* [[IndieAuth]] support for signing in with my domain
* [[WebFinger]] for Fediverse discovery

=== Publishing ===
* [[h-entry]] markup on all blog posts
* Microformats2 for semantic HTML (p-name, dt-published, e-content, p-category, p-author)
* Full-content [[RSS]] and [[JSON Feed]]
* 300+ blog posts published

=== Social Features ===
* [[Webmention]] support via [https://webmention.io webmention.io]
* JavaScript-based webmention display (likes, reposts, replies, mentions)
* [[Bridgy]] integration for backfeeding social interactions from Bluesky
* Reply buttons (email, Bluesky, Micro.blog) on each post

=== POSSE ===
* Publish on [https://micro.blog Micro.blog] at [https://social.joshuapsteele.com social.joshuapsteele.com]
* Cross-post to [[Bluesky]] and [[Mastodon]] via Micro.blog
* Original posts remain on joshuapsteele.com
