Drew Michael McWeeney is a Ph.D. student and faculty member in the early childhood education department at both Central Connecticut State University and University of Hartford where he supervises student teachers and teaches various teacher education courses. He also works in both freelance web programming and music part-time.<br>
<br>
Mr. McWeeney is also emerging as a leader in the use of online (distance) learning. He leads online meetings with teachers across Connecticut to help them create, design, build, and launch their virtual learning classrooms. If you are a teacher looking for assistance, visit his website and contact him.<br>
