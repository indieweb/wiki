Hi! I'm <mark style="background-color:#ab80a5;border-radius:5px;padding:0 2px">Jo</mark> and you can find me attending [[HWC]] Europe, occasionally attending [[fresh]] and also in other IndieWeb online meetings when I have time (also you can find me in the [[discuss|chat]]). I've only been "self" hosting since about 2022 and had a few sites on [[NeoCities]] and various [[tildeverse|PubNIX]]es before that.

== My site :^) ==
My domain is dead.garden and it's called that because it '''sounds cool''' and is '''poetic or something'''.
I have a website but the domain is also available via the [[Gemini protocol|gemini]], [[gopher]] and [[finger]] protocol.
=== Contents ===
I have a blog but also show my [[watch]]ed films and [[books]] I'm reading; as well as an unprofessional [[portfolio]] and a [[photo]] feed. I also share [[poetry]].
=== Technologies ===

My site is static and hand written for the most part, though the blog is generated using a personally adapted version of the [[static site generator]] [[bashblog]]. I have [https://github.com/deathcomestotime/indieweb-bashblog forked this thing] to include stuff like [[webmention]] support (with [[webmention.io]] and [[webmention.js]]) and [[microformats]]. It can be much improved but I wanted to get it out there. 

At the moment my site is served by [[nginx]] and hosted on a [[VPS]] in Frankfurt. 

My gemini capsule runs on [https://github.com/michael-lazar/jetforce jetforce], my gopherhole uses [https://github.com/gophernicus/gophernicus Gophernicus] and my finger server is [https://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/noble/man8/efingerd.8.html efingerd].
