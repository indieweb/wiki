== Who is Channy? ==

'''[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Channy_Yun  Channy]''' works for [http://aws.amazon.com Amazon Web Services] as a Technology Evangelist in Korea and has promoted Open APIs & Web standards as a developer evangelist in Daum.net and leader of [http://webstandards.or.kr Web Standards Korea], Open-source software as a professor of Jeju National University and a lead of [http://forums.mozilla.or.kr Mozilla Korean Community].

He was chosen in one of [http://www.eweek.com/c/a/IT-Management/OSBC-Names-20-Most-Powerful-Open-Source-Voices-of-2010-758420 top 20 powerful voices of Open-source software] and known tech writer of his Channy's Blog with over 64K subscribers, 35k Twitter followers, 9k G+ follower and 5k Facebook fans. Also he helped developer's relationship as a founder of BarCamp Seoul, WebAppsCon, and DevOn.
