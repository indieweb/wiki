== fnhipster.com ==

https://fnhipster.com is a personal blog and stream of consciousness on technology, creativity, and a random series of events. It features critical takes on AI, thematic music playlists with narrative arcs, technical deep dives, and haikus that punctuate the noise.

The site is organized around three content vertices:
* '''Dreams''' — concepts, aspirations, speculative thoughts, and personal reflections
* '''Echoes''' —  reactions, responses, and ongoing dialogues
* '''Constructs''' — practical builds, projects, and technical implementations

It runs on Adobe Edge Delivery Services and Cloudflare Workers with a retro terminal aesthetic. Stick around long enough, and you might catch a live ASCII broadcast.
