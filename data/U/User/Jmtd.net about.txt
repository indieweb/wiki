[https://jmtd.net/about Jonathan Dowland] is a free-software developer, professional software engineer, PhD student (part-time) and Indieweb enthusiast from Newcastle, England. Jonathan's main contribution to IndieWeb is serving as a maintainer for [https://ikiwiki.info/ IkiWiki], a static-site/wiki generator.

Jonathan's interests in IndieWeb are to help to adapt IkiWiki towards better IndieWeb compliance, as well as influence IndieWeb's development based on his experience.
