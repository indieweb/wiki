After spending 5 years researching theoretical physics at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(https://www.ust.hk/home), Cheuk has transferred her analytical and logical skills in natural science and built a career in data science. Cheuk has been a Data Scientist in one of the biggest worldwide wholesalers in the travel business;  an AWS partnered consultancy which delivers machine learning model; a startup aiming to revolutionise revenue management with data science and; a global bank using machine learning to investigating financial crime. Now Cheuk is working in a team of developers building a revolutionary graph database(https://terminusdb.com/).

Cheuk constantly contributes to the community by giving AI and deep learning workshops and organize sprints(https://python-sprints.github.io/) for open source projects, at the same time contribute to open source projects including Pandas, Keras, Scikit-learn, Dateutil and maintaining her open-source library - PicknMix(https://picknmix.readthedocs.io/en/latest/). Cheuk has also been a guest speaker at Universities and various conferences. Believing in gender equality, Cheuk is currently a co-organizer of AI Club for Gender Minorities(https://www.meetup.com/ai-club/) to support Tech Diversity and Inclusion. This year, on top of speaking at conferences, Cheuk has been deeply involved in EuroPython as one of the organizers and master of lightning talks.
