<h1 style="margin:-2em 1em 1em;color:#FFF;border:0" class="p-title">Homebrew Website Club Meetup</h1>

'''[[Homebrew Website Club]] Meetup''': Are you building your own website? Personal publishing web app? Or some other digital magic, like an Indie feed reader? 

If so, come on by and join a gathering of likeminded people who share your interests. Bring friends who want to start their own personal web site. Exchange information, swap ideas, talk shop, help work on a project…

Join us for <span class="p-summary">an evening of quiet writing and IndieWeb demos and discussions!</span>
* Create or update your personal web site!
* Finish that blog post you’ve been writing!
* Edit the wiki!
* Demo recent IndieWeb breakthroughs, share what you’ve gotten working!

It’s a friendly space, with friendly people who welcome newcomers.

Any questions? Ask '''[[discuss#Join_Discussions|in the chatroom]]'''!

Here’s how it all began: [http://tantek.com/2013/332/b1/homebrew-website-club-newsletter Homebrew Website Club Newsletter Volume 1 Issue 1].
