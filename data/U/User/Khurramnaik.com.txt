<div style="line-height:1.6; margin-bottom:18px;">
I work with high-performing litigators who want more control over their careers. Most of my work centers on patent litigation and complex commercial litigation, helping lawyers create real leverage in competitive markets.

I am a partner at Freshwater Counsel, where I advise elite attorneys on platform moves, growth strategy, and long-term positioning. My focus is always practical. Clear options. Strong execution. No noise.

Outside of recruiting, I host a legal podcast called <i>Khurram’s Quorum</i>. The show features long-form conversations with managing partners, judges, and lawyers operating at a high level. The goal is simple: extract real insight from people who have actually built something meaningful.

Writing, podcasting, and recruiting all connect back to the same idea. Help talented people make better decisions.
