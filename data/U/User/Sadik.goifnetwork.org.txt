Sadik Shahadu is an open advocate and a professional project manager with over 4 years of experience in managing community projects. he studied Information technology and Database Administration at Wisconsin International university college in Ghana and IPMC College of technology respectively.
Sadik is a member of PMI and a volunteer chapter lead at PMI Ghana chapter.
In 2018, he volunteered as a track co-chair and a volunteer of the 2018 CC Summit Advisory Committee.
He is a certified professional at NTEN and currently volunteer in coordinating Wikipedia related projects in Ghana.

He is the co-founder and Director of Communications for the GOI Foundation. As the Director of communications, he is in charge of community engagement, social media and partnerships
