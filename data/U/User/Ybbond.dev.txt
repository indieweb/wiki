== Yohanes Bandung bondowoso ==
Yohanes Bandung Bondowoso (nick: Bandung) is from Jakarta, Indonesia. He currently work as a Frontend Engineer at [https://kumparan.com/ kumparan].

=== Connection to IndieWeb ===
Bandung discovered about IndieWeb on April 2020 when he read [https://www.amitgawande.com/indiewebify-hugo-website/ a blog post] to ''indiewebify'' [[Hugo]] website. He then try to make his current site as IndieWeb as possible.
