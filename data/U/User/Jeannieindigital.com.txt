== [https://jeannieindigital.com Jeannie McGeehan] ==

Jeannie is a 20+ year corporate veteran who changed careers to full-time caregiver with the birth of her son in 2015.  She has two chidren (born 1996 and 2015) and one grandchild (born 2015). Her husband, son, and she reside in Baltimore, MD, USA.

Jeannie believes in the right of everyone to own their online identity and has created several projects supporting that aim.
