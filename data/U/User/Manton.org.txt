= Manton Reece =

Manton is the creator of [https://indieweb.org/micro.blog Micro.blog] and a co-author of [https://jsonfeed.org/ JSON Feed]. He writes at [https://manton.org/ manton.org]. His podcasts include [http://coreint.org/ Core Intuition] and [http://timetable.fm/ Timetable].

Some of Manton's recent blog posts about the IndieWeb:

* [https://www.manton.org/2020/02/10/announcing-the-indiewebcamp.html Announcing the IndieWebCamp Austin speakers]
* [https://www.manton.org/2019/10/28/content-ownership-is.html Content ownership is not complicated]
* [https://manton.org/2018/03/23/indieweb-generation-and.html IndieWeb generation 4 and hosted domains]
