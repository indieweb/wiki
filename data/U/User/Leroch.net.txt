== About Me ==

Hello :) I'm Christian Leroch. Online I like to use '''Nirastich''', which is an anagram of "Christian" and uncommon enough, that it's still available practically everywhere.

In my free time I enjoy building with LEGO, [[3D printing]] (Bambu Lab P1S), reading (currently the Xeelee Sequence by Stephen Baxter), and traveling. I document my trips in a self-developed photo gallery with interactive maps on my personal website: [https://leroch.net/gallery leroch.net/gallery].

I'm a member of [https://bricking-bavaria.de Bricking Bavaria], a Bavarian LEGO User Group, and attend exhibitions like Skaerbaek Fan Weekend in Denmark.

== My Web Presence ==

I have a personal domain ecosystem built on privacy-first principles: no third-party cookies, no ads, no frameworks and no external dependencies where possible. All sites are handwritten PHP/HTML/CSS/JS, currently hosted on shared hosting by [https://www.one.com one.com].
