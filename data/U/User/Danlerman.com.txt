I’m Dan Lerman. My site is where I publish essays and notes I’m thinking through—mostly about learning, school culture, and what it takes to get better at hard things, plus the occasional piece that’s just for fun.

== What I tend to write about ==
* Education and how people actually learn
* Stories from teaching / tutoring life (the human side of it)
* Comedy, pop culture, and other personal rabbit holes
