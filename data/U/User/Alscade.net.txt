=== Description ===
This is a personal and self-hosted website with no rational reason to exist. It's an IndieWeb site project that contains a blog, a bunch of photos photos, and more nerdy IndieWeb stuff. The site includes Webmention support and uses microformats for publishing posts in a structured way.
