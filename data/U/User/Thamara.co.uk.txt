== Intro ==
Hello, I'm Thamara, furniture-peddler by day and wearer-of-many-hats by night. Currently based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. I've had a personal website since 2014.

I recently discovered the IndieWeb community, and I've been enjoying modifying my site to to adhere to the IndieWeb ethos. I'm usually active on the [https://indieweb.org/discuss IndieWeb chat].

== Personal site ==
My current website is built on [https://indieweb.org/WordPress WordPress]. I'm in the process of [https://thamara.co.uk/topic/the-great-astro-migration/ migrating] to [https://indieweb.org/Astro Astro], a static site generator.

=== Working on ===
* Writing my site from the ground up in HTML and CSS, with minimal JavaScript.
* Implementing IndieWeb building blocks such as h-card and other microformat properties, Webmentions, and IndieAuth to name a few.
