* https://web.archive.org/web/20210419025845/https://sandwichcode.org/

== Why ==
What problems would a sandwich code solve? In particular, a few scenarios:
* Order and get the exact sandwich you want, across a variety of sandwich makers (delis, restaurants, fast food, etc.).
* Place an order with a proxy (person) who can then more reliably convey that order to a sandwich maker so you get the exact sandwich you want
