Hey, I am <dfn>Amit Gawande</dfn>. I am a software developer by profession, a tinkerer on the web by liking and an aspiring writer by choice. I am a learning IndieWeb enthusiast from Pune, India, completely enthralled by the activeness of the community.

== Journey Embracing IndieWeb on Site ==
Amit's homepage was earlier built with '''[[Hugo]]''', deployed by '''Netlify''' and themed by a [https://github.com/am1t/ghostwriter custom port] of Ghostwriter theme. He recently migrated his complete web presence to '''[[Blot]]''' and has [https://github.com/am1t/blot-musings themed it] to his liking. He has [https://www.amitgawande.com/indiewebify-hugo-website/ embraced IndieWeb] principles across his website since <dfn>2018-01-25</dfn>.

* Web Sign-in via [[IndieAuth]]
* Identity via [[h-card]]
* Content definition with microformats ([[h-entry]])
* Cross-site conversations via [[Webmentions]]
** Using [[webmention.io]] as endpoint
* Interactions from Social Media
** Using [[bridgy]]
* Micropub to enable posting via [[micro.blog]]
