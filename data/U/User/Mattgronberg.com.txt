 I use this site as a place to think in public. Most of what I publish starts as a question I am trying to answer for myself, usually about work, technology, habits, and how people build lives they actually enjoy.
      
 My writing is intentionally simple and personal. I am not trying to convince anyone of anything. I am mostly trying to notice patterns, document what I am learning, and come back later to see which ideas held up.
      
 When I am not writing, I spend my time building, experimenting, and staying curious. This site is my long-term notebook.
