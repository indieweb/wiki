'''<dfn>Universal 2nd Factor</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn><abbr>U2F</abbr></dfn>) is a standard for [[two-factor authentication]] using hardware tokens.

Specifications: https://fidoalliance.org/specs/fido-u2f-v1.2-ps-20170411/fido-u2f-overview-v1.2-ps-20170411.html

== Support ==
=== Browsers ===
Currently native in Chrome and Opera. Support in Firefox [https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1065729 planned] and currently existing using [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/u2f-support-add-on/ an addon] (which hasn't been updated yet to a WebExtension).
