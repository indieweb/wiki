'''<dfn>Ulysses</dfn>''' is a subscription markdown writing app for iOS and macOS with an internal library of notes that is automatically synced through Apple's iCloud service. It supports publishing blog posts to Wordpress through XML-RPC and Medium. Use for IndieWeb through Micropub publishing is on their list of future features to support.
