'''<dfn>[https://mail.yahoo.com Yahoo Mail]</dfn>''' is an [[email]] service hosted by [[Yahoo]].

== How to Export ==
Yahoo Mail does not have an export feature, but suggests: [https://help.yahoo.com/kb/SLN5033.html]
* Printing a hard copy
* Forward a single email to another email address
* Save multiple emails by setting up POP access in Yahoo Mail
* Copy and paste each email that you want saved into a text editor

Yahoo Mail does support IMAP, so that may also be an option to backup/migrate your email messages. [https://help.yahoo.com/kb/SLN4075.html]

[[ProtonMail]] says they are working on a tool to help you import and export your emails. [https://protonmail.com/support/knowledge-base/export-import-emails/]
