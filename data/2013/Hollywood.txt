= <span class="p-name summary">Indie Web Camp : Hollywood 2013</span> =

<div class="p-description description">
Own your data.

Rather than posting content on third-party silos of content, we should all own the content we're creating. Publish on your own domain, and syndicate out to silos.

Join us at [http://farmhouse.co The Farmhouse] for '''two days''' of a BarCamp-style gathering of web creators building and sharing open web technologies to empower users to own their own identities & content, and advance the state of the indie web.
</div>
