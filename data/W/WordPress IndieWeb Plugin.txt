The '''<dfn>[https://wordpress.org/plugins/indieweb/ WordPress IndieWeb Plugin]</dfn>''' is a plugin that helps you establish your IndieWeb identity by extending your profile with [[rel-me]] and [[h-card]] fields, as well as helping you install additional IndieWeb plugins.

The project is maintained on [https://github.com/indieweb/wordpress-indieweb GitHub] and issues can be filed there.
