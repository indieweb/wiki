'''<dfn>Web of trust</dfn>''' (sometimes <dfn>PGP Web of Trust</dfn>) is a decentralized way of sharing identities that you trust or distrust, with your friends or strangers. Additionally a ranking can be assigned to convey different levels of trust or distrust.
