'''<dfn>[http://wunderlist.com Wunderlist]</dfn>''' is an app for managing todo lists that [[Microsoft]] is shutting down 2020-05-06 [https://www.wunderlist.com/blog/join-us-on-our-new-journey/]. Until then, it is available for many desktop and mobile platforms, and has an API.

== History ==
[[Microsoft]] acquired Wunderlist in 2015.[https://techcrunch.com/2015/06/02/microsoft-purchase-of-wunderlist-confirmed/] On 2017-04-19, they announced a preview of [[Microsoft To-Do]] and that they would eventually retire Wunderlist.[https://blogs.office.com/2017/04/19/introducing-microsoft-to-do-now-available-in-preview/]
