'''<dfn>[https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/Add-ons/WebExtensions WebExtensions]</dfn>''' are a cross-browser way to develop browser add-ons. The [https://www.w3.org/community/browserext/ Browser Extension Community Group] works on [https://browserext.github.io/browserext/ the Browser Extensions standard].

== Browser Documentation ==

As there isn’t a single resource or standard (yet), different [[browsers]] publish separate documentation. Even though there is much overlap. [[MDN]] publishes an overview of [https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/Add-ons/WebExtensions/Browser_support_for_JavaScript_APIs browser support of the separate APIs].

* [[Google Chrome]]: [https://developer.chrome.com/extensions Extensions]
* [[Microsoft Edge]]: [https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/extensions Microsoft Edge extensions]
* [[Mozilla Firefox]]: [https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/Add-ons/WebExtensions Browser Extensions]
* [[Opera]]: [https://dev.opera.com/extensions/ Extensions Documentation]
* [[Safari]]: [https://developer.apple.com/documentation/safariservices/safari_web_extensions Safari Web Extensions]
