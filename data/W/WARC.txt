    '''<dfn>WARC</dfn>''' is a web archive (Web ARChive) format designed to collect all of the resources that make up a web page into a single item that does not require any external references when opened.

== IndieWeb Examples ==

[[indie map]] publishes its raw crawl data in WARC format.
