'''<dfn>WebPageTest</dfn>''' is a web performance testing platform known for its incredibly detailed waterfall charts and number of options including scripting.

The project is built on open source - https://github.com/catchpoint/WebPageTest - but the instance provided by the company operating it has been popular. 

== Criticism ==

After being acquired by a larger company, the product seemed to be de-emphasized a great deal. A very large UI change in 2025/2026 led to a large amount of criticism about its quality.
