'''<dfn>[https://weirdweboctober.website/ Weird Web October]</dfn>''' is a 31 day [[challenge]] to make a website every day in [[October]] according to the theme of the day, inspired by [[Inktober]], and open to anyone to participate by posting about their new sites with the hashtag <kbd>#weirdweboctober</kbd>. 

[[Octothorpes]] can optionally be used to share your creations on https://octothorp.es/~/weirdweboctober.
