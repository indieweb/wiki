'''<dfn>The Web We Found</dfn>''' was a talk and presentation by {{kevinmarks}} at the Le Web 2013 conference that summarized the start of various positive trends and patterns from 2003 onward, like blogging, building on open protocols, which have grown since, many incorporated into the IndieWeb, and provides hopeful counterpoint to [[The Web We Lost]].
