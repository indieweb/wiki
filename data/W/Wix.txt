'''<dfn>Wix</dfn>''' is a point-and-click website creation service.

== Criticism ==

Wix does not support exporting, which makes [[migration]] difficult, and is thus not recommended for [[hosting]] your IndieWeb site. 

* https://quickthoughts.jgregorymcverry.com/2019/04/22/dangerousmere-for-everyone-who-is-replying-to
* https://support.wix.com/en/article/exporting-or-embedding-your-wix-site-elsewhere.

> Wix.com, which helps small businesses build and operate websites

Wix absolutely does not help anyone do anything but give them money. They don't expose standards; they have been litigated against for violating OpenSource license of WordPress
