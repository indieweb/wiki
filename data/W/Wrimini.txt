<dfn>'''[https://wrimini.net/ Wrimini]'''</dfn> is a [[Micropub/Clients|Micropub client]] for Android. It supports notes, replies, likes, bookmarks and photo uploads to a media-endpoint.

The app is available on [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.stuifzand.micropub the Play Store].

=== Features ===

* Note (via app)
* Media upload (via app)
* Bookmark (via share)
* In reply to (via share)
* Like (via share)

=== Supports ===
* Notes
* Articles
* Replies
* Likes
* Bookmarks
* Media endpoint
* Post status
* Visibility


The source code can be found on [https://github.com/pstuifzand/micropub-android Github].
