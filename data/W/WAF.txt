'''<dfn>WAF</dfn>''' is Web Application Firewall and usually refers to protection provided to web servers in the form of traffic filters that scan requests for known attacks/vulnerabilities before passing it on to the application. There are many providers, some with a free tier, like [[Cloudflare]] and [[AWS WAF]].
