'''<dfn>Wavelist</dfn>''' (<nowiki>wavelist.xyz</nowiki>) was a collection of [[podcast]] episodes curated into playlists by individuals, and appears to be a [[lost site]] as of 2022-08.

It was launched 2015-05-04.[http://werd.io/2015/if-you-like-podcasts-we-made-something-for-you-] The last archived version of the site appears to be [https://web.archive.org/web/20190812033950/http://wavelist.xyz/ 2019-08-12]. A 2022 [https://web.archive.org/web/20220816074516/https://wavelist.xyz/ archive snapshot] indicates it is a [[lost site]] now.
