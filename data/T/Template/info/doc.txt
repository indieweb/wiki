<span class="p-summary">This template is used to display an important information message on a page without seeming urgent or a warning.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the warning text.

<pre>{{info| Here is some key information you should know about this page}}</pre>

== Example ==
<pre>{{info| Here information you should know about this page}}</pre>

Will generate:

{{info| Here information you should know about this page}}
