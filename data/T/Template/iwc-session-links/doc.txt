This template is used to display an IWC session name, [[Jitsi Meet]] link, [[etherpad]] link, and wiki link.

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the information:

<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = 
| hashtag = 
| jitsi = 
| video = 
}}</pre>

== Parameters ==
;title
:Title of the session

;hashtag
:Short hashtag for the session. Used as path in etherpads and Jitsi Meet, so should be alphanumeric and may have hyphens

;jitsi
:If there is a Jitsi Meet for the session, enter any value for this parameter. Leave blank to display no Jitsi link

;video
:If there is a video URL for the session, enter it here. Leave blank to display no video link.

== Examples ==
<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = Microformats
| hashtag = microformats
| jitsi = 
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
}}</pre>

Will generate

{{iwc-session-links
| title = Microformats
| hashtag = microformats
| jitsi = 
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
}}
