This template is used to display an IWC session name, [[Jitsi Meet]] link, [[etherpad]] link, and wiki link.

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the information:

<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = 
| hashtag = 
| jitsi = 
| video = 
| duration = 
| archived = 
}}</pre>

== Parameters ==
;title
:Title of the session. This can be a wiki link to the session's page.

;hashtag
:Short hashtag for the session. Used as the path in etherpad and Jitsi Meet URLs, so should be alphanumeric and may have hyphens

;jitsi
:Enter <code>yes</code> if there is a Jitsi Meet for the session. Otherwise leave blank or omit this parameter.

;video
:Enter the video URL for the session, including live streams. Leave blank or omit this parameter if using Jitsi Meet.

;duration
:After the event, enter the duration for archived videos. If this is entered, the [[:Template:videoduration|videoduration]] template will be used.

;archived
:Enter <code>yes</code> once the etherpad for this session has been archived to the wiki. This will remove the etherpad link from the session links.

== Examples ==
'''Session with live video'''
<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
}}</pre>

Will generate

{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
}}

'''Session with Jitsi Meet'''
<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| jitsi = yes
}}</pre>

Will generate

{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| jitsi = yes
}}

'''Archived session with video and duration'''
<pre>{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
| duration = 42:03
| archived = yes
}}</pre>

Will generate

{{iwc-session-links
| title = [[2019/Austin/Microformats]]
| hashtag = microformats
| video = https://example.com/microformats.mp4
| duration = 42:03
| archived = yes
}}
