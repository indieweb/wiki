<span class="p-summary">This template is used to display a note on a page.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the text.

<pre>{{note| Text inside the note}}</pre>

== Example ==
<pre>{{note| Text inside the note}}</pre>

Will generate:

{{note| Text inside the note}}
