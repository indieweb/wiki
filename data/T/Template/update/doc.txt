<span class="p-summary">This template is used to display an update on a page with a light green background.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the warning text.

<pre>{{update| Here is an update}}</pre>

== Example ==
<pre>{{update| Here is an update}}</pre>

Will generate:

{{update| Here is an update}}
