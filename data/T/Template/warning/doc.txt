<span class="p-summary">This template is used to display a warning message on a page.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the warning text.

<pre>{{warning| Here be dragons}}</pre>

== Example ==
<pre>{{warning| Here be dragons}}</pre>

Will generate:

{{warning| Here be dragons}}
