This template is used to generate the [[Front End Study Hall]] navigation box at the bottom of related pages. It uses the [[Template:Navbox|navbox]] and [[Template:Navbox-row|navbox-row]] templates.

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and done.

<pre>{{Front End Study Hall}}</pre>

== Parameters ==
If you pass date=1970-01-01 it will load the group photo at the bottom as long as the group photo has the format <code><b>YYYY-MM-DD</b>-FrESH-Group-Photo.jpg</code>

== Example ==
{{Front End Study Hall}}
