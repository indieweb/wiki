<span class="p-summary">This template is used to display a message that something needs screenshots.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in the warning text.

<pre>{{needs screenshots| feature XYZ}}</pre>

== Example ==
<pre>{{needs screenshots| post editing}}</pre>

Will generate:

{{needs screenshots| post editing}}
