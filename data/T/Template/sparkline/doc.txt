<span class="p-summary">The '''<dfn>sparkline template</dfn>''' is used to display an inline photo with an optional <code>class</code> attached, e.g. to make a <code>u-photo</code> microformat. It is commonly used to create templates for users to sign their name on event RSVPs or other wiki pages.</span>

== Blank Template ==
Copy, paste, and fill in URL of the photo:

<pre style="line-height: 1.4em; padding: 1em; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: black; background-color: #f9f9f9;">{{sparkline| http://example.com/profile.jpg}}</pre>

If you want to add a class, add a class= parameter: 
<pre style="line-height: 1.4em; padding: 1em; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: black; background-color: #f9f9f9;">{{sparkline| http://example.com/profile.jpg|class=u-photo}}</pre>

== Parameters ==
This template accepts one unnamed parameter, which should be the URL of the photo, and a named parameter <code>class</code> for class(es) to add to the <code>&lt;img&gt;</code> tag.

== How to make a small h-card template ==
To make a small h-card template that allows you to easily add your name, link, and photo to a wiki page:

* On any wiki page, add: <code><nowiki>{{yourname}}</nowiki></code>
** Replace "yourname" with your name or screen name
* Click the resulting link to edit the template.
* Copy, paste, and edit with your information (note that you do '''not''' edit the word ''sparkline''):
<pre style="line-height: 1.4em; padding: 1em; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: black; background-color: #f9f9f9; white-space: pre-wrap;"><nowiki><span class="h-card">{{sparkline| http://example.com/your-profile-image.jpg}} [[User:Example.com|Your Display Name]]</span></nowiki></pre>

== How to include your h-card in your wiki signature ==
After creating your h-card template, open the [[Special:Preferences]] page and scroll to the "Signature" section. In the "New Signature" field, add <code><nowiki>{{subst:yourname}}</nowiki></code>. Check the box for "Treat signature as wikitext (without an automatic link)" and click "Save."

You will now be able to sign your comments on the wiki with your h-card, using three tildes: <nowiki>~~~</nowiki>

To sign with your h-card + current datetime, use four tildes: <nowiki>~~~~</nowiki>
