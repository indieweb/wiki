'''<dfn>TUAW(.com)</dfn>''' (AKA <dfn>The Unofficial Apple Weblog</dfn>) was a long-lived technology media site and blog which shutdown in 2015 whose domain was purchased in 2024 and relaunched as a [[zombie]] site using [[slop]] articles based on its prior content and [[GenAI]] fake images of prior authors using their actual names.
