'''<dfn>Tweetstamp</dfn>''' is tweetstamp.org a Twitter account @tweet_stamp and website used to timestamp and archive individual tweets.

== See Also ==

* @-reply to a tweet with the text "@tweet_stamp Stamp" and the site will reply to you with a permalink of the tweet you replied to, archived at tweetstamp.org.
