'''<dfn>[https://github.com/alltogethernow/server Together Server]</dfn>''' is a [[GraphQL]] server that acts as a bridge to [[Microsub]] and [[Micropub]]. It powers the [[Together]] microsub reader.

The project is mainly developed by {{grantcodes}} and is [https://github.com/alltogethernow/server available on GitHub].
