'''<dfn>TBT</dfn>''' is an abbreviation for <dfn>ThrowBack Thursday</dfn> (or <dfn>ThrowBack Tuesday</dfn>), and used as a [[hashtag]] on silos like [[Instagram]] to signify a [[throwback]] post posted on a Thursday (or less frequently on a Tuesday).
