'''<dfn>[https://github.com/TrsstProject/trsst Trsst]</dfn>''' is an open source extension to [[Atom]] allowing people to create self-signed and/or self-encrypted feeds. It is an attempt to build a decentralized version of [[Twitter]].

== History ==
A Kickstarter campaign raised USD 65k on 2013-09-13 [https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1904431672/trsst-a-distributed-secure-blog-platform-for-the-o/].

Trsst helped [[2013/Hollywood#Trssts|sponsor IndieWebCampHollywood 2013]].

The domain name for the project, <nowiki>trsst.com</nowiki>, expired 2018-07-08 and currently appears to be someone else's personal site. An [http://web.archive.org/web/20141026013050/http://www.trsst.com/ archived] version is available along with the [http://web.archive.org/web/20141204033649/http://www.trsst.com/paper/ white paper].

Trsst had a working feed view preview: <nowiki>https://home.trsst.com</nowiki>
