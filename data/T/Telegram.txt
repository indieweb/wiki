'''<dfn>[https://telegram.org/ Telegram]</dfn>''' is a cross-platform encrypted messaging app. Telegram is a centralized service, with a closed source server and open source clients.

It was founded by Pavel Durov, the creator of [http://vk.com vKontakte] (the russian analogue of [[Facebook]]), and might be considered the doppelgänger of FB Messenger app.
