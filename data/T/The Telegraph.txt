'''<dfn>The Telegraph</dfn>''' is a UK mainstream newspaper print media publication with a website that has additional content like columnist blogs.

== Blogs outage 2016-02-26 ==
It appears that on 2016-02-26 their entire blogs collection written by columnists went off line (appeared to be deleted). [https://twitter.com/textfiles/status/703129155576070144]
But is back as of 2016-02-27, e.g.:
* http://www.telegraph.co.uk/comment/blogs/
* http://blogs.telegraph.co.uk/news/author/benedictbrogan/
