'''<dfn>TOFU</dfn>''' is an acronym for Trust On First Use. It's an authentication scheme where clients trust whichever cryptographic keys are presented by a service when first connecting there; after that, a change of cryptographic keys is treated as suspicious.

Used by default in [[SSH]] and recommended by [[Gemini protocol|the Gemini protocol]], it stands in opposition to chains of trust built on certificate authorities.
