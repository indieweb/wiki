'''<dfn>TPAC</dfn>''' is the [[W3C]]'s annual Technical Plenary and Advisory Committee meeting week where most W3C Working Groups meet, a few Community Groups, and there is an all hands plenary day on the Wednesday of that week for general presentations and BarCamp-style break out sessions.
