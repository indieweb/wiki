'''<dfn>Transport Layer Security</dfn>''' (<dfn><abbr>TLS</abbr></dfn>) and (previously, since prohibited, <dfn>Secure Sockets Layer</dfn> or <dfn><abbr>SSL</abbr></dfn>) is a cryptographic protocol used to encrypt all data across a TCP socket. It is used to protect data for VPNs (Virtual Private Neworks), Instant Messaging services and is used by [[HTTPS]].
