'''<dfn>TikTok</dfn>''' is a video sharing app similar to [[Vine]], where users create and share short videos, often set to music.

== Why not ==
Why not to use TikTok, or even install it, and why to delete your data, account, and uninstall it:

* Avoid visiting, installing, using, and delete all content & apps if you already did unless you want to possibly/likely have Chinese State Intelligence tracking your device & activities: https://twitter.com/senschumer/status/1187386699170701313
