'''<dfn>[https://telegraph.p3k.io Telegraph]</dfn>''' is an open source API and service for sending [[webmentions]], part of the [[p3k]] suite. 

The service also has a [https://telegraph.p3k.io/send-a-webmention Send a Webmention page] which will allow a user to manually send a webmention by entering their source and target.
