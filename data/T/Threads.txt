'''<dfn>[https://www.threads.com/ Threads]</dfn>''' (threads.com) is a [[Twitter]]-like [[social media]] service with partial [[ActivityPub]] support, launched by [[Meta Platforms]] on 2023-07-06 at www.threads.net. The team is working on expanding to full ActivityPub server-to-server support.
