'''<dfn>Threads</dfn>''' is a [[Twitter]]-like [[social media]] service launched by [[Meta]]/[[Facebook]] on 2023-07-06 UTC that has plans to add [[ActivityPub]] support.

== Features ==
* [[rel-me]] support added 2023-08-09, see [[rel-me#Threads]]
