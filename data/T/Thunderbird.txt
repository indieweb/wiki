'''<dfn>[https://www.thunderbird.net/ Thunderbird]</dfn>''' is an open source [[email]] client for [[macOS]], [[Windows]], [[Linux]] and other Unix-like operating systems. It is one of the few remaining graphical email clients that is cross-platform.


=== Enigmail ===

[https://enigmail.net/ Enigmail] is a plug-in for Thunderbird that supports sending [[OpenPGP]] signed or encrypted email.  [https://emailselfdefense.fsf.org/en/ A guide on doing this] is published by the Free Software Foundation.
