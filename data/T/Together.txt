'''<dfn>[https://github.com/cleverdevil/together/ Together]</dfn>''' is a [[reader]] that was initially conceived at the [[2017]] IndieWeb Summit in Portland by {{cleverdevil}} and several others during the [[2017/together|Putting it all together]] session. {{cleverdevil}} pitched the idea during [[2017/Demos|demos on day two]].

The project is [https://github.com/cleverdevil/together/ available on GitHub] and there is a hosted version available at [https://alltogethernow.io https://alltogethernow.io].
