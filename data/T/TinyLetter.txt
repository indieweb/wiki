'''<dfn>[https://tinyletter.com/ TinyLetter]</dfn>''' is a message [[silo]] and service to organize [[email]] [[newsletter|newsletters]], with an optional blog-like web archive, that was acquired by [[MailChimp]] in 2011. Sometimes used as a simple blog with restricted viewership.

== How export data ==
Subscriber lists can be exported as CSV, past posts not(? at least not documented).
