'''<dfn>[https://tinyletter.com/ TinyLetter]</dfn>''' was a message [[silo]] and service to organize [[email]] [[newsletter|newsletters]] that closed 2024-02-29[https://www.theverge.com/2023/11/29/23981363/mailchimp-shutting-down-tinyletter]. It had an optional blog-like web archive and was sometimes used as a simple blog with restricted viewership.

== How to export data ==
'''Export subscribers:'''

<blockquote>
You can export a full list of your subscribers at any time by going to Subscribers then clicking the Export button in the top right corner. This will start the export process of email addresses, subscribe dates, and notes for your subscribers into a CSV file.

https://tinyletter.com/site/help/your-subscribers-and-mailing-lists/exporting-subscribers-in-tinyletter/ ([http://web.archive.org/web/20231129215723/https://tinyletter.com/site/help/your-subscribers-and-mailing-lists/exporting-subscribers-in-tinyletter/ archived])
