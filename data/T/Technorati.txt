'''<dfn>Technorati</dfn>''' was a real-time blog search engine that provided date-ordered results for text phrases or links, typically within seconds of when people published on their blogs. Technorati was used on some blogs and news sites to link to or show a realtime list of [[backlink]]s to a specific article or an entire site.
