'''<dfn>[https://www.netflix.com/browse?jbv=81254224 The Social Dilemma]</dfn>''' is a 2020 [[Netflix]] docudrama that is critical of [[social media]] silos, and their use [[recommendation engine]]s in [[algorithmic feed]]s to create more and more addictive behaviors by their users to maximize their [[advertising]] business models, though presented from the viewpoint of former employees of those [[silos]] who have since turned into critics, without mentioning long-standing critical activists.
