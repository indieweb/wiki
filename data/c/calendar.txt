A '''<dfn>calendar</dfn>''' is a page or part of a page on a personal site that shows [[event]]s that the person is going to, interested in, recommending to others, or other [[RSVP]]s, sometimes in a tabular presentation of a whole month, sometimes as a list.

If you’re looking for the annual IndieWeb community gift calendar, see:
* '''[[IndieWeb Gift Calendar]]'''
If you’re looking for monthly / yearly archives of posts often displayed like months or a calendar year, see:
* '''[[archive]]'''

== Why ==
{{section-stub}}
Publishing a calendar of your past and future events enables:
* subscribe to it in device specific Calendar programs (e.g. via conversion to ICS)
* help friends and other readers discover events they may be interested in, and what kinds of events you have participated in in the past
* ...

== How to ==
{{section-stub}}
There is no one method people use to put calendars on their sites. Here are a handful of known methods used by independent sites:
* custom backend programming
* [[Google Calendar]] embed
* ...
