'''<dfn>cryptocurrency</dfn>''' is a use of a proof of work [[blockchain]] to create exchange value, has been discussed at some IndieWebCamp sessions, and has in the past been used for personal [[tip]] page [[payments]] specifically using [[Bitcoin]] addresses.
