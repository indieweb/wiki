The '''<dfn>clacks</dfn>''' is a fictional communications network of semaphore towers used in Terry Pratchett's ''Discworld'' novels, that IndieWeb and other internet users with their own websites reference with the [[X-Clacks-Overhead]] HTTP response header as a tribute.
