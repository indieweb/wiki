The '''<dfn>clacks</dfn>''' is a fictional communications network of semaphore towers used in Terry Pratchett's ''Discworld'' novels. IndieWeb and other internet users with their own websites reference this with the [[XClacksOverhead]] HTTP response header as a tribute to the author and his work.
