'''<dfn>comment reactions</dfn>''' are a feature of several [[silos]] that allow users to tap a hollow heart or other affordance next to [[comments]] on [[posts]] to [[like]] a specific comment or sometimes react with a limited set of [[reacji]]. Silo examples: [[Facebook]], [[Instagram]], [[GitHub]], and recently [[Strava]].
