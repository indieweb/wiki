A '''<dfn>caw</dfn>''' is the sound of a crow's call and a [[page]] on several IndieWeb sites typically at <code>/caw</code>, the contents varying based on interpretation. The first example was created by {{fractalkitty}} and was shared at [[events/2026-02-04-hwc-pacific|Homebrew Website Club 2026-02-04]].
