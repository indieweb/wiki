'''<dfn>[https://cohost.org/ Cohost]</dfn>''' was a social networking site built by [https://antisoftware.club Anti Software Software Club, LLC]. Its objective was to provide a platform with no [[advertising|ads]], only a [[chronological_feed|chronological feed]] and a plan for a [[business-models|subscription model]] to support the site.
