'''<dfn>[https://cohost.org/ Cohost]</dfn>''' was a social networking site built by [https://antisoftware.club Anti Software Software Club, LLC]. Its objective was to provide a platform with no [[advertising|ads]], only a [[chronological_feed|chronological feed]] and a plan for a [[business-models|subscription model]] to support the site.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20220701002043/https://cohost.org/rc/welcome cohost! landing page]</ref><ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20250107224912/https://cohost.org/staff/post/39-welcome-to-cohost 2022-02-03 welcome to cohost!]</ref>

== Shutdown ==
ASSC announced on September 9th, 2024 that cohost would be shut down at the end of 2024 after going read-only on October 1st.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20240917095757/https://cohost.org/staff/post/7611443-cohost-to-shut-down 2024-09-09 cohost to shut down at end of 2024]</ref> Users had the next few weeks to coordinate their relocation plans. [[data_portability|Data exports]] became available starting in October,<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20241007203922/https://cohost.org/staff/post/7906102-the-final-age-of-pos 2024-09-30 the final age of posting]</ref> and the site eventually went offline in January 2025.<ref>[https://mastodon.social/@cohost/113810018192229454 2025-01-11 announcement]</ref> In the time between, ASSC coordinated with the [[Internet Archive]] team to archive many of Cohost's public pages on the [[Wayback Machine]] in a dedicated mass archival effort that spanned from November 2, 2024 to January 10, 2025.<ref>[https://wiki.archiveteam.org/index.php/Cohost Archive Team Wiki: Cohost]</ref>
