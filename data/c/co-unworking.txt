A '''<dfn>co-unworking</dfn>''' call (also co-unworking [[Zoom]] etc) is an interactive [[video]] chat where the participants verbally [[chat]] and do some work or non-work related things in the background. Sometimes, espresso is ground up and [[coffee]] is made, that's an "espresso livestream" often put on by {{t}}.
