'''<dfn>command line interface</dfn>''' refers to the use of text-based commands in a terminal application to take actions such as install or update software, and is part of setting up most [[CMS]]s and running [[SSG]]s; due to its difficult to remember and easy to typo commands, for developers only, and not recommended for any general purpose IndieWeb tools or software.
