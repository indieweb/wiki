'''<dfn>chronological feed</dfn>''' is a stream of [[posts]] in time order, typically in reverse chronological order of their published date (newest first), popularized on the web by journals and blog home pages, feed readers, and [[social media]], until the latter switched to [[algorithmic feed]]s, frustrating many users.
