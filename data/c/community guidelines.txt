'''<dfn>IndieWeb community guidelines</dfn>''' are advice, tips, and a loose collection of emergent norms in the IndieWeb community beyond the [[code-of-conduct]] — new community members should read through these to get a better sense of how to participate harmoniously in shared IndieWeb spaces such as the [[discuss|chat channels]] and virtual Zooms ([[HWC]], [[Pop-ups]], [[FrESH]], and any others shared in chat).
