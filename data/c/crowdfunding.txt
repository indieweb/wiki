'''<dfn>crowdfunding</dfn>''' is the practice of asking people to promise to buy a project, sometimes with a set goal to trigger payments, other times with no set goal, which is generally implemented by siloed organisations, although there is at least one [https://github.com/cmod/craigstarter IndieWeb alternative to Kickstarter], one of the most popular crowdfunding silos.

== Other Independent Examples ==
=== Craigstarter ===
[https://github.com/cmod/craigstarter Craigstarter]  is an open source crowdfunding tool built on [[Shopify]] by [https://craigmod.com/ Craig Mod]. It's free to use / edit / extend crowdfunding tool with multiple goals and variants as campaign tiers.

Craig Mod built the platform to fund [https://shop.specialprojects.jp/products/kissa-by-kissa?variant=34630892683419 his book] which met its goals and is shipping in October 2020.

=== Ignition Deck ===
[https://ignitiondeck.com/id/ Ignition Deck] is an ecommerce, crowdfunding, and fundraising solution (via paid plugin) for [[WordPress]] that can be integrated with [[WooCommerce]], and is 100% theme independent. The project is maintained by [https://alexhasnicehair.com/ Alex Vasquez].
