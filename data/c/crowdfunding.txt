'''<dfn>crowdfunding</dfn>''' is the practice of asking people to promise to buy a project, sometimes with a set goal to trigger payments, other times with no set goal, which is generally implemented by siloed organisations. There is, however, at least one [https://github.com/cmod/craigstarter IndieWeb alternative to Kickstarter], one of the most popular crowdfunding silos.
