'''<dfn>Cook what you want</dfn>''' is a food-based metaphor for the IndieWeb [[principle]] of [[make what you need]], encouraging creators to make improvements on their personal sites that they themselves want to use. It is the complementary metaphor to [[eat what you cook]].

== Brainstorming ==
=== Expanding to include others ===
Similar to: [[make_what_you_need#Expand_from_you_to_others]], cooking in particular is a good metaphor to encourage eventual broadening to include others:
* Cook for yourself and a friend
* Cook and host a dinner party
Etc.
