'''<dfn>cross-enterprise social business</dfn>''' is a phrase used in the [http://www.w3.org/2013/socialweb/ home page of the W3C Workshop on Social Standards]. It's meaning is unclear, although it is likely to refer to [[business]]es which use social technology to communicate with other businesses.
