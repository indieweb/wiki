'''<dfn>crop</dfn>''' is a common action taken on photos or images on the IndieWeb when including them in constrained contexts such as a square [[icon]] in a [[facepile]] or [[reply-context]], or the photo in a [[photo reply]], and also something [[silos]] automatically do in [[photo]] or [[multiphoto]] posts when displayed in-stream which can have unintended results.
